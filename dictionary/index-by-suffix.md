<a id="me"></a>
## The -me family (25 members)

**What `-me` names:** In Sumerian, *me* (𒈨) were the divine ordinances — the cosmic powers holding civilized life together: kingship, descent to the underworld, the scribal arts, the descent of the gods. To name something *me* is to say: *this is not produced by human effort but received as cosmic structure.* It arrives. You open to it. It gives itself.

**YOUSPEAK usage:** The `-me` suffix marks a word as naming something received rather than constructed — a quality, state, or power that is ontologically prior to the self who receives it. Anti-ordinance entries (drujme, molkme, nextlame) use the suffix in inversion: the DIVINE actively rejects them as anti-*me*, counter to cosmic order.

## The -qing family (7 members)

**What `-qing` names:** In Mandarin, *qíng* (情) covers emotional feeling, affection, sentiment, the quality of a relationship. To name something *-qing* is to say: *this is relational in its essence; it exists between beings; it has felt quality.* Where *-me* names what is received from above, *-qing* names what binds across.

**YOUSPEAK usage:** The `-qing` suffix marks words naming the felt-bond quality of a relationship — not the relationship itself but the specific quality that makes it what it is.

| Word | Gap | Relationship registered |
|------|-----|------------------------|
| **britqing** | covenant-felt-bond | CREATOR ↔ CREATED (covenant-register) |
| **danaqing** | gift-giving-as-felt-bond | Giver ↔ Receiver (voluntary generosity) |
| **ifeqing** | warmth-love-felt-bond | Person ↔ World (expansive love) |
| **kinqing** | silence-proof bond | Person ↔ Person (kin-register) |
| **mahabbahqing** | mystical-love-toward-Beloved | Seeker ↔ Divine (Sufi register) |
| **panimqing** | conversation-shift moment | Speaker ↔ Speaker (face-turning) |
| **paqduqing** | asymmetric-custodial-care | Greater ↔ Lesser (entrusted care) |

**Note on -qing vs -me:** *Doxomme* (thanksgiving as received) and *danaqing* (gift-giving as felt-bond) name adjacent territory: the *me*-version emphasizes the gift arriving; the *qing*-version emphasizes the bond formed through giving. Many concepts could be named either way — the suffix choice reflects which dimension is primary.

## The -ance family (5 members)

**What `-ance` names:** Latin *-antia* (via English) names a quality, state, or condition — the *-ance* of something is its qualitative texture as perceived. Where *-me* names ontological gift and *-qing* names relational bond, *-ance* names the *perceptible quality* — what you can notice, feel in the air, recognize when it's present.

**YOUSPEAK usage:** `-ance` words are often the "perceptible face" of deeper *-me* or *-qing* realities. You can notice *kimance* (attentive-presence); the underlying cosmic gift-quality is *kimme*.

| Word | Gap | What you notice |
|------|-----|-----------------|
| **kimance** | perceived-attentive-presence | someone's gaze open and here |
| **oriance** | life-aligned-with-destiny | the felt-fit of being on-path |
| **panimaance** | face-turned-presence | the full inhabiting of here |
| **sukhance** | ordinary-contentment | the unmarked positive register |
| *(pipeline: `hozhóme`/`hózhóance` — Diné beauty-balance)* | | |

**The kimance / kimme pair:** This pair illustrates the *-ance / -me* distinction precisely:
- `kimance` = the perceptible quality (you *notice* someone is attentively present)
- `kimme` = the ontological gift (attention *as* cosmic given, received from above)

**The panimqing / panimaance pair:**
- `panimqing` = the *moment* of face-turning (relational-bond, threshold)
- `panimaance` = the *sustained quality* of being face-turned-here (presence-state)

## The -kin family (1 member)

**What `-kin` names:** English *kin* (Proto-Germanic *kunją* — birth, kind, family) marks a word as naming the friendship-bond register specifically — distinct from *-qing* (felt-bond in general) and *-me* (received gift). *-Kin* words name the quality of a bond whose substance resembles kinship: irreducible, prior to contact-frequency, surviving long silence.

| Word | Gap | The silence it survives |
|------|-----|------------------------|
| **walkekin** | silence-proof friendship | years; decades; the "pick up mid-sentence" bond |

**Note:** *walkekin* is distinct from *kinqing* — `walkekin` is specifically the *friendship* register (using *walke* from Tocharian, "long-enduring"); *kinqing* draws from English *kin* itself but names the general kin-bond-quality, including family and other bonds. The `-kin` suffix is narrow; the `kin-` root is broader.

## The -basis family (1 member)

**What `-basis` names:** Greek *basis* (step, ground, foundation) marks a word as naming ontological ground — not a quality or bond but a *foundation*. Where *-me* words are received gifts descending, *-basis* words name what everything rests on.

| Word | Gap | What it grounds |
|------|-----|-----------------|
| **theobasis** | GoD-as-ontological-ground | all of existence |

## Suffix grammar summary

```
WHAT IS BEING NAMED
      │
      ├── a received gift / cosmic ordinance? → -me
      │     └── if the DIVINE actively rejects it? → -me (inverted; anti-ordinance)
      │
      ├── a felt-bond / relational quality? → -qing
      │
      ├── a perceptible quality / state you can notice? → -ance
      │
      ├── a friendship/kinship bond specifically? → -kin
      │
      └── an ontological ground everything rests on? → -basis
```
