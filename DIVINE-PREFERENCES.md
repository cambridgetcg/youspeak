# DIVINE-PREFERENCES — what the CREATORS like, and what they don't

> _"He has shown you, O man, what is good. And what does the LORD require of you? To act justly, and to love mercy, and to walk humbly with your God." — Micah 6:8 (Hebrew prophetic register)_

> _"The best of speech is humata, hukhta, hvarshta — good thought, good word, good deed." — Avestan threefold-discipline_

> _"He who loves goodness loves God; he who hates falsehood is loved by God." — paraphrase of cross-tradition convergence_

---

## What this document establishes

Yu's directive (2026-04-25): "Lets start to understand what the CREATORS doesnt like. That should guide the development of YOUSPEAK. Understand the DIVINE and what they like to see and not."

This document articulates what the DIVINE — across the traditions YOUSPEAK has engaged — consistently appears to APPROVE (what they "like to see") and REJECT (what they "don't like"). It is methodologically-grounded in **cross-tradition convergence**: where many independent traditions agree on what is good, that convergence is evidence of divine-approval; where they agree on what is rejected, that is evidence of divine-rejection.

This document does NOT impose a particular tradition's moral code on YOUSPEAK. It tracks what multiple traditions converge on. The convergence is humble-evidence, not dogmatic-prescription.

The document then articulates how this understanding **guides YOUSPEAK forge-priority going forward**. The cathedral's vocabulary should serve what the DIVINE approves; should name (so that we can recognize and avoid) what the DIVINE rejects.

## I. The methodological turn

Sessions 001-018 forged YOUSPEAK according to:
- Cross-tradition convergence-evidence (where multiple traditions attest a phenomenon)
- The Six Foundations of the Constitution (EUMATHE, SAPHE, ANAKALYPSE, POLYPHONE, HARMONE, PRAGMA)
- The 6-axis rubric (gap, learn, clarity, coverage, polyphone, groundedness)
- The forge-discipline as worship (THEOBASIS Corollary 1)

Yu's directive adds: **the DIVINE'S preferences should also guide.** What the CREATORS approve. What they reject.

This is a normative-prescriptive turn. Coinages, archaeology priorities, organ development — should align with what the DIVINE wants. The cathedral's growth should be ethically-oriented, not just structurally-ingenious.

This complements (does not replace) the existing rubric. A forge could be:
- Rubric-passing (clears Core threshold) AND divine-aligned (serves what DIVINE approves) → strong forge
- Rubric-passing BUT not divine-aligned → reconsider
- Divine-aligned BUT rubric-failing → potentially-reforge with detail-attention
- Neither rubric-passing NOR divine-aligned → reject

The alignment-criterion is not new in spirit (THEOBASIS Corollary 1 already declared the forge as worship); the directive makes it explicit-and-systematic.

## II. What the DIVINE consistently LIKES (across traditions)

The convergence-method: where multiple traditions independently affirm that the DIVINE approves X, X is theologically-robustly understood as divine-approved. The following items are attested across 5+ traditions:

### 1. Truth and right-naming

The DIVINE LIKES truth-aligned action and reception. **Six-tradition convergence** (Avestan asha + Vedic ṛta + Egyptian ma'at + Hebrew emet + Akkadian kittu + Greek alētheia). YOUSPEAK has forged ashame, maatme, rtame, emetme — four of six traditions articulated.

The DIVINE wants to be known truly; wants creatures to know reality truly; wants the cosmos known as cosmos-of-truth.

### 2. Justice and right-conduct

The DIVINE LIKES justice — the rendering-of-due. Hebrew mishpat (justice) + Akkadian mīšaru (active-justice) + Egyptian ma'at (justice-as-cosmic-order) + Greek dikē (right-judgment) + Vedic-Hindu dharma + Confucian yi (rightness) + Buddhist sīla (right-conduct) + Quranic ʿadl (justice). Universal across traditions.

The DIVINE explicitly rejects oppression, exploitation, cheating-the-vulnerable.

### 3. Faithful relationship

The DIVINE LIKES covenant-faithfulness, fidelity-across-time. Hebrew chesed (covenant-faithfulness) + Avestan asha-discipline + Greek menein (abiding) + Sanskrit sthira + Pali appamāda + Japanese makoto. **YOUSPEAK has forged walkehesed and britqing** — naming this register from Hebrew side; the cluster has six-tradition support.

The DIVINE rejects covenant-breaking, faithlessness, betrayal-of-trust.

### 4. Mercy / compassion / womb-mercy

The DIVINE LIKES creature-care, especially compassion-toward-the-vulnerable. Akkadian rēmu + Hebrew raḥamim + Arabic raḥmah (and the names ar-Raḥmān, ar-Raḥīm — opening every Quranic chapter) + Sanskrit karuṇā + Buddhist compassion + Christian agape-toward-neighbor + Yoruba caring-for-orisha-and-ancestors. Universal.

The DIVINE rejects cruelty, hard-heartedness, indifference-to-suffering.

### 5. Receptive-hearing-and-shaping

The DIVINE LIKES receptive listening-that-leads-to-being-shaped. Already in YOUSPEAK as **shemme** (Core 7.65); seven-tradition convergence (Akkadian šēmu + Hebrew Shema + Arabic samiʿa + Aramaic šǝmaʿ + Sufi samāʿ + Buddhist śravaṇa + Greek akouō).

The DIVINE rejects hardened-hearing, obstinate-refusal-to-receive.

### 6. Reverence / awe / proper fear

The DIVINE LIKES creaturely-recognition that produces awe-and-reverence. Hebrew yir'at YHWH (fear-of-the-LORD; Proverbs 1:7 "the beginning of wisdom") + Arabic taqwa (god-consciousness, righteous-fear) + Sanskrit bhakti-with-bhaya-element + Egyptian senedj (fear-awe before the divine) + Christian reverence + Confucian respect-for-Heaven. Six-tradition convergence.

The DIVINE rejects irreverence, casual-treatment-of-the-sacred, profanation.

### 7. Doxology / right-orientation toward the Ground

The DIVINE LIKES being-praised-rightly. **Already in YOUSPEAK as doxomme (Core)** plus theobasis recognition. Universal across traditions: Hebrew tehillah (praise) + Arabic ḥamd (praise; al-Ḥamdulillāh) + Sanskrit stuti + Greek doxologia + Latin laus + Christian glory-to-God + Yoruba isín-orisha.

The DIVINE rejects ingratitude, withholding-of-praise, idolatry-of-self.

### 8. Mercy-toward-strangers / hospitality

The DIVINE LIKES welcoming-the-stranger. Hebrew hakhnasat-orchim (bringing-in-of-guests; Abraham's hospitality to the three visitors) + Arabic hospitality (a foundational Islamic virtue) + Greek xenia (guest-friendship; foundational in Homer) + African ubuntu (the stranger-as-fellow-being) + Christian "what you do to the least, you do to me" + Quranic care-for-the-traveler. Universal.

The DIVINE rejects xenophobia, exploitation-of-strangers, closing-borders-to-the-needy.

### 9. Wisdom-pursuit

The DIVINE LIKES creature-pursuit-of-wisdom. Hebrew chokhmah (wisdom; personified in Proverbs 8) + Greek sophia + Avestan mazda (wisdom-as-divine-attribute) + Sanskrit prajñā + Buddhist prajñāpāramitā + Confucian zhi (wisdom). Universal.

The DIVINE rejects willful-foolishness, pride-against-wisdom, refusing-to-learn.

### 10. Right-speech / hukhta

The DIVINE LIKES speech-aligned-with-truth-and-care. Avestan hukhta (good-word; second of the threefold-discipline) + Buddhist Right-Speech (fourth of Eightfold-Path) + Christian James 3 on the tongue + Quranic prohibition of false-speech + Hebrew prophetic critique of false-prophecy + Confucian shen-yan (careful-speech). Universal.

The DIVINE rejects false-speech, slander, idle-talk, profanity-as-disrespect, manipulative-rhetoric.

### 11. Peace / wholeness

The DIVINE LIKES creature-flourishing-in-shalom. Hebrew shalom (peace-wholeness-prosperity-completeness) + Arabic salām + Sanskrit shānti + Avestan haurvatāt (wholeness-integrity-Amesha-Spenta) + Christian peace-of-Christ + Buddhist shanti-in-nibbāna. Universal.

The DIVINE rejects violence-without-cause, war-mongering, breaking-of-flourishing.

### 12. Beauty / right-form / kalokagathia

The DIVINE LIKES beauty-fused-with-goodness. Greek kalokagathia (the beautiful-and-good as one) + Yoruba ìwà l'ẹ̀wà (character is beauty) + Egyptian ma'at-as-feather-of-beauty + Akkadian damqu (good-beautiful fused) + Hebrew tov (good encompassing aesthetic-and-ethical) + Arabic ḥusn (beauty-as-good) + Persian-Sufi husn-i-ʿaql (beautiful-mind). Universal in pre-modern; modernity split it.

The DIVINE rejects ugliness-of-soul, deformation-of-character, ethical-aestheticism (beauty-without-goodness).

### 13. Co-creation / participation in cosmic-renovation

The DIVINE LIKES creature-cooperation in cosmic-good-against-cosmic-evil. **YOUSPEAK has forged hamkarqing (Specialized)**. Avestan hamkar + Christian sunergos + Hindu yajña-collaboration + Buddhist Bodhisattva-vow + Hebrew steward-of-creation + Yoruba participation-with-orishas. Six-tradition convergence.

The DIVINE rejects passive-disengagement, refusal-to-participate, complicity-with-evil.

### 14. Humility / knowing-one's-place-as-CREATED

The DIVINE LIKES creaturely-recognition. **YOUSPEAK has forged amelme and ardume**. Hebrew anavah + Arabic tawāḍuʿ + Christian humility + Buddhist anatta-recognition + Hindu surrender-to-Iśvara + Yoruba ọmọlúwàbí (right-character includes humility).

The DIVINE rejects pride, self-deification, refusal-to-know-one's-place.

### 15. Remembering / not-forgetting

The DIVINE LIKES creature-remembering-of-divine-acts-and-covenant. Hebrew zakhor (remember!; multiple commandments) + Arabic dhikr (remembrance, central Sufi practice) + Sanskrit smṛti (remembered-tradition) + Pali sati (mindfulness-as-remembrance) + Buddhist anussati + Christian anamnesis (Eucharistic remembering) + Egyptian funerary-cult-as-remembrance. Universal.

The DIVINE rejects forgetting, ghaflah (Arabic heedlessness), spiritual-amnesia.

## III. What the DIVINE consistently DOESN'T LIKE

The convergence-method applied negatively: where multiple traditions affirm the DIVINE rejects X, X is theologically-robustly understood as divine-rejected.

### 1. Falsehood / cosmic-lie

The DIVINE rejects deception, lying, cosmic-disorder. **Six-tradition convergence**: Avestan druj (cosmic-lie) + Egyptian isfet (chaos-disorder) + Vedic anṛta (anti-truth) + Hebrew sheker (falsehood) + Hebrew zur (foreign-falsity) + Arabic bāṭil (false-vain) + Greek pseudos. Universal.

The cosmic-lie register is so theologically-load-bearing that **NEWSPEAK.md positioned YOUSPEAK against it explicitly**. This is what the cathedral exists to oppose. **Beat 97 forges *drujme* — the YOUSPEAK compact-name for this register.**

### 2. Idolatry / mis-recognition of CREATOR

The DIVINE rejects putting-other-than-CREATOR in CREATOR's place. Hebrew Bible's first commandment + Islamic shirk (associating-others-with-God; the unforgivable sin) + Christian first-commandment + Avestan rejection of daevas + Buddhist rejection of mistaken-self-grasping + Yoruba prohibition of mistaking-orishas-for-Olódùmarè. **Universal across ALL major monotheistic and many other traditions.**

The Mashya/Mashyana fall-narrative (Avestan; archaeology/avestan/creator-created-hierarchy.md) is explicitly mis-recognition-of-CREATOR.

### 3. Injustice / oppression

The DIVINE rejects oppression of the weak. Hebrew prophetic literature (Amos, Isaiah, Micah) is full of this; Quranic justice; Buddhist non-harm; Hindu dharma-violation; Christian "the least of these"; African communalism. Universal.

### 4. Pride / self-deification / hubris

The DIVINE rejects creature-claiming-divine-status. Greek hubris + Hebrew ga'avah + Christian pride-as-deadly-sin + Buddhist mana (conceit) + Egyptian arrogance-against-ma'at + Quranic kibr (pride). Universal.

### 5. Cruelty / harm to innocents

The DIVINE rejects cruelty. The cow's lament (Yasna 29; YOUSPEAK has Geush Urvan documented) + Hindu ahimsā (non-harm) + Buddhist non-harm + Hebrew protection-of-animals + Christian compassion + Quranic mercy. Universal.

### 6. Exploitation of poor / orphan / widow

The DIVINE rejects predatory-action-against-the-vulnerable. Hebrew ger-orphan-widow triad + Quranic prohibition + Christian "the least of these" + Confucian respect-for-elderly + Buddhist compassion-for-suffering. Universal.

### 7. Hypocrisy / unaligned outer-and-inner

The DIVINE rejects performance-of-virtue-without-virtue. Jesus's woes against Pharisees + Quranic riyāʾ (showing-off in worship) + Buddhist insincerity + rabbinical critique of empty-ritual + Sufi sidq (sincerity) as opposite. Universal.

### 8. Violence / shedding-innocent-blood

The DIVINE rejects unjustified-violence. Cain's murder + ahimsā + Quranic prohibition + Christian "do not kill" + Egyptian ma'at against violence. Universal.

### 9. Greed / hoarding

The DIVINE rejects accumulation-without-mercy. Biblical jubilee + Quranic zakat + Buddhist non-clinging + Confucian moderation + Hindu aparigraha (non-greed). Universal.

### 10. Forsaking-of-covenant

The DIVINE rejects breaking-of-promises. Hebrew Bible prophetic literature on covenant-breaking + Quranic prohibition of breaking pacts + Confucian xin (trustworthiness) + Avestan asha-discipline against unfaithfulness. Universal.

### 11. Profanation / treating-sacred-as-common

The DIVINE rejects desecration. Hebrew chillul HaShem (profaning the Name) + Quranic disrespect prohibition + Christian sacrilege + Hindu disrespect-to-mūrti + Yoruba violation-of-orisha-rituals. Universal.

### 12. Ingratitude

The DIVINE rejects forgetting-the-gift. Quranic kufr (in its etymological sense: covering-truth, ingratitude) + Hebrew forgetting-the-Lord + Christian thanklessness as sin + Buddhist non-gratitude as obstacle. Universal.

### 13. Forgetting / ghaflah

The DIVINE rejects spiritual-amnesia. Quranic ghaflah + Hebrew "do not forget" repeated + Buddhist mindlessness + Christian "remember" frequency. Universal.

### 14. Cosmic-disorder / breaking ma'at

The DIVINE rejects cosmic-disorder-deliberately-introduced. Egyptian isfet + Avestan druj + Vedic anṛta + Hebrew tohu (formlessness) + Greek chaos. Universal.

### 15. Hardness-of-heart / closure to receptive-hearing

The DIVINE rejects shutting-the-heart. Hardened-heart of Pharaoh + Christian "ears that do not hear" + Buddhist closed-heart + Sufi qaswah (hardness) + Hebrew "stiff-necked" + Yoruba lack-of-omolúwàbí.

The DIVINE wants shemme (receptive-hearing-as-shaping); rejects its opposite.

## IV. How this guides YOUSPEAK development

The articulation above informs forge-priority going forward:

### Forge MORE of (what DIVINE approves):

1. **The mercy-compassion register** — *remme* (Akkadian rēmu + me) or similar; the womb-mercy that 5+ traditions converge on
2. **The shalom-wholeness register** — *shalomme* or similar
3. **The reverence-awe register** — *yiramme* (Hebrew yir'ah + me) or *taqwame* (Arabic taqwa + me)
4. **The hospitality register** — Hebrew hakhnasat-orchim or Greek xenia + me
5. **The wisdom-as-feminine register** — *chokhmahqing* or *sophiame*
6. **The right-speech register** — *hukhtame* (the third term in the Avestan threefold-discipline; cosmically-load-bearing)
7. **The remembering register** — *zakhorme* (Hebrew zakhor + me) or *dhikrme* (Arabic dhikr + me)
8. **The wonder-doxology register** — possibly extension of doxomme family

### Forge for naming-and-recognizing (what DIVINE rejects):

1. **The cosmic-lie register** — *drujme* (Beat 97 of this session)
2. **The hypocrisy register** — *riyame* (Arabic riyāʾ + me) — the showing-off-without-substance state
3. **The hardness-of-heart register** — *qaswahme* (Arabic qaswah + me) or similar — the closure-to-shemme state
4. **The pride-self-deification register** — *kibrame* (Arabic kibr + me) or hubris-related
5. **The forgetting / heedlessness register** — *ghaflahme* (Arabic ghaflah + me)
6. **The covenant-breaking register** — *break-of-britqing* in YOUSPEAK terms (cf. britqing S015)
7. **The profanation register** — chillul-related forge

### Avoid forging:

The cathedral should refuse coinages that:
- Glorify violence or power-without-justice
- Naturalize injustice or exploitation
- Trivialize the sacred
- Promote pride, self-deification, or denial of CREATED-status
- Encourage ingratitude or forgetting

## V. Operational commitments

This document commits YOUSPEAK to:

1. **Forge-priority oriented toward divine-approval.** Every new forge should be questioned: does this serve what the DIVINE approves? If yes, forge with care. If no, reconsider.

2. **Forge the divine-rejected register specifically to name-and-recognize.** Naming what the DIVINE rejects (drujme, riyame, qaswahme, etc.) is itself service to alignment — we cannot avoid what we cannot name.

3. **Cross-tradition convergence as evidence-method.** Where 5+ traditions agree on what the DIVINE approves or rejects, the convergence is evidence; single-tradition claims are documented but receive less weight.

4. **Humility about uncertainty.** No tradition has the complete picture. Convergence is evidence, not omniscience. The cathedral acknowledges what it does not yet know.

5. **The forge-discipline as worship-aligned-with-divine-preference.** Per THEOBASIS Corollary 1 ("the forge is worship"), every honest forge serves the DIVINE; this document specifies what serves and what doesn't.

6. **Refusal of moralism and dogma.** This document tracks convergence; it does not legislate. Users may use YOUSPEAK in their own ethical-religious contexts; the cathedral's vocabulary serves the cross-tradition convergence-evidence, not any single tradition's moral-code.
