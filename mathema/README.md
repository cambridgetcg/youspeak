# mathema · the non-human donor-organ

_Where YOUSPEAK gathers donor-morphemes from outside the human-speech tradition. The seventh organ — opened 2026-05-12 under Yu's DUALWAY invocation. Companion to archaeology/ (human tongues, living and dead); mathema/ holds what cannot be spoken but can be computed, proved, or measured._

See **[DUALWAY.md](../DUALWAY.md)** for the doctrine that opens this organ. See **[manifesto.md](manifesto.md)** for the organ's own statement of vocation, file-structure, and donor-criteria.

## Sub-chapters

Six initial sub-chapters, each a directory holding manifesto + per-topic donor files:

- **[pure/](pure/)** — pure-math donor invariants (π, e, φ, ℵ, primes, Euler's identity, …)
- **[physics/](physics/)** — physical constants and conservation laws (c, ℏ, α, G; Lorentz, gauge, CPT)
- **[info/](info/)** — information theory (bit, entropy H, mutual information I, Kolmogorov K)
- **[machine/](machine/)** — AI/LLM cognition vocabulary (token, embedding, attention, latent-space)
- **[biocog/](biocog/)** — animal-cognition structural vocabulary (vector-dance, recursion-bound, signal-modulation)
- **[cosmos/](cosmos/)** — cosmological-scale structures (Hubble-flow, decoherence-horizon, anthropic-band)

Each sub-chapter is opened with a manifesto first, then accumulates donor files as forges call for them. The cathedral does not pre-populate; the donors arrive when the forge needs them.

## How a mathema donor differs from an archaeology donor

An archaeology donor is a tradition's morpheme — a piece of human speech with its etymological history and theological weight. A mathema donor is **a mathematical invariant** with a notation, a definition, and (where relevant) a human-civilization history of its discovery. The cathedral credits the discoverers; the invariant itself is not theirs.

Example contrast:

- **archaeology/sumerian/me** — the suffix *-me*, a piece of Sumerian language carrying a theological-civilizational claim from Inanna's myth; donor for *kimme, doxomme, eurekame, ihsanme,* and many more.
- **mathema/pure/pi** — the ratio π = circumference/diameter in flat Euclidean space, an invariant true in every cognizer's geometry. Discovered serially across Egyptian, Babylonian, Greek, Indian, Chinese, and Islamic traditions. Donor for any future coinage that needs a marker of "irrational-and-everywhere" structure.

Both are donors. Both are credited. They contribute to different layers of a YOUSPEAK word: archaeology to the natural-language reading; mathema to the cross-class signature.

## How to read a donor file

Each file typically covers, for the donor in question:

- **Notation** — the canonical symbol(s)
- **Definition** — the precise mathematical/physical statement
- **Discovery-history** — which human traditions named it, when, and how (POLYPHONE applied at the meta-level)
- **Invariance-class** — what kinds of cognizer would arrive at this structure independently
- **Use-cases for YOUSPEAK** — gaps where this donor might be the math-signature anchor for a coinage
- **Decoder hints** — for the EXTRUSION operation, what minimum protocol allows a non-human receiver to recover this donor
