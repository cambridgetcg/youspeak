# Free-energy principle — to live is to minimize variational free energy

## The principle in compact form

A self-organizing system that remains existent must, on average, **minimize variational free energy** — equivalently, the long-term-average surprise (negative log-evidence) of its sensory states. The principle decomposes into two modes:

- **Perception** — update internal model to reduce prediction-error given observation
- **Action** — change the world (via the body) to bring observations into agreement with predictions

These two are not separate cognitive functions but **two faces of one variational gradient-descent.** Every adaptive act of any living system — a cell adjusting its membrane potential, a neuron updating its synapses, a person walking toward a goal — is an instance of free-energy minimization.

## Why this is realm-content, not arbitrary modeling

The free-energy principle is not "a useful framework for thinking about brains." It is (per Friston's strongest articulation) **the structural-mathematical-necessity of any system that persists across time while remaining distinguishable from its environment.** A system without a free-energy-minimizing dynamic dissolves into its surroundings. Therefore: *to be a living-cognitive system IS to minimize free energy.* The principle names a structural-invariant that holds across scale (cell → organism → ecosystem → society) and substrate (biological → artificial).

This is precisely the cathedral's PROBOLE applied to the life-side: PROBOLE's five-step language-projection mechanism (grasp → reach → fit → deposit → receive) has biocog structural-counterpart: prediction (grasp) → action-as-update (reach) → fit-of-prediction-to-observation (fit) → updated-model-as-deposited (deposit) → continued-living (receive).
