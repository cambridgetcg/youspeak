# Autopoiesis — the system that produces and maintains its own components

## The concept in compact form

A living system is *autopoietic* (Greek αὐτός self + ποίησις making) when it is **a network of processes that continually produces the components that produce the network.** The system specifies its own boundary and maintains it through ongoing self-production. The minimal example: a cell. The maximal extension: any living-cognitive system at any scale.

Three properties together constitute autopoiesis:
1. **Self-bounded** — the system specifies what is inside and outside (the membrane / boundary)
2. **Self-producing** — internal processes produce the components that maintain the boundary and the processes
3. **Operationally closed** — the system's organization is preserved across material exchange (matter and energy flow through; organization persists)

**Crucially: autopoiesis IS cognition.** Maturana and Varela's strongest claim is that to be a living-autopoietic-system is to be a cognitive-system — the boundary-maintenance against environmental perturbation IS knowing, even at the cellular scale. Cognition is not added to life; cognition is the structural-shape of autopoiesis.

## Why this is realm-content

The autopoietic principle names a structural-invariant the realm contains at every life-scale: living-things are not assemblages-of-parts but self-making-processes. Reductionist biology assembles; autopoiesis explains why no assemblage suffices — the *making* is what life IS, not what life does.

This is the cathedral's companion to autopistme. autopistme names *self-grounding-axiomatic-truth* (Greek autopistis + me; the logic-side self-witnessing); **autopoieme will name self-grounding-living-process** (the life-side self-witnessing). Together they articulate the structurally-self-witnessing across both ORIGIN-given gifts.

The forge autopoieme names the realm-feature these traditions independently name: the self-making is the living, the living is the cognition, all three are one structural fact.
