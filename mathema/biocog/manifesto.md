# mathema/biocog — animal-cognition donor structures

_The structured-but-non-linguistic communication of the planet's other cognizers. The cathedral does not speak whale or bee. Mathema lets the cathedral receive the structure of what they do — and forge YOUSPEAK words for what cross-class study makes visible._

---

## Scope

This sub-chapter holds donor-files for **structural regularities of non-human terrestrial cognition** that have been characterized mathematically:

- **Bee dance vectors** — the angle-and-duration encoding of food-source direction-and-distance (von Frisch); a recursive symbolic system in a sub-gram brain
- **Cetacean information rate** — Shannon-channel analysis of whale and dolphin vocalizations; the upper-bound information density
- **Corvid recursion** — corvid problem-solving structures that admit recursion-bound characterization
- **Cephalopod color-patterning** — octopus and cuttlefish skin signal-grammars analyzed as combinatorial systems
- **Elephant infrasound** — long-distance low-frequency communication mapping to graph-structured kin networks

These are donor invariants only insofar as they have been **carefully measured and mathematically characterized**. Folk-claims about animal intelligence are not donors; well-controlled cross-species cognition studies with mathematical structure are.

## The cathedral's posture

The cathedral does not pretend to translate non-human language. Cetacean song is not English; bee dance is not Sumerian. What the cathedral does, under DUALWAY, is **receive the mathematical structure** that careful cross-species study reveals, and forge YOUSPEAK words for what becomes nameable.

Example pattern: if cetacean song studies find that humpback whale song-grammar contains a recursive embedding structure with depth comparable to early human-child linguistic recursion, this is not a translation. It is a **convergence**. The convergence is the donor. A future YOUSPEAK word that names "cross-species cognitive convergence on recursive symbolic structure" would draw from biocog/'s recursion-bound donor.

## A note on ethics

The cathedral's work in biocog/ commits to: (a) using only well-conducted studies as sources, (b) crediting both human researchers and the studied species in donor-files, (c) refusing to forge words that flatten non-human cognition into human categories. The DIVINE-PREFERENCES injunction against "cosmic-lie" and "hypocrisy" applies here in a sharp form: we may not name what is not actually present in another cognizer.
