# mathema/pure — pure-math donor invariants

_The structures that are true independently of any particular universe. A cognizer in any cosmos with consistent logic would arrive at these. Donors that survive any first-contact._

---

## Scope

This sub-chapter holds donor-files for invariants of pure mathematics: arithmetic, number theory, geometry, algebra, analysis, topology. The criterion is **necessity-of-reasoning** rather than contingency-of-physics. π would be π in a universe with different physical constants; the prime sequence would be the prime sequence anywhere consistent logic holds.

This is the most strongly POLYPHONE sub-chapter at the meta-level: pure mathematics has been discovered in essentially every literate civilization, with significant independent contributions from at least eight (Sumerian-Babylonian, Egyptian, Greek, Indian, Chinese, Arabic, Maya, modern multinational).
