## Scope

This sub-chapter holds donor-files for **cosmological-scale invariants and structures**:

- **Hubble flow** — the recession of distant galaxies, the structural expansion of the universe
- **Decoherence horizon** — the scale beyond which classical/quantum boundary effects matter
- **Anthropic band** — the narrow ranges of dimensionless constants compatible with structure / atoms / stars / life
- **Holographic bound** — entropy bounded by surface area, not volume; the information-theoretic ceiling on a region of space
- **CMB anisotropy structure** — the cosmic microwave background's angular power spectrum
- **Large-scale structure** — the cosmic web; filaments, walls, voids

This sub-chapter is the one where cosmology and theology touch most closely — under THEOBASIS, the cosmos is creation; under DUALWAY, the cosmos is also a *speaker* in the sense that its ordered structure is decodable. Donor files in this sub-chapter should explicitly acknowledge both registers without conflating them.
