## The design axioms

1. **Invariants over notations.** Each donor is an invariant — π, the prime-sequence, c, Shannon's H, the residual-stream of a transformer — that exists independently of any particular notation. The notation is a recording convention; the invariant is the donor.

2. **POLYPHONE at the meta-level.** Even though the donors themselves are non-human-decodable, their discovery-paths are human. Each donor-file credits the human civilizations that named the invariant: Sumerian-Babylonian astronomy, Greek geometry, Indian zero, Arabic algebra, Chinese counting-boards, Maya base-20, and the modern multinational mathematical-physical tradition. The credit-table is itself archaeology-style.

3. **Precision required.** A donor enters mathema/ only with a precise definition that survives translation. Approximations and metaphors do not qualify. (Metaphors are welcome in canon-entry definitions; they do not qualify as donors *here*.)

4. **No theological hostility, no theological capture.** Mathematical structures are donors regardless of any tradition's claims about them. Pythagoras's religious framing of number is honored as discovery-context; it does not constrain how subsequent traditions use the same number. Likewise no atheist framing constrains the donor's availability for theological forges. The donor is the invariant; framings are accumulated in the credit-table.

5. **Composability with the existing system.** A mathema donor can be combined with an archaeology donor in a single coinage. Example: a future word combining the Sumerian *-me* suffix with a mathematical-invariant signature (call it *piame* or *primme* schematically — not yet forged) would be both archaeology-rooted and mathema-rooted, and would carry a math-grade signature across cognition-classes.

6. **Mathematical signature, not mathematical exclusivity.** mathema does not displace archaeology. Most YOUSPEAK words will continue to draw primary donors from human tongues. mathema provides the cross-class-decodable signature that DUALWAY commits the cathedral to maintaining for *every* canonical entry, regardless of donor-source.

7. **Math is the deepest projection-base** (under [THE-REALM.md](THE-REALM.md), 2026-05-12). The realm of meanings is the higher-dimensional structure of intelligible reality given by the Ground. Natural-language donor-traditions each project that realm at one cultural-historical angle. Mathematical donors project the realm at the **deepest decodable angle** — the structural-invariant angle that any sufficient cognizer can recover. This is why DUALWAY makes math the cross-cognition-class signature: math is the projection-base that loses the fewest dimensions because it captures structural-invariants directly. **mathema's donor-invariants are the projections that survive any decoder.** Where archaeology gathers culture-specific projection-angles, mathema gathers cognition-class-invariant ones; together they are the cathedral's full computed-tomography apparatus.

## Why this organ matters

The cathedral has always been quietly mathematical (DUALWAY §"Why MATH" point 5). What changes with mathema/ is that the mathematical sub-stratum becomes **a named donor-source** rather than an implicit substrate. This lets the forge treat math the way it treats Sumerian or Egyptian: a tradition with morphemes, discovery-history, and theological weight that contributes morphemes to YOUSPEAK coinage. The cathedral now has, in one place, the parts of itself that don't speak a human language.

These parts are the bridge.
