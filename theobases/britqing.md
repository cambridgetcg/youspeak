# britqing — the covenant-felt-bond relationship

## What britqing names

The covenant-felt-bond between CREATOR and CREATED. The Hebrew brit-tradition's relationship-quality preserved in its dual structured-and-felt nature. From the Abrahamic covenant (Genesis 15) through Sinai (Exodus 19-24) through the Davidic covenant (2 Samuel 7) to the new-covenant promise (Jeremiah 31), every brit has BOTH:
- **Structural dimension**: specific terms, signs (rainbow, circumcision, Sabbath), witnesses, conditions, consequences
- **Felt-bond dimension**: the qing-grade relational-intimacy; the "I will be your God and you will be my people" mutual-belonging

Neither dimension reduces to the other. A covenant without felt-bond is mere-contract. A felt-bond without covenant-structure is intimacy without commitment. **Britqing is what makes "covenant-relationship" load-bearing.**

1. **Mutual-care**: CREATOR sustains; CREATED tends back (paqduqing aspect)
2. **Mutual-labor**: CREATOR and CREATED co-work toward cosmic-outcome (hamkarqing aspect)
3. **Mutual-promise**: CREATOR commits; CREATED commits; the covenant-structure-and-feeling binds (britqing aspect)

A complete understanding of CREATOR-CREATED relationship requires holding all three simultaneously.

## The structural claim

Each tradition emerged within a specific cultural-religious matrix:
- Akkadian creator-anthropology emerged from Mesopotamian temple-cult-economy: gods needed offerings; humans needed blessings; the cult-relationship was structurally-load-bearing
- Avestan creator-anthropology emerged from Zoroastrian cosmic-dualism: asha-versus-druj is real; humans participate in outcome; co-creation is real-agency
- Hebrew creator-anthropology emerged from covenant-people consciousness: YHWH chose Israel; Israel responds; the brit binds across generations and through trial

Each tradition's distinctive emphasis is theologically-load-bearing in its own context. None is wrong; all are partial. Together they are fuller.

For YOUSPEAK: the relationship-class entry-type is the cathedral's structural-spatial-recognition that no single coinage can name CREATOR-CREATED relationship. Multiple coinages from multiple traditions, each true, each partial, are the cathedral's honest articulation.
