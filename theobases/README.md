# theobases — the dual-pole groundings

_For each creature-experience YOUSPEAK has named, there is a divine-attribute that grounds the experience. The creature-pole names what is felt; the divine-pole names what makes the felt-thing possible. The relation is theobasis-instantiation: theobasis (THE Ground) grounds reality at large; each pair-entry tracks how a specific creature-experience-word is grounded in a specific divine-attribute._

_This organ exists because the dual-pole map identified in Session 005 Beat 33 and tracked in Session 007 forge-status.md has its own structural coherence — distinct from convergences (which is cross-tradition) and from canon (which is single-word). The pair is the unit. The pair-relation is theological._

---

## What a theobasis-pair is

A theobasis-pair is a registered pairing of:

1. **A creature-pole word** — a YOUSPEAK Core or Specialized entry that names a creature-experience-state (e.g., walkekin, kimme, shemme)
2. **A divine-pole word** — a YOUSPEAK divine-property word (Core or Specialized; possibly forged or candidate) that names the divine-attribute grounding the creature-pole (e.g., walkehesed, theokimme, theoshemme)
3. **The grounding-relation** — a theological claim about HOW the divine-pole grounds the creature-pole

The name "theobases" is plural of theobasis (Core, Session 001) — the foundational divine-Ground word. Each pair is a SPECIFIC INSTANCE of theobasis-grounding: not the universal theobasis itself but a particular grounding-relation between one divine-attribute and one creature-experience.

Theologically: theobasis is the universal Ground; theobases (plural-instantiation) are the specific groundings recognizable in YOUSPEAK's vocabulary structure.

## Why this organ exists

Session 005 Beat 33 sketched the dual-pole map: each Core creature-state word has a divine-source companion. Session 007 forge-status.md tracked the 11 dual-pole candidates as a table.

Per SENSIBILITY.md observation:
- The dual-pole structure is not the same as cross-tradition convergence (which is property-by-property, multi-tradition)
- It is not the same as single-word canon-entry (which is word-by-word)
- It is the relation between paired-words, the genealogy from divine-attribute to creature-experience
- The pair-relation has no organ-home; the data lives only in scattered beat-summaries and a tracker-row

The new organ that this overflow asks for: **theobases/** — sibling to convergences/, where each entry is a paired-grounding-relation rather than a single-property cross-tradition map.

## File structure

Each theobasis-pair is a single file in this directory, named for the pair: `<creature-pole>-<divine-pole>.md`. Format:

```yaml
---
pair: <creature-pole-word> ↔ <divine-pole-word>
creature_pole: <Core or Specialized YOUSPEAK word>
creature_pole_status: <Core / Specialized>
divine_pole: <YOUSPEAK divine-property word, forged or candidate>
divine_pole_status: <Core / Specialized refine / candidate>
grounding_relation: <one-line description of how divine-pole grounds creature-pole>
---

# <creature-pole> ↔ <divine-pole>

## The creature-pole

<the creature-experience-state named; cross-reference to canon entry>

## The divine-pole

<the divine-attribute named; cross-reference to canon or experiment>

## The grounding-relation

<the theological claim: how the divine-pole grounds the creature-pole; what the pair-structure attests>

## Cross-tradition support (if applicable)

<reference to convergences/ entry if the divine-pole has a cross-tradition convergence map>

## Cross-references

- canon entries: <list>
- experiment files: <list>
- convergences/ entry: <if applicable>
- session beats: <list>
```

### Note on entry-classes

theobases/ now houses **two entry-classes**:

- **Pair-entries**: name a creature-pole word, a divine-pole word, and the grounding-relation between them. The pair-entry shows how a specific divine-attribute grounds a specific creature-experience.
- **Relationship-entries**: name a single word that BOTH poles share — the bidirectional relationship-itself. The relationship-entry shows what CREATOR-and-CREATED do toward each other in one named-quality.

The expansion was occasioned by the forge of *paqduqing* (Session 012), which inherently named the bidirectional-relationship rather than one pole. Per SENSIBILITY: the entry-classes expand to accommodate what forges produce.

## Three theological commitments

This organ rests on three claims that follow from THEOBASIS:

1. **Every creature-experience that YOUSPEAK can rightly name reflects a divine-attribute that grounds it.** Under THEOBASIS Corollary 2, reality witnesses its Ground; specific reality-witnessings have specific divine-attributes as their genealogical-source.

2. **The pair-relation is one of grounding-instantiation, not mere correspondence.** The divine-pole does not merely "match" the creature-pole; it grounds-and-makes-possible the creature-pole. Walkekin (creatures' bond-through-silence) exists because walkehesed (GoD's bond-through-silence as covenant-faithfulness) is structurally prior. The creature reflects-and-participates-in what the divine grounds.

3. **The map is unfinished by design.** Eight dual-pole candidates remain unforged. Many more pairings will become recognizable as the cathedral grows. The atlas of theobases is endless because GoD is endless; YOUSPEAK names what it can.

## Distinction from sister-organs

- **convergences/** — property-by-property; tracks cross-tradition attestations of a single divine-property; many traditions converging
- **theobases/** — pair-by-pair; tracks paired-grounding-relations; one creature-pole + one divine-pole
- **canon/** — word-by-word; single-word entries
- **labs/logos/experiments/** — forge-genealogy of specific coinages

A property may appear in multiple organs simultaneously: walkehesed has an entry in `convergences/bond-through-long-silence.md` (cross-tradition) AND in `theobases/walkekin-walkehesed.md` (pair-grounding) AND in `labs/logos/experiments/core/126-walkehesed.md` (forge-record). Each organ's entry serves a distinct function; together they triangulate.
