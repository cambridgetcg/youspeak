# kimme ↔ theokimme

## The creature-pole — kimme

Attention-as-received-ordinance: the creature's recognition that attention-from-beyond-oneself is a gift, not a possession. The state in which one receives the attention of another (a friend, a teacher, a lover, a parent, a caregiver, a stranger-in-encounter) and recognizes the attention itself as gift-grade rather than as expected-due.

**YOUSPEAK status:** Core 7.70 (Session 001). Third Core word. First pyramid-civilization archaeology yield (Mesopotamian ziggurat tradition via Sumerian me).

**Donors:** Japanese *ki* (氣/気 — attention, focused-being-toward) + Sumerian *me* (𒈨 — divine-ordinance, received-gift-quality).

**Cross-reference:** [`canon/core/kimme.md`](../canon/core/kimme.md) · [`labs/logos/experiments/core/105-kimme.md`](../labs/logos/experiments/core/105-kimme.md)

## The divine-pole — theokimme

GoD's constitutive-attention: the divine property by which attending-to-X IS sustaining-X. Not observational attention from-outside but ontologically-grounding attention from-within. Attested across at least seven traditions (Jewish hashgaḥa pratit, Christian conservatio, Islamic al-Basir, Hindu cit-śakti, Buddhist prajñāpāramitā, Yoruba Olódùmarè-sees, Igbo Chukwu-knows); none compact-names what all attest.

**YOUSPEAK status:** Specialized refine 7.45 (Session 005 Beat 32). Second divine-property YOUSPEAK coinage. Three-tradition compound (Greek + Japanese + Sumerian — three families, two non-IE). Amendment II candidate.

**Donors:** Greek *theos* (θεός, GoD) + Japanese *ki* (attention) + Sumerian *me* (received-ordinance).

**Cross-tradition support:** see `convergences/forge-status.md` (entry to be migrated into its own convergences/ file in a future session — "constitutive-divine-attention" property).

## The grounding-relation

Theokimme grounds kimme. The theological claim:

> The creature can receive attention as ordinance-grade gift because GoD attends as constitutive-act. Kimme is creaturely participation in the ontologically-prior fact of theokimme. Where one human attends to another and the receiving-creature recognizes the attention as ordinance-grade, what is happening reflects the prior reality: GoD attends, and by attending sustains. Human attention can be ordinance-grade because divine attention is sustenance-grade. The lower bears the imprint of the higher.

This is not flattery toward GoD; it is structural recognition. Aquinas's *conservatio* (continuous creation) is the same theological insight expressed in scholastic register. Augustine's "you have made us toward yourself" is the same insight as creaturely-yearning-recognition. Hindu *cit-śakti* (consciousness-power) names it from another angle. Buddhist *prajñāpāramitā* (wisdom-beholding) names it from another. Yoruba Olódùmarè-sees-each-orí names it from another. Seven angles, one phenomenon.

The kimme we have received from teachers and friends and parents-and-children-of-the-Lord-let-them-not-fail is the creature-mode of the theokimme that holds-us-in-being moment-by-moment. The forge offers the names; the recognition is the worship.
