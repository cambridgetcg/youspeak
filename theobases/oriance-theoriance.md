## The grounding-relation

Theoriance grounds oriance. The theological claim:

> The creature can align with its destined-trajectory (oriance) because the trajectory was first allocated by the One who allocates trajectories (theoriance). The Yoruba metaphysics makes this explicit: each person chose their orí in pre-existence in conversation with Olódùmarè; once-born, the life-task is alignment-with-the-already-allocated-trajectory. Oriance is not self-construction of a life-shape but participation-in-an-already-given-shape.

This is theologically distinctive. The classical-Western-modern-secular trajectory of "self-actualization" treats the life-shape as something the self constructs via choices and effort. The Yoruba register (and theoriance-grounding) holds the inverse: the life-shape is allocated; the self participates by alignment; the allocation is gift, not achievement.

For the cathedral: theoriance complements walkehesed (S005), theokimme (S005), theoshemme (S008) as the fourth named-divine-property in the dual-pole map. The pair structure shows: oriance is creaturely-participation in what theoriance grounds.

## Cross-tradition support

The divine-trajectory-allocator register is broadly attested:

- **Yoruba**: Olódùmarè allocates orí pre-birth; explicit and central
- **Akkadian**: the Anunnaki assembly decrees šīmtu (destiny); per archaeology/akkadian/creators-and-human.md
- **Christian**: providence; predestination (especially in Augustinian/Calvinistic register); explicitly theological
- **Hindu**: karmic-trajectory mediated by Iśvara
- **Islamic**: qadar (divine-decree of destiny)
- **Avestan**: the Amesha Spentas have specific creation-allocations though less explicitly predestinarian
- **Hebrew**: God's foreknowledge and ordering (less explicitly predestinarian than Christian but related)

Six-tradition convergence on the divine-trajectory-allocator register. The cross-tradition evidence supports the forge's gap-validity.
