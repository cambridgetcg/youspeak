# hamkarqing — the co-creation relationship

## What hamkarqing names

The cooperative-co-creation relationship between CREATOR and CREATED. Humans-as-hamkar (co-workers, allies, collaborators) WITH Ahura Mazda in the cosmic struggle of asha against druj. Each asha-aligned human action contributes to cosmic-outcome; the cosmic-battle requires human-participation; CREATED has agency-in-cosmic-result.

This is theologically-distinct from servant-vocation (ardume), recipient-of-ordinance (kimme), and asymmetric-reciprocity-custodial-care (paqduqing). It names a register no other tradition compact-articulates: the human-side-of-co-creation as felt-bonded-partnership with the divine-side.

The Zoroastrian preservation is theologically-precise. In Akkadian creation-anthropology, humans are made *ana ardūtim* (into servitude) to bear the labor the gods grew tired of bearing. In Zoroastrian creation-anthropology, humans are **hamkar** (co-workers) with Ahura Mazda in renovating cosmos toward frashegird. The two registers are not contradictory but complementary aspects of the broader CREATOR-CREATED relationship; YOUSPEAK preserves both via two distinct coinages (ardume + hamkarqing).

## The relationship-class — now two entries

The theobases/ relationship-class opened with paqduqing (S012). hamkarqing is the second instance. The two together articulate complementary aspects of the CREATOR-CREATED relationship:

| Word | Relationship-aspect named |
|---|---|
| **paqduqing** | asymmetric-reciprocity custodial-care (CREATOR sustains; CREATED receives-and-responds; the bidirectional felt-mutual-tending) |
| **hamkarqing** | cooperative co-creation (CREATOR and CREATED co-labor toward cosmic-outcome; the partnership-grade-bond within ontological-asymmetry) |

Both are true of the same relationship. Paqduqing emphasizes the felt-mutual-CARE (different kinds of contribution from each side; the cult-economy aspect). Hamkarqing emphasizes the felt-mutual-LABOR (shared-direction-of-action; the cosmic-battle aspect).

A complete CREATOR-CREATED relationship articulation requires both registers. Future relationship-class entries may add further aspects (break-of-relationship registers; community-extensions; eschatological-deepening).

## The structural claim

In the Akkadian register the cathedral already houses (S009 archaeology + amelme + ardume), the CREATOR-CREATED relationship is articulated as DECREE + SERVITUDE + RECIPROCAL-CULT. Humans are decreed-into-being (amelme); humans serve as ordinance-vocation (ardume); the cult-economy operates bidirectionally (paqduqing).

In the Zoroastrian register the cathedral now houses (S013 + S014 archaeologies + ashame + hamkarqing), the CREATOR-CREATED relationship adds: COSMIC-COOPERATION + ASHA-PARTICIPATION + CO-WORKER-AGENCY. Humans are hamkar with Ahura Mazda (hamkarqing); humans align with cosmic-truth-order received as gift (ashame); the cosmic-renovation-toward-frashegird requires both divine and human action.

The two registers together — Akkadian and Zoroastrian — articulate a fuller CREATOR-CREATED relationship than either alone. **POLYPHONE asks for both; the cathedral preserves both.**

## Cross-tradition support

The co-creation register has parallels across traditions:

- **Hebrew**: Genesis 2:15 *l'avdah u-l'shamrah* — to serve (avdah) and to tend (shamrah) the garden; co-stewardship register; some-rabbinic-tradition treats humans as God's "junior partners" in creation
- **Christian** (NT Greek): *sunergos* — co-worker (Romans 16:9, 1 Corinthians 3:9 — "we are God's fellow-workers, sunergoi"); Paul's explicit naming of humans as co-laborers with God in mission
- **Vedic Sanskrit**: the *yajamāna* (sacrifice-sponsor) is a co-worker with the gods in maintaining cosmic-order through ritual; the *Bhagavad Gītā* 3.10-13 articulates the gods-and-humans mutual-feeding
- **Buddhist**: the *Bodhisattva-vow* — committing to cosmic-liberation-of-all-beings; the bodhisattva is co-worker with all Buddhas-and-Bodhisattvas in saving sentient-beings
- **Yoruba**: *ọmọlúwàbí* (the complete-character-person) participates in cosmic-order alongside Olódùmarè and the orishas; orí-aligned action contributes to cosmic-good

The co-creation register is broadly attested. Avestan *hamkara* names it most economically and explicitly. The cross-tradition convergence supports the forge's gap-validity.
