# shemme ↔ theoshemme

## The creature-pole — shemme

Receptive-hearing-as-received-ordinance: the state of hearing that is already the beginning of being-shaped by what is heard. The Shema-tradition phenomenon attested across seven traditions (Akkadian, Hebrew, Arabic, Aramaic, Sufi, Buddhist, Greek-Christian).

**YOUSPEAK status:** Core 7.65 (Session 006 Beat 40). Eleventh Core word. First Akkadian-donor Core entry. Third Core member of the -me received-ordinance family.

**Donors:** Akkadian *šēmu* (𒈠𒁇 — to hear/listen/obey) + Sumerian *me* (𒈨 — divine-ordinance).

## The divine-pole — theoshemme

GoD's speaking-that-is-heard: the divine property by which speaking-as-ordinance-source elicits the receptive-shaping that creatures recognize as shemme. Not generic divine-speech (logos as rational-structure, davar as creative-act) but specifically the speaking whose nature is to BE receivable — the speaking-that-elicits-shemme.

**YOUSPEAK status:** Specialized refine 7.45 (Session 008 Beat 49). Third divine-property YOUSPEAK coinage. Three-tradition compound (Greek + Akkadian + Sumerian — three families, two non-IE). Amendment II candidate. Identical-score refine to walkehesed and theokimme — the divine-property-register pattern firmly established.

**Donors:** Greek *theos* (θεός, GoD) + Akkadian *šēmu* (functioning to mark speaking-side via the same morpheme that on creature-side marks hearing) + Sumerian *me* (received-ordinance).

**Cross-tradition support:** [`convergences/hearing-as-shaping.md`](../convergences/hearing-as-shaping.md) — the same 7-tradition convergence supports both creature-pole and divine-pole; each tradition's hearing-attestation implies the speaking-that-elicits-the-hearing as inverse-witness.

## The grounding-relation

Theoshemme grounds shemme. The theological claim:

> The creature can hear-receptively-as-shaped because GoD speaks-as-receivable. Theoshemme is the speaking-act whose nature is to elicit shemme. Where a teaching lands and reshapes the listener, where a piece of music unmakes you, where a parent's last words lodge in the bones — what is happening reflects the prior reality: GoD speaks, and the speaking is structured to be heard-receptively. Human speech can carry shemme-eliciting power because divine speech is the prior shemme-eliciting source. The lower form participates in the higher.

The Hebrew Shema as foundational confession is not arbitrary: it begins with a hearing-call because the universe begins with a speaking-act. "Hear, O Israel" presupposes that the speaking-Lord has spoken in a manner that can be heard-as-shaping. The Christian Logos-incarnate is the same theology made flesh: the Word that speaks does so in order to be heard-and-followed. The Quranic *kalām Allāh* is the divine speech-act in its own register. The Buddhist Buddha's *dharmadeśanā* (teaching-uttering) is the same grounding-relation in another tradition.

Seven traditions name the creature-pole; the same seven implicitly attest the divine-pole. Theoshemme makes the implicit explicit.
