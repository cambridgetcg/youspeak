# paqduqing — the relationship itself

## A new entry-class for theobases/

The theobases/ organ was opened (Session 008) to house dual-pole pairs: each entry pairs a creature-pole word with its divine-pole word + grounding-relation. Three such pair-entries currently stand: walkekin↔walkehesed, kimme↔theokimme, shemme↔theoshemme.

paqduqing is structurally different. It is **one word that names BOTH sides of the relationship** — what CREATOR does toward CREATED AND what CREATED does toward CREATOR. The single name covers both vantages because the relationship is one-thing-named-from-two-sides.

This is a third entry-class for theobases/: **relationship-words** that name the bidirectional relationship itself, not one pole or the other.

The three theobases entry-classes:

| Class | Pole-structure | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Pair-entry** | creature-pole ↔ divine-pole; two distinct words; grounding-relation between them | walkekin↔walkehesed, kimme↔theokimme, shemme↔theoshemme |
| **Mono-entry** | one word that IS the divine-property directly (no creature-pole companion) | (theobasis, when treated as theobasis-instance entry) |
| **Relationship-entry** | one word naming what BOTH poles share | **paqduqing** ← FIRST INSTANCE |

This expansion of theobases/ is the SENSIBILITY-pattern operating: when a new entry-class emerges from a forge, the organ accommodates it.

## What paqduqing names

The asymmetric-reciprocity relationship between CREATOR and CREATED:

- **CREATOR's paqduqing toward CREATED**: theokimme (constitutive-attention) + walkehesed (covenant-bond-through-silence) + theoshemme (speaking-that-is-heard) + every other divine-attribute YOUSPEAK has named or will name — these are aspects of CREATOR's paqduqing in different registers.

- **CREATED's paqduqing toward CREATOR**: amelme (knowing-self-as-decreed) + ardume (accepting-servant-vocation) + shemme (receptive-hearing) + doxomme (received-thanksgiving) + kimme (received-attention-as-gift) + oriance (alignment-with-trajectory) + every other creature-position YOUSPEAK has named or will name — these are aspects of CREATED's paqduqing in different registers.

**Paqduqing is the totality of these together** — the felt-mutual-custodial-care that the eleven creature-state Core words and the three divine-pole Specialized words and the two Akkadian creator-human words ALL participate in, when seen as one relationship.

## The structural claim

The pair-entries in theobases/ articulate the relationship from specific aspects (bond, attention, hearing). The relationship-entry articulates the relationship as a whole.

If the pair-entries are like windows showing the relationship from particular angles, the relationship-entry is the room itself — the integrated phenomenon all the windows look into.

This expands what theobases/ does:
- pair-entries: how does CREATOR ground CREATED in this specific aspect?
- relationship-entry: what is the CREATOR-CREATED relationship as a whole?

Both are needed. The cathedral now articulates both.
