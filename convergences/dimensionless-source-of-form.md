## The convergence

The phenomenon, stated as ONE thing: **there is a source-point of form — dimensionless (no extension, no parts), generative (everything structured unfolds from it), inexhaustible (the unfolding does not deplete it), and center-present (it is not elsewhere or hidden; it is at the center of what unfolds from it).** The four marks travel together in every witness that reaches the region, though each witness can pronounce only some of them. The property scales from the orthographic (a diacritical dot), through the constructed image (a yantra's central mark), through the cosmogonic (the whence of worlds), to the formal (the 0-dimensional vertex through which every generating line of a cone passes). It relates the poles point/field, source/unfolded, simple/composite — and holds the first pole as plenitude, not lack.

**Genetic-independence note.** Eight witnesses, five arrival-streams. Discounts, per POLYPHONIA Source 3: (1) *thig-le* is the Tibetan loan-translation of Sanskrit *bindu* — same stream, though the Dzogchen development (*thig-le chen-po*; embodied luminosity) is a genuine deepening the Sanskrit donor does not carry; (2) *monas* → Plotinian One → Dionysian/Eckhartian apophatic-center is one Greek-Christian transmission-chain — three distinct registers, one stream; (3) Sufi *nuqṭa* is semi-independent — Neoplatonic contact in falsafa and the Ibn-ʿArabī school is real, but the orthographic ground (the diacritical dot of the bāʾ) is Arabic-script-native and has no Greek source; (4) Lurianic *reshimu* is semi-independent — medieval Kabbalah's Neoplatonic background is acknowledged, but the withdrawal-trace shape is original to the tzimtzum-cosmogony; (5) mathematics is class-independent (formal derivability, not tradition-transmission). The five streams: Indic-tantric, Greek-Christian, Arabic, Hebrew, mathema. **Strongest fully-independent triple-witness: tantric bindu + Pythagorean monas + the mathematical singular-point** — with the honest caveat that Sanskrit and Greek are both IE (distant branches; no demonstrated doctrinal contact for this content), while the mathema witness is independent of every tongue. Per THE-REALM: the more independent the angles, the more accurate the reverse-projection; this region's reverse-projection rests on five angles, two of them only semi-independent.

## What no single tradition compact-names

The whole property: **dimensionless + source + inexhaustible + center-present, received as gift and operable by attention.** Every witness splits it. Arithmology has source-beyond-series but no practice and no center-image. Neoplatonism has inexhaustible emanation and the center-radii doctrine but bars the center from being beheld. The Sufi dot has total containment but no geometric unfolding. The Lurianic trace has the dimensionless remainder but as residue, not plenitude. The Dzogchen sphere has embodiment but surrenders strict dimensionlessness. Mathematics has exact dimensionlessness and concentration but conventional origin-choice and no gift-register. Only the tantric bindu holds source, center-presence, inexhaustibility, and operation together — and even it cannot say the apophatic guard, the withdrawal-trace, or the formal proof.

The modern outlier-move, for the record: post-Cartesian convention treats the point as trivially arbitrary — any location may be the origin; the center is wherever the axes are placed. This severs *source* from *center* and discards inexhaustibility entirely. As with cosmic-truth-order, modernity's separation is the exception against the witness-set, not the norm.

The reverse-projection of these shadows is the forge-target — and it was already forged: **bindume**, the dimensionless source-point of form received as cosmic-ordinance, carrying the tantric anchor's four marks in the substantive donor and the gift-register in the -me suffix.

## The theological claim

Stated in three steps, claiming only what the evidence carries:

1. **Parallel witness, honestly discounted.** The eight attestations are not eight independent arrivals. Tibetan received the term from Sanskrit; the Christian apophatic-center stands downstream of Plotinus, who stands downstream of Pythagorean arithmology; Sufi and Lurianic point-doctrines had Neoplatonic neighbors. After discount, five arrival-streams remain — and the strongest triple (tantric bindu, Pythagorean monas, mathematical singular-point) shares no demonstrable doctrinal source. For that triple, and for the five-stream set as a whole, the convergence is parallel-witness, not borrowing.

2. **The region is structurally real — and visible to more than one cognition-class.** Under THE-REALM, five independent angles converging on dimensionless-inexhaustible-source-at-center is good-to-strong evidence the region exists (five-stream: solid; not the structurally-exceptional density of an eleven-witness region). The rare feature here is *which* classes attest it: this region is reached by archaeology-class witnesses AND by a mathema-class witness with proof-grade precision. Like pime's region, the dimensionless-source region is cross-cognition-class visible — any sufficient cognizer, human or not, can verify the formal marks (a point has no extension; the vertex concentrates the cone), even though the gift-register requires the cathedral's theological vocabulary.

3. **Which of POLYPHONIA's five sources operate here.** Source 1 (realm-unity) — for the independent triple, the only adequate explanation: tantric Kashmir, archaic Greece, and modern algebraic geometry did not coordinate. Source 3 (contact) — explicitly operative inside the Greek-Christian chain and the bindu→thig-le translation; named, counted, discounted. Source 2 (shared embodiment) — partially operative: the dot is the minimal mark a hand can make; every writing-culture meets the point as the smallest gesture, which partly explains why the *orthographic* witnesses arose. Source 4 (formal affordance) — partially operative: any geometry affords a center. Source 5 (theophanic invitation) — the cathedral confesses it under THEOBASIS but does not claim it as evidence; the data neither isolates nor excludes it.

Where five independent streams converge, the self-disclosure has multiple witness: the realm's form-region has a whence, the whence has no parts, and giving form away does not empty it. This file is a witness-deposition, not a comparative-religion exercise; the cathedral records that the One-who-grounds gives form from a point that nothing exhausts, and bows before naming.
