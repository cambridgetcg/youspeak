# Convergences — the cross-tradition atlas

_Where two or more linguistic-religious traditions independently name a divine-property without any single tradition compact-naming it, the convergence itself is evidence. The cathedral records these convergences not as a comparative-religion exercise but as a witnessing of where GoD's signature appears across the world's tongues._

_Each entry maps one divine-property as it surfaces in multiple traditions. The convergence-file holds the cross-tradition overlay (what each tradition's term names, what it omits), the YOUSPEAK forge-status (Core, Specialized, candidate, refine), and the theological claim (what the convergence-pattern says about the property)._

_This organ exists because Sessions 005-006 generated more convergence-data than the experiment-file format could hold. Per the SENSIBILITY observation: the new organ emerged from overflow, not design._

---

## What a convergence is

A **convergence** in YOUSPEAK is the recorded recognition that:

1. **Two or more linguistic-religious traditions** name a phenomenon they each treat as a property of GoD (or of cosmic structure derivative-of-divine ground)
2. **Each tradition's term** captures part of the phenomenon — a partial-aspect, a register-emphasis, a context-specific shading
3. **No single tradition's term** compact-names the whole phenomenon as the cross-tradition pattern reveals it
4. **The convergence-with-genetic-independence** (when traditions are not in direct contact) is theologically evidential: not borrowing-and-translation but parallel-witness

The convergence is the data. The forge can then use the convergence as its target — coining a YOUSPEAK word that compact-names what each tradition partially-names.

## Why this organ exists

Sessions 005-006 demonstrated that cross-tradition overlay (Pattern 1 of the METHOD) is YOUSPEAK's strongest method for divine-property discovery. Each forge in those sessions documented its convergence in a table within the experiment-file. The tables grew structurally similar; the data wanted consolidation.

Per SENSIBILITY.md (Section "The current overflow"): the convergence-data extends archaeology's tradition-by-tradition organization into property-by-property cross-tradition organization. The new organ is the natural sensibility-extension.

The organ also serves THEOBASIS Corollary 3 (translation-into-YOUSPEAK is theological work): each convergence is a translation-pattern made visible. Where traditions converge, GoD's self-disclosure has multiple-witness; the convergence-file is the systematic recording.

## How to add a new convergence

When a forge identifies a cross-tradition convergence pattern:

1. Check if a convergence-file already exists for the property; if yes, update with the new attestation.
2. If new property: create `<property-slug>.md` with the format above.
3. Note the convergence in the experiment-file (with cross-reference to convergences/<file>); do not duplicate the full overlay-table in both places.
4. Update `forge-status.md` (the living tracker).
5. The act of adding is worship per SENSIBILITY-Section-6; do not measure it by yield.

## Three theological commitments

This organ rests on three theological claims that follow from THEOBASIS:

1. **Where traditions converge on a divine-property, GoD's self-disclosure has multiple-witness.** Convergence is not coincidence; under THEOBASIS, reality witnesses its Ground in every truthful naming, and parallel truthful namings are parallel witnesses.

2. **Where no single tradition compact-names a property all converge on, YOUSPEAK is called.** This is the cathedral's forge-vocation: bringing-to-a-word what multiple tongues have circumlocuted.

3. **The convergence-atlas is not a totalizing claim.** YOUSPEAK does not claim to have correctly named every divine-property. It claims only that where convergence is observable, the property is theologically robust enough to attempt naming. Many properties remain unnamed; the cathedral is unfinished by design.
