# Longing-deepened-by-fulfillment — the pothos / ishtiyāq / epektasis / ansias convergence

_Five independent traditions (nine attestations counting bridges and borders) converge on one state: a standing longing toward the divine that each beholding deepens instead of stilling — and not one of them gave the state itself a noun, while the desert gave its privation one (akēdia) that survives in Merriam-Webster. The data accumulated in experiment 246's witness-overlay (session 086); the apply-and-break run of Path 3 (session 087) added the Carmelite cross-check and the behavioral witnesses, and prompted this consolidation._

---

## The realm-feature stated

A creature ordered toward an inexhaustible object lives most of its life **between beholdings**, and the between is not empty: it is filled by a structured state — dispositional, flaring occurrent; forward-leaning though memory-fed; defined by orientation (the lean) rather than felt warmth; whose absence-pole is the absence of the *beholding*, never of the object (the object is omnipresent); and whose defining mark is that arrival does not still it — because the object cannot be exhausted, every beholding deepens the lean. The state has a counterfeit (longing for one's own former felt-states), a privation (the lean lost), and an anesthetized register (the lean persisting with all sweetness stripped). The feature relates the subject-pole (beholder) to the anchor-pole (the divine beauty) across the whole non-beholding stretch of a worshipper's life — which is most of it.

## The witness-table

| Tradition | Native term | What it attests | What it cannot say |
|---|---|---|---|
| Hebrew-Psalter (Afro-Asiatic Semitic) | תַּעֲרֹג taʿarog (Ps 42:1), כָּמַהּ kamah (Ps 63:1), נִכְסְפָה nichsefah (Ps 84:2) | GoD-directed longing as bodily, creaturely state (the deer's panting, flesh fainting) — three verbs circling one region | has no NOUN for the state; three verbs is a circumlocution; no deepened-by-meeting clause |
| Greek-Platonic (IE Hellenic) | πόθος pothos vs ἵμερος himeros (Cratylus 420a) | the absence-marking as lexical, on Plato's own philological authority: same desire, present vs absent object; Arrian's pothos — the longing no arrival stilled | object-generic (any absent beloved); does not bind to the divine or to beauty |
| Greek-LXX (bridge, not new witness) | ἐπιποθέω epipotheō (Ps 41:2 LXX) | the contact-event that bound the pothos-root to GoD-longing — the same translation-event that bound doxa to kavod | contact-explainable by definition; counts as the joint, not a new angle |
| Patristic Greek (IE Hellenic, downstream) | ἐπέκτασις epektasis (Gregory of Nyssa, Life of Moses) | no satiation because no exhaustion; the beatific vision as perpetual stretching — the inexhaustibility clause made doctrine | names the trajectory, not the felt engine; an ascesis-shape, not a state-noun |
| Sufi Arabic (Afro-Asiatic Semitic) | شوق shawq / اشتياق ishtiyāq (al-Qushayrī, Risāla) | THE decisive distinction as technical vocabulary: shawq stilled by meeting; ishtiyāq increased by it — object-typology, nine centuries old | Beloved-as-person-directed, not beauty-attribute-directed; no transparency-clause for created anchors |
| Desert-monastic (Greek/Coptic; privation-witness) | ἀκηδία akēdia (Evagrius's logismoi; the noonday demon) | the PRIVATION lexicalized: the lean lost, the monk who wants to be anywhere but here — naming the negative witnesses the positive region | names only the absence of the state; the state itself left nameless |
| Spanish-Carmelite (IE Italic; count cautiously — possible Sufi contact) | ansias (Noche Oscura st. 1); the second sign (Dark Night I.9); gula espiritual (I.6) | the anesthetized register: the lean surviving total loss of felt sweetness ("the memory dwells ordinarily upon God with painful anxiety and carefulness") — and the counterfeit named (consolation-hunger) | chapter-length circumlocutions; no noun; discernment-criteria in place of a lexeme |
| German-literary (IE Germanic, downstream of the Western line) | Sehnsucht (Lewis's "inconsolable longing") | the refusal of every finite object; sweeter than satisfaction; holdable anonymously (Lewis pre-conversion) | object-unspecified; cannot say what the longing is FOR |
| Tuareg (Afro-Asiatic Berber) | ehan-family longing-poetry (tifinagh) | a tradition that reserved its sacred script primarily FOR longing — longing as the motor of sacred expression itself | expressive-act register; no state-noun; not divine-beauty-specific |

Genetic-independence note: the LXX is the explicit contact-joint between the Hebrew and Greek lines (count as one bridge); Gregory and the desert tradition are downstream of that joined line (count with it, though the akēdia-privation is an independent *kind* of witness — a negative one); John of the Cross is possibly contact-linked to Sufi vocabulary (the Asín Palacios question — count cautiously); Sehnsucht is downstream of the Western Christian line. The strongest fully-independent triple: **Hebrew Psalter + Platonic Greek + Sufi Arabic**, with **Tuareg** as a fourth angle from outside the book-traditions entirely and the **desert privation-witness** as a fifth angle of a different logical type. Honest cardinality after discount: **5**.

## Cross-modal witnesses

| Mode | Tradition / practice | Witness-form | What it attests |
|---|---|---|---|
| gait / geography | Camino de Santiago; the Finisterre continuation | pilgrims, arrived at the shrine and finding arrival anticlimactic, keep walking three more days to the Atlantic | the ishtiyāq-structure performed publicly: arrival does not still the lean; the longing is legible in feet, without words |
| travel-rhythm / temporal | umbraphile practice (secular) | the post-totality refrain "So, when's the next one?"; lives arranged around future totalities (Russo's SPACED ending in Desire-to-repeat) | the deepened-not-stilled clause observed outside all worship-vocabulary — anonymous direction through a transparent created orthophanes |
| script | Tuareg tifinagh | a sacred script reserved primarily for longing-poetry | longing as the privileged content of sacred projection itself |
| performative silence | Ps 137, the hung harps | refusal of substitute-beholding ("how shall we sing the LORD's song in a strange land?") | the grief-register: the lean intact enough to refuse proxies; longing witnessed by what is NOT sung |

Modal-note: these are not illustrations of the lexical witnesses but independent projection-angles (POLYMORPHIA): the pilgrim's continuation and the umbraphile's bookings are *behavior* that carries the deepened-not-stilled structure with no lexeme present at all — which is precisely what a region that no tongue lexicalized would leave behind: behavior without a word. The silence-witness of Ps 137 projects the state apophatically, by refusal.

## What no single tradition compact-names

Each register splits the one state: Hebrew gives the creaturely verbs but no noun; Plato gives the absence-marking but no divine direction; Gregory gives the inexhaustibility but as trajectory, not state; al-Qushayrī gives the decisive deepened-not-stilled distinction but person-directed, without the beauty-anchor or the transparency-tolerance for created doors; John of the Cross gives the anesthetized register and the counterfeit but only as discernment-criteria, never a lexeme; German gives the inconsolability without an object; Tuareg gives longing the place of honor without naming the state; the desert names only the privation. Modernity's outlier-move is worse: English flattens the whole region to "longing" (a mood) and lexicalizes the pathology-side (acedia, via the monks) while leaving the positive pole nameless — the asymmetry of a tongue that names the thirst and not the slaking. The YOUSPEAK target is the reverse-projection of all nine shadows: **doxapothos** — the standing-longing toward doxakallos in the non-occurrence of beholding, deepened by each beholding because its object is inexhaustible.

## The convergence-claim

(1) The witnesses had no common source for this naming-pattern: the Psalter's verbs predate contact with the Greek line; Plato's pothos/himeros distinction is internal Greek philology; al-Qushayrī's shawq/ishtiyāq is internal Arabic form-VIII morphology; Tuareg longing-poetry is outside the book-traditions. The convergence is parallel-witness, not borrowing — with the borrowings (LXX; possibly Carmelite-from-Sufi) explicitly discounted in the cardinality.

(2) Under THE-REALM, each term is one projection-angle on one realm-region. Five independent arrivals — including one *negative* arrival (the privation lexicalized) and several *behavioral* arrivals (gait, travel-rhythm, silence) — evidence that the region is structurally real. Cardinality 5: good disclosure-density, not exceptional; the report is honest.

(3) Of POLYPHONIA's five convergence-sources: **realm-unity** (the inexhaustibility of the object is doctrine in three lines independently — Gregory's epektasis, Qushayrī's ishtiyāq, the wheel's own arithmetic) and **shared embodiment** (the deer's panting, the fainting flesh, the walking feet — the lean is creaturely before it is doctrinal) are operating; **contact** is operating at the marked joints (LXX; possibly Carmelite) and is discounted; formal-parallel cannot explain the behavioral witnesses; theophanic invitation is claimed by the traditions themselves (the deer-verse is a liturgy, not a lexicon entry) and the file records the claim without weighing it.

Where traditions converge, the self-disclosure has multiple-witness. This file is a witness-deposition: five angles, one of them the shape of the hole the state leaves when it goes missing, agree that the between-time of the worshipper's life is a structured, real, unnamed state. The deposition stands whether or not the candidate-word ever clears the canon line.

## Connected canon-words

- **doxalgia** (canon, legacy 7.55 / v2 6.0, specialized) — the presence-pole sibling: ache *at* beholding. Holds the himeros-side of Cratylus 420a's symmetry; does not crowd the absence-pole, which its own canon entry marks as un-owned ("saudade — ache of absence; doxalgia is ache of presence").
- **doxakallos** (canon, 8.25 / 6.1, specialized) — the anchor; the longed-for itself. The convergence-region is the standing *relation toward* it in non-beholding.
- **orthophanes** (canon, 7.55 / 5.75, specialized) — the door. Load-bearing for the region's boundary: the transparency-condition (anchor leaned-through, not collected) is phrased in this word's canon language ("Orthophanes is the door; doxakallos is the room").
- **anagnoristasis, athaumasma, metastrophesis** (canon) — threshold, trace, turning-kept: the wheel-positions the longing leans toward and is fueled by. The trace (athaumasma) is the memory-fuel clause; no crowding — none of the three names the between-state.
- Pipeline-adjacent: **epektasisme** (trajectory-slot; reserved — the inexhaustible onward-stretch as its own word), **ehanme** (expressive-act slot), **acedime** (the privation; the question whether YOUSPEAK names pathologies, raised in the Coptic excavation, stands).
