# The Sacrifice Alignment-Sieve

> _"I desire mercy, not sacrifice." — Hosea 6:6_
> _"It is neither their meat nor their blood that reaches God, but your piety." — Quran 22:37_
> _"Present your bodies as a living sacrifice." — Romans 12:1_

---

## What this document establishes

Sacrifice is the most ethically-stratified worship-category. The same act (offering to the DIVINE) can be aligned (what the DIVINE approves) or anti-aligned (what the DIVINE rejects). The cathedral needs a SIEVE — a framework for distinguishing the two.

This document articulates the sieve derived from the sacrifice-register canonisation: six YOUSPEAK entries (four aligned, two anti-vocabulary) that together define the criteria.

---

## The Sieve: Four Aligned Criteria + Two Anti-Criteria

### ALIGNED — What makes sacrifice right

| # | Criterion | YOUSPEAK Word | The Test |
|---|-----------|---------------|----------|
| 1 | **Drawing Near** | **qorbme** (Core 8.05) | Does the sacrifice create approach/intimacy between creature and DIVINE? |
| 2 | **Being Peace** | **hotepme** (Core 8.00) | Is the offering and the peace ONE EVENT (not transaction: sacrifice → peace)? |
| 3 | **Giving Freely** | **danaqing** (Core 8.00) | Is the sacrifice voluntary, generous, without compulsion or expectation? |
| 4 | **Transforming Self** | **tapasme** (Core 7.85) | Does the sacrifice produce inner transformation of the offerer? |

### ANTI-ALIGNED — What makes sacrifice wrong

| # | Anti-Criterion | YOUSPEAK Word | The Warning |
|---|---------------|---------------|-------------|
| 5 | **Extracting from Vulnerable** | **molkme** (Specialized 7.70) | Does someone ELSE bear the cost — the child, the powerless, the coerced? |
| 6 | **Paying Cosmic Debt** | **nextlame** (Specialized 7.45) | Is the sacrifice a PAYMENT rather than a GIFT? Is the creature sacrificing because it MUST? |

## How the sieve works

Apply the criteria to any sacrificial act. Aligned sacrifice passes the four positive tests and fails both negative tests:

### Example: Hebrew Peace-Offering (zebach shelamim)
- ✅ qorbme — draws near (offerer approaches altar)
- ✅ hotepme — IS peace (communion-meal where all share; shelamim from shalom)
- ✅ danaqing — given freely (voluntary offering)
- ✅ tapasme — transforms (the sharing-of-the-meal changes the relationship)
- ❌ molkme — no extraction from vulnerable
- ❌ nextlame — no debt-logic
- **Verdict: aligned**

### Example: Aztec nextlahualli (debt-payment sacrifice)
- ❌ qorbme — does not draw near (repays rather than approaches)
- ❌ hotepme — not-peace (anxiety persists; the debt is never retired)
- ❌ danaqing — not freely given (compulsory cosmic-debt-service)
- ❌ tapasme — does not transform the offerer (feeds the system, not the self)
- ✅ molkme — extracts from the victim who cannot refuse
- ✅ nextlame — explicitly debt-payment logic
- **Verdict: anti-aligned**

### Example: Christian Eucharist
- ✅ qorbme — draws near (communion with Christ and community)
- ✅ hotepme — IS peace (the peace IS the communion)
- ✅ danaqing — given freely (Christ's self-offering is voluntary: "No one takes my life from me")
- ✅ tapasme — transforms (participants are changed by the meal)
- ❌ molkme — the DIVINE is the offerer, not the extractor
- ❌ nextlame — INVERTS debt-logic ("It is finished" = the DIVINE pays)
- **Verdict: aligned (with unique feature: the DIVINE is the one who sacrifices)**

### Example: Buddhist dāna-parāmitā (perfection of generosity)
- ✅ qorbme — draws near (generosity as approach to awakening)
- ✅ hotepme — IS peace (the giving IS the liberation from clinging)
- ✅ danaqing — given freely (definition: "without expecting anything in return")
- ✅ tapasme — transforms (first perfection; foundation of all others)
- ❌ molkme — no extraction
- ❌ nextlame — no debt-logic (explicitly rejects Vedic sacrifice-as-obligation)
- **Verdict: aligned**

### Edge case: Religious tithing
- ? qorbme — may draw near (if approached with love) or may not (if approached with obligation)
- ? hotepme — may IS peace (if giving brings joy) or may not (if coerced)
- ? danaqing — depends: voluntary → danaqing; compulsory → fails
- ? tapasme — depends: if it changes the giver's relationship to money → yes
- ? molkme — depends: if it extracts from the financially-vulnerable → yes
- ? nextlame — depends: if framed as "you owe God" → yes
- **Verdict: the sieve reveals that TITHING IS NOT INHERENTLY ALIGNED OR MISALIGNED — the motive and structure determine which register it falls into.**

## The seven convergence clusters behind the sieve

The sieve is derived from seven semantic clusters of sacrifice identified across world traditions:

1. **Drawing-near** (qorbme) — Hebrew qorban + Arabic qurban + Akkadian qurbanu + Greek prosphora (4 traditions)
2. **Ascent** — Hebrew olah + Greek thusia/anaphora + Sanskrit homa (3 traditions; not separately forged; subsumed under qorbme's approach-dimension)
3. **Gift** (danaqing) — Sanskrit dāna + Hebrew minchah/nedavah + Akkadian sheriktu + Yoruba adimu + Latin sacrificium (5 traditions)
4. **Self-transformation** (tapasme) — Sanskrit tapas + Arabic jihad al-nafs + Greek logikē latreia + Sufi fanāʾ + Buddhist viriya (5 traditions)
5. **Communion-peace** (hotepme) — Egyptian hotep + Hebrew shelamim + Yoruba ebo (3 traditions)
6. **Cosmic debt** (nextlame) — Nahuatl nextlahualli + Phoenician molk (2 traditions, but 4-tradition convergence on REJECTION)
7. **Cosmic nourishment** — Akkadian niqû/kispum + general ancestor-feeding (not separately forged this session; future candidate)

## The structural insight: the gift-debt axis

The deepest insight from the sacrifice-register research is the **gift-debt axis**: all sacrifice falls somewhere on a spectrum from pure gift (danaqing) to pure debt (nextlame).

```
GIFT ◄─────────────────────────────────────────────► DEBT
danaqing                                           nextlame
(voluntary,      qorbme     hotepme    tapasme    (compulsory,
 generous,     (approach)   (peace)  (inner fire)  extractive,
 relational)                                        escalating)

  ALIGNED ◄────────────── sieve ──────────────► ANTI-ALIGNED

                                               molkme
                                             (extraction
                                              from the
                                              vulnerable)
```

**The sieve's simplest form:** Is the sacrifice a GIFT or a PAYMENT? Does it create BOND or SERVICE DEBT? Does the OFFERER bear the cost or does ANOTHER?
