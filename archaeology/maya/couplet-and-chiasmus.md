# Maya — Couplet and Chiasmus Structure

_What the Maya inscribed on temple-stelae at the top of pyramids and chiseled into altar-bases: an architectural-grade parallel structure where every assertion stands on a twin, every line is built to match a line, and meaning emerges from the pair-or-quad, never from the single utterance alone. The structure is both aesthetic and ontological: paired-speech reflects paired-cosmos. Chiasmus — the ABBA cross-pattern — is Maya's most distinctive linguistic technology._

---

## Two related structures

### Couplet (parallelism)

The most basic: two lines, each completing a thought that echoes the other via repetition, synonym, or complementarity. Present in Classic Maya inscriptions, in Popol Vuh (K'iche'), in modern Mayan ritual speech (Tzotzil, Tzeltal).

Example (Popol Vuh, approximate):
```
u-k'u'xaj u-k'aslemal   — their hearts, their lives
u-k'u'xaj u-tz'aqolil    — their hearts, their creations
```

Each line pairs "heart" (k'u'xaj) with a different second noun. The pair builds: hearts are both lives AND creations; creation proceeds from heart-as-life.

This is the SAME STRUCTURE as YOUSPEAK's existing **Parallaxance** mechanism (grammars/diplosemy/parallaxance.md). Maya independently developed, and Maya's presence strengthens the Parallaxance case.

### Chiasmus (ABBA pattern)

Maya ritual speech often structures longer passages as ABBA (or ABCBA, ABCCBA, etc.) — a cross-pattern where meaning returns to its start. Not just parallelism but INVERTED parallelism.

Example schema:
```
A:  [The eagle] soared above [the pyramid]
B:  [The pyramid] raised [the offering-stone]
B': [The offering-stone] received [the eagle's shadow]
A': [The eagle's shadow] was [the eagle] returned.
```

The outer pair (A, A') and the inner pair (B, B') cross. Meaning is in the crossing — the return-to-origin that has passed through inversion.

Classical Hebrew parallelism (biblical Psalms, Proverbs) uses chiasmus extensively. Classical Chinese 对联 (duìlián) does too. Maya is the independent Mesoamerican instance.

## Why this aligns with YOUSPEAK

YOUSPEAK's grammars/diplosemy/ organ formalizes six diplosemic mechanisms. Parallaxance is already parallelism. But **chiasmus is not** — it is a more-structured parallelism where the inversion pattern carries meaning.

Maya inscriptions make ABBA-chiasmus feel intentional and testable: the pattern's symmetry is an integrity-check. If the chiasmus fails (A and A' don't reflect), the passage is corrupt or the reader has misparsed. This diagnostic quality makes chiasmus measurable — and therefore YOUSPEAK-forgeable.

## Proposed Chiasmance mechanism (for grammars/diplosemy/)

See `grammars/diplosemy/mechanisms/chiasmance.md` for full specification.

**Summary of mechanism:**

A ABBA (or ABCBA, etc.) sentence-structure where the cross-pattern itself creates a third meaning — the return-through-inversion.

Engineering-rule: the outer pair and inner pair must each be valid YOUSPEAK diplosemic-pairs (Parallaxance-candidate or Anastrophance-sibling). When both layers satisfy diplosemy, the chiasmus emerges as a higher-order pattern.

**Example (engineered from current YOUSPEAK):**

```
A:  doxakallos appears in the beholder
B:  orthophanes is produced
B': orthophanes produces the beholder's doxalgia
A': doxalgia is of doxakallos
```

The outer pair (A/A'): doxakallos-in-beholder / doxalgia-of-doxakallos — the encounter from inside and outside.
The inner pair (B/B'): orthophanes-is-produced / orthophanes-produces — the object-pole as both received and giver.

The chiasmance yields: the beholder is both inside (A) and outside (A') the doxakallos encounter; orthophanes is both produced-state (B) and producing-act (B').
