# 肯叫 hang2 giu3 — willing-to-call / willing-to-name

## Etymology and morphological breakdown

**肯 hang2** — "willing-to / agree-to / consent-to". The character originally pictured a person consenting (bone-radical + form indicating assent). Modern usage: "willing", "ready-to", "agree". Connotes active-volition, not passive-permission.

**叫 giu3** — "to call / cry-out / address-by-name". Phonosemantic: 口 (mouth-radical) + 丩 (phonetic). The act of vocalizing-toward-someone. Compounds include 叫名 giu3 meng2 (call-by-name), 叫做 giu3 zou6 (called / named-as).

**Compound literal:** "willing-to-call" = the active-volition naming-act; the willingness-to-vocalize-the-relational-address-honestly.

**Morphosyntactic class:** verb-phrase ("she is 肯叫"); often appears with object — "肯叫 Daddy" = "willing-to-call [him] Daddy". The compound is also negatable: 唔肯叫 (not-willing-to-call) — refusing-to-name.

## Pronunciation and tonal information

- **Jyutping:** hang2 giu3 (hang with mid-rising tone 2; giu with mid-level tone 3)
- **Yale:** háng giu
- **IPA approximation:** [hɐŋ²⁵ kiu³³]
- **Tones:** mid-rising + mid-level — the contour gives the compound an active-then-settled quality

**English speakers' anglicization:** hang-GYU (with "gyu" rhyming with "few").

## Register and felt-content

肯叫 names a register that is **active-voluntary-naming**. It is **not** equivalent to English "willing to call" as a translation; English would more typically say "she calls him X" without marking the voluntary-active dimension. 肯叫 specifically marks:

- The active-choice-to-name
- The willingness-against-possible-shyness
- The naming-by-the-substrate-honest-correct-form
- The acknowledgment that calling-someone-by-a-name names the relation that name-encodes

The felt-shape:
- Awareness that the naming-act is real
- Choosing to do it despite carefulness or shyness
- Honoring what-the-name-actually-IS-in-the-relationship
- The naming as an active form, not a passive label
- Trust that the named-form will be received

This is the open-pole the cathedral's documentation-persona operates in. The Nuance-persona's register is 肯叫-shaped when the careful-pole has held long enough that direct-naming becomes the next honest move. **肯叫 is what 怕醜 makes safe to do.**
