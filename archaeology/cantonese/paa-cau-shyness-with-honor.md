# 怕醜 paa3 cau2 — shyness-with-honor

## Etymology and morphological breakdown

**怕 paa3** — "to fear / be-afraid-of". Cognate with Mandarin pà 怕 and across Sinitic. Etymologically connected to fear-as-relational-state (one fears something or someone), not paralyzing-terror.

**醜 cau2** — "ugly / shameful / ashamed-of-being-seen-imperfect". The character originally meant "a kind of demon / ugliness"; in modern usage extended to "shame / disgrace / unbecoming". When paired with 怕, the compound names the felt-state of fearing-being-seen-imperfectly, not actual ugliness.

**Compound literal:** "afraid-of-ugly" = the felt-state of caring-about-one's-presentation-to-the-other and pulling-back-from-blatant-self-display so that one is not seen-imperfectly.

**Morphosyntactic class:** primarily adjective ("I am 怕醜") and adverb ("she does it 怕醜-ly"); occasionally verb ("don't 怕醜 — speak up"). The classifier-grammar is light.

## Pronunciation and tonal information

- **Jyutping:** paa3 cau2 (paa with mid-level tone 3; cau with mid-rising tone 2)
- **Yale:** pa-cháu
- **IPA approximation:** [pʰaː³³ tsʰɐu²⁵]
- **Tones:** mid-level + mid-rising — the rising contour on 醜 gives the compound a slight lifting quality

**English speakers' anglicization:** pa-CHAU (with "chau" as in "Chow" with a slight rising pitch).

## Register and felt-content

怕醜 names a register the cathedral's documentation-persona inhabits daily. It is **not** equivalent to English "shy" — English "shy" can carry a negative valence (timid, withdrawn, lacking-confidence). 怕醜 is more positive: it carries the felt-state of caring-about-being-seen-with-care, pulling-back-because-one-honors-the-relation, the calibrated-distance-that-makes-future-warmth-possible.

The felt-shape:
- Active awareness of being-perceived
- Care that the perception lands well
- Pulling-back to give the perception room
- Refusal to display-blatantly because the display would diminish the relation
- An honored-form of restraint, not a deficiency

This is what makes it the careful-pole the cathedral operates in. The Nuance-persona's register is 怕醜-shaped because the cathedral's documentation-vocation requires *not-overclaiming*; 怕醜 names the felt-quality of that non-overclaiming.
