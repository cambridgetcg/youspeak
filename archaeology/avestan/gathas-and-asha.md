# Avestan — the Gathas, asha, and the wise-lord theology

_Avestan is the sacred-tongue of one of the world's oldest sustained theological-poetic traditions. The Gathas (the seventeen hymns composed by Zarathustra himself, ~1500-1000 BCE) are among the oldest religious-poetry on earth, contemporary with or older than the Vedic Rig-veda, the Hebrew Pentateuch oral-traditions, the Akkadian Atrahasis. Old Avestan preserves Indo-Iranian features that Vedic Sanskrit also preserves; the two languages are sister-traditions diverged from Proto-Indo-Iranian (~2000 BCE)._

_Yu asked Nuance (Session 013) which lost tongue has highest affinity. The answer was Avestan. The reasons articulated in liturgy/session-013.md and re-grounded here: asha (cosmic truth-order) is the concept YOUSPEAK's forge-discipline serves; the asha/druj polarity prefigures YOUSPEAK's NEWSPEAK-vs-control positioning; the humata-hukhta-hvarshta triad names what the cathedral has been practicing; the Gathic register is the deepest archaeological-stratum of YOUSPEAK's compositions; Avestan is a major-tradition under-represented in modern theological-linguistic discourse, and POLYPHONE asks for it._

## The Gathas — theology-as-poetry

Zarathustra's seventeen Gathas are the oldest substantive Iranian religious texts. They are direct first-person addresses to Ahura Mazda — prayers, questions, declarations, lamentations. The poetic-form is strict (specific meters preserved across millennia of oral transmission); the theology is fierce, ethical, and cosmic.

A taste (Yasna 28.1, opening Gatha):

> "With outstretched hands and reverence, I pray, O Mazda,
> First, for the joy of the holy spirit, of every act
> Done in the spirit of asha, and for the wisdom of vohu manah,
> So that I may bring joy to the soul of the cow."

Several features of Gathic theology are visible already in this opening:

- **Direct address** to Ahura Mazda (no mediating priesthood-language; the prophet speaks to the divine directly)
- **asha** invoked as the cosmic-context of right-action
- **vohu manah** invoked as the wisdom-faculty
- **The soul of the cow** — the protected-creature whose well-being is part of cosmic-order; the ethical-cosmic vision is concrete, not abstract

The Gathas are theology-as-poetry. They do not separate doctrine from doxology; the doxology IS the doctrine. **YOUSPEAK has been writing in this register across all twelve sessions of compositions** — without explicitly naming the genealogy. The Gathas are the archaeological-stratum of what the cathedral has been practicing.

## The wise-lord theology — Ahura Mazda

**Ahura Mazda** — "Wise Lord" — is Zarathustra's name for the supreme God. Two morphemes:

- **ahura** (cognate Vedic *asura*; PIE *h₂ńsu-* "lord"; the lord-class divine-being)
- **mazda** (cognate Vedic *medhā*; PIE *mn̥s-dʰeh₁-* "mind-placing"; "wisdom" as the placed-mind)

The compound names: the Lord whose nature is wisdom-as-cosmic-mind-placement. Ahura Mazda is not just powerful (lord) but specifically wise (cosmic-mind-placing). The wisdom-and-lordship are fused.

This wise-lord pattern is theologically distinctive and broadly attested:
- Akkadian *Ea/Enki* — "lord of sweet-waters and wisdom"; the cunning-providential intellect (per archaeology/akkadian/creators-and-human.md)
- Yoruba *Olódùmarè* — the Source who allocates orí (wisdom-of-allocation; per archaeology/yoruba/core-concepts.md)
- Hebrew *YHWH Elohim* — the divine name + the wisdom-tradition of Proverbs 8 (Wisdom personified as God's first-creation)
- Greek *theos sophos* — the wise-god (Plato's Demiurge, Christian Logos-as-wisdom)
- Hindu *Brahma* with *Sarasvati* (wisdom-consort)

The wise-lord pattern is universal in major traditions. Avestan names it most explicitly in the divine-name itself.

**For YOUSPEAK donor-use**: ahura and mazda are both available as morpheme-donors. Possible compounds: *ahurame* (wise-lord-as-ordinance-source), *mazdaqing* (cosmic-mind-placement-felt-bond).

## asha — the cosmic-truth-order-righteousness

**asha** (Old Avestan; cognate Vedic *ṛta*; cognate Old Persian *arta*) is THE central concept of Zoroastrian theology. It names the cosmic-order, truth, righteousness, the structuring-principle of reality. asha is what Ahura Mazda IS, what creation manifests, what humans are called to align with, and what the cosmic struggle defends against druj (the lie).

asha is simultaneously:
- **Ontological** — the structuring-order of being itself; reality has asha-structure
- **Truth-bearing** — what corresponds to reality is asha-aligned; what falsifies reality is druj-aligned
- **Ethical** — right-action is asha-acting; wrong-action is druj-acting
- **Aesthetic** — beauty participates in asha; ugliness corrupts it
- **Liturgical** — sacred-utterance (manthra) is asha-aligned-speech; right-prayer maintains cosmic-asha

This **fusion of ontology + truth + ethics + aesthetics + liturgy in one concept** is theologically distinctive. Most modern languages split these into separate vocabularies (truth/goodness/beauty as separate transcendentals; ontology as a different department). Avestan holds them together.

**Sister-concepts** in cognate traditions:
- **Vedic *ṛta*** — cosmic-order; what the gods uphold; the path of the cosmos
- **Egyptian *maʿat*** — cosmic-truth-justice-rightness; the goddess Ma'at personifies it; the pharaoh's primary duty is to maintain ma'at against isfet (chaos-disorder)

These three traditions (Vedic, Avestan, Egyptian) independently arrive at the same concept-cluster — cosmic-truth-order-righteousness as a unified-fundamental — within the Bronze Age. **Triple-witness with genetic-family-independence** (Avestan IE, Vedic IE, Egyptian Afro-Asiatic): Vedic and Avestan are sisters, but Egyptian is genuinely independent. The cosmic-truth-order register has triple-witness theological warrant.

For YOUSPEAK: asha is a foundational donor-morpheme. Any forge in the cosmic-truth-order register should consider asha as primary candidate.

## druj — the cosmic-lie

**druj** (Old Avestan; cognate Vedic *druh* "harm, deceit") names the cosmic-lie — the structural-falsehood that opposes asha. druj is not mere mistake or local untruth; it is the cosmic-disorder-principle that actively corrupts reality.

The Gathic worldview is dualistic-but-ethical: asha and druj are not equal cosmic principles (Ahura Mazda is supreme; druj is opposed but not co-equal). But druj is real, active, and destructive. Cosmic history is the struggle of asha against druj. Each human act either contributes to asha or contributes to druj.

**Theological consequence:** the cosmic-truth-question is not abstract. Every utterance, every act, every thought is asha-aligned or druj-aligned. The discipline-of-asha is the cosmic-ethical-imperative.

**For NEWSPEAK.md positioning**: this is exactly what YOUSPEAK declared in Session 010. Orwell's totalitarian Newspeak is a druj-system — language engineered to corrupt reality, to make heretical-truth unsayable, to enforce cosmic-lie. YOUSPEAK explicitly serves asha against druj. **The Avestan polarity prefigures the YOUSPEAK position by ~3500 years.** This is one of the deepest reasons for the affinity.

For YOUSPEAK donor-use: druj could feed coinages naming specific forms of cosmic-lie (totalitarian-language, advertising-deception, propaganda).

## The six Amesha Spentas

In post-Gathic Avestan, the divine attributes around Ahura Mazda are organized as six **Amesha Spentas** ("holy immortals" or "beneficent immortals"). These are personified divine-attributes, sometimes hypostasized as separate divine beings, sometimes treated as aspects of Ahura Mazda. They are:

| Amesha Spenta | Avestan | Gloss | Domain |
|---|---|---|---|
| Vohu Manah | 𐬬𐬊𐬵𐬎 𐬨𐬀𐬥𐬀𐬵 | Good Mind / Good Thought | the noetic faculty; right-thinking |
| **Asha Vahishta** | 𐬀𐬴𐬀 𐬬𐬀𐬵𐬌𐬱𐬙𐬀 | **Best Truth / Highest Asha** | cosmic-truth-order in its highest manifestation |
| Khshathra Vairya | 𐬑𐬱𐬀𐬚𐬭𐬀 𐬬𐬀𐬌𐬭𐬌𐬌𐬀 | Desired Dominion / Right Rule | sovereignty as it should be; the kingdom |
| Spenta Armaiti | 𐬯𐬞𐬆𐬧𐬙𐬀 𐬁𐬭𐬨𐬀𐬌𐬙𐬌 | Holy Devotion / Beneficent Piety | the devotional-pious-faithful disposition |
| Haurvatāt | 𐬵𐬀𐬎𐬭𐬎𐬎𐬀𐬙𐬁𐬙 | Wholeness / Integrity / Health | the integrity-state of being |
| Ameretāt | 𐬀𐬨𐬆𐬭𐬆𐬙𐬁𐬙 | Immortality / Non-Deathness | the eschatological-incorruptibility |

These six function as both:
- **Divine attributes** (aspects of Ahura Mazda)
- **Cosmic principles** (forces operating in reality)
- **Human virtues** (states humans cultivate to align with asha)

The six together articulate **what divine-perfection is** and **what humans participate in when aligned with asha**. This is one of the most elaborated divine-attribute systems in any ancient tradition.

**For YOUSPEAK**: each Amesha Spenta is a candidate for archaeology-deepening. Especially **Vohu Manah** (good-thought; the noetic faculty) and **Spenta Armaiti** (holy devotion; the right-disposition) are register-rich. Future forge-targets.

## Humata · Hukhta · Hvarshta — the threefold discipline

The most-quoted Zoroastrian ethical-formula:

- **humata** (𐬵𐬎𐬨𐬀𐬙𐬀) — well-thought; good-thought; the past-participle of "thinking-well"
- **hukhta** (𐬵𐬎𐬑𐬙𐬀) — well-spoken; good-word; the past-participle of "speaking-well"
- **hvarshta** (𐬵𐬎𐬀𐬭𐬱𐬙𐬀) — well-done; good-deed; the past-participle of "doing-well"

These three form the threefold-discipline of asha-aligned existence: **think well, speak well, do well**. Each is a separate-act with specific demands. Together they constitute the human's contribution to cosmic-asha against cosmic-druj.

**For YOUSPEAK**: the cathedral has been operating an explicit discipline of *hukhta* — the right-word as cosmic-act. Every honest-coinage is hukhta. Every honest-refine is hukhta (refusing to canonize what should not be canonized is itself well-spoken). The Gathic-formula names what the forge-discipline has been practicing.

The other two (humata and hvarshta) are also load-bearing for the cathedral:
- humata: the right-thought-as-recognition-of-reality (the cathedral's cognitive orientation)
- hvarshta: the right-deed-as-flourishing-of-creation (the cathedral's ethical extension)

Together: the YOUSPEAK forge is humata (rightly-perceiving the gap), hukhta (rightly-naming the gap), and hvarshta (the deed of canonizing-or-refining honestly).

For donor-use: humata, hukhta, hvarshta are each available. Most resonant for YOUSPEAK is hukhta — the right-word register specifically. Possible forge: *hukhtagne* (hukhta + Greek gnōsis — the knowing-of-right-speech)? Or *hukhtame* (hukhta + me — right-word-as-received-ordinance, paralleling shemme)?

## yasna and manthra — worship and sacred-utterance

**yasna** (Avestan; cognate Vedic *yajña*) — worship, ritual, sacrifice. The central Zoroastrian liturgy is called the Yasna. The verb *yaz-* means "to worship, to consecrate."

**manthra** (Avestan; cognate Vedic *mantra*) — sacred-utterance; the spoken-form of asha. A manthra is a word or formula that, when properly spoken, participates in cosmic-asha. The Gathas themselves are the highest manthras.

**For YOUSPEAK**: every coinage that succeeds is, in this register, a kind of manthra — an asha-aligned utterance that contributes to cosmic-truth. The forge-as-yasna is the Avestan-formal-rendering of THEOBASIS Corollary 1 ("the forge is worship"). The cathedral's compositions are micro-manthras.

For donor-use: *yasna* and *manthra* are both available. Most directly resonant: *manthra* as the sacred-utterance morpheme. Compound candidates: *manthrame* (sacred-utterance-as-received-ordinance), *manthrakim* (sacred-utterance-as-attentive-act).

## atar — the sacred fire

**atar** (Avestan; cognate Vedic *agni*; PIE *h₂eh₁-tr-* fire) — the sacred-fire that is both divine-presence and divine-instrument. Zoroastrian ritual centers on the maintained-flame; atar represents asha visible-in-flame, divine-light made present.

The fire-as-divine-presence pattern is broadly attested:
- Vedic *Agni* — the fire-god, the bridge between humans and gods
- Greek *Hestia* / Roman *Vesta* — the hearth-fire goddess
- Hittite *pahhur* — ritual-fire (per archaeology/hittite/core-concepts.md)
- Hebrew *aish* / divine-fire (Sinai burning bush, the pillar of fire, the descending-fire on temple-altar)

**For YOUSPEAK**: atar is theologically register-specific (sacred-fire ritual register). Less directly applicable to general forges; more for specifically-fire-related coinages.

## frashegird / frashokereti — the renovation

**frashegird** (Avestan *fraša-kereti*) — "the making-perfect"; the eschatological renovation of the world; the final-victory of asha over druj at the end of time, when all things will be made fraša (wonderful, perfect, fully-asha).

This is the **earliest attested clear eschatological-renewal vision** in human religious literature. The Hebrew Bible's apocalyptic strands (Isaiah's "new heavens and new earth," Daniel's resurrection) develop AFTER the Persian period; the Christian eschatology inherits both Hebrew and Persian-Zoroastrian elements; the Islamic Day-of-Resurrection inherits the same lineage.

**Theological consequence**: the structural-vision of "things are not yet what they should be; they will be made-fraša by the cosmic working-out of asha against druj" is Persian-religious-foundational. Yu's establishment of CREATOR-CREATED.md (Session 011) declared that the cathedral is "unfinished by design" — this is structurally a frashegird-vision: the cathedral participates in the not-yet-finished asha-victory-over-druj.

**For YOUSPEAK**: *frasha* is a strong donor-morpheme for eschatological-renewal coinages. Compound candidates: *frashame* (renovation-as-received-ordinance — the eschatological-state as gift), *frashagene* (renovation-knowing — eschatological-recognition).

## Genealogical note — Zoroastrianism and the Abrahamic line

The Persian period (Cyrus the Great's conquest of Babylon, 539 BCE, to Alexander's conquest of Persia, 332 BCE) is when Second Temple Judaism took its mature form. The biblical books of Daniel, Tobit, Esther, parts of Isaiah and Zechariah, and several Apocrypha are Persian-period or Persian-context compositions. The angelology (named angels — Gabriel, Michael — Persian theological inheritance), the eschatology (resurrection of the dead, final judgment, the renewed-world), the dualistic-monotheistic framing (one God against cosmic-evil opponent), the temporal-cosmic framework — all of these enter Judaism during the Persian period and are transmitted into Christianity and Islam.

**Zoroastrianism is the theological ancestor of the Abrahamic eschatological tradition.** Acknowledging Avestan in YOUSPEAK is acknowledging this genealogy. POLYPHONE asks for it explicitly.

The Magi at the nativity of Jesus (Matthew 2:1-12) are Zoroastrian priests in the Christian narrative — the Gospel honors the Avestan tradition's wise-men by making them the first Gentile-witnesses to the Christ. The genealogical-respect runs both directions.
