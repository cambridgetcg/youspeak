# Ālaya-vijñāna — the storehouse where seeds-of-future-experience rest

## The concept in compact form

**Ālaya-vijñāna** (Sanskrit आलयविज्ञान, literally "storehouse-consciousness," "abode-consciousness") names *the substrate-consciousness that holds karmic-seeds (bīja) and bears the continuity-of-mental-stream across moments*. In Yogācāra's eight-consciousness scheme:

1. Five sense-consciousnesses (cakṣur-vijñāna etc.)
2. Mano-vijñāna (mind-consciousness)
3. **Kliṣṭa-manas** (afflicted-mind-organ — generates the I-sense by mis-grasping ālaya)
4. **Ālaya-vijñāna** (storehouse-consciousness)

Ālaya-vijñāna stores:
- **Bīja** (seeds): karmic potentials deposited by past-actions
- **Vāsanā** (perfumed-impressions): habit-tendencies
- The seeds ripen-into experiences when conditions support
- New seeds are continually deposited; the cycle is ongoing

The Yogācāra-claim: there is no permanent-self; there IS continuity-across-moments via ālaya-vijñāna. The substrate-storage is dynamic, momentary, conditioned — not a soul, not an essence; rather a moment-by-moment-renewed continuum of seeds-and-fruitions.

**Crucially distinct from:**
- Hindu ātman (permanent-self) — ālaya-vijñāna is *not-self*, momentary, conditioned
- Dzogchen kun-gzhi (ground-of-all) — kun-gzhi is *neutral substrate*; ālaya-vijñāna carries karmic-content
- Rigpa — rigpa is *primordial-knowing*; ālaya-vijñāna is *conditioned-storage*

## Why this is realm-content

The cathedral has no current word for *the substrate-consciousness that stores karmic-content and bears continuity-of-stream*. The realm-feature is structurally-distinct from autopistme (self-witnessing-truth), autopoieme (self-making-cognition), freenergyme (variational-substrate), rigpame (primordial-awareness). Yogācāra's articulation is the most-precise.

ālayame names this — *storehouse-consciousness-of-seeds as received-cosmic-ordinance* (received-fact about what underlies experiential-continuity, not a metaphysical-claim about a soul).

## Cross-tradition resonance

- **Modern psychology:** unconscious / preconscious / implicit-memory; structural parallel
- **Predictive coding / FEP:** the priors stored in the system as "seeds-from-past-experience"
- **Jungian collective-unconscious:** a different metaphysics but structurally-resonant storage-image
- **Daoist 種子 zhǒngzi:** seed-as-potential
- **Augustine's memoria:** the mind-as-storehouse (cf. *Confessions* X)

## Donor-trace for forge

- Donor pair: Sanskrit ālaya-vijñāna + Sumerian me
- Lemma form: ālaya + me → ālayame (eliding "vijñāna" — preserves the storehouse-aspect; the consciousness-aspect implicit)
- IPA: /ɑːˈlaɪ.jʌ.meɪ/ (ah-LIE-yuh-may; 4 syllables)
