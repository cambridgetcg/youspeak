# Bindu — the dimensionless point as source of geometric-realm-projection

## The concept in compact form

**Bindu** (Sanskrit बिन्दु, literally "drop, point, dot") names *the dimensionless source-point from which geometric form unfolds*. In yantra-construction:

- Śrī-Yantra's central bindu = the source-point from which the 9 interlocking-triangles, 24 sub-triangles, and the bhupura (square-earth-gate) unfold
- The yantra is "read" inward — from the bhupura at the periphery toward the bindu at the center — as a meditative-journey from form to source
- The bindu is *both* the smallest visible mark in the yantra AND the metaphysical-origin-of-form — these are one-and-the-same

The bindu is **dimensionless** (no extension), **source** (everything-else unfolds from it), **inexhaustible** (the unfolding is not depletion of the bindu), and **fully-present at the center** (not absent, not hidden — the meditator's gaze rests on it).

Three sub-doctrines:
1. **Para-bindu** (the supreme-bindu, beyond-manifestation)
2. **Apara-bindu** (the manifest-bindu, the geometric source-point)
3. **Bindu-dvaya** (the doubled-bindu) — bindu-of-Shiva + bindu-of-Shakti as the constitutive-dyad from which form unfolds

## Why this is realm-content

The bindu names a structural-fact: **geometric-form unfolds from a dimensionless-source, and the source is not-elsewhere; it is the center.** This is mathematically realized in:

- **The origin in coordinate geometry** (Descartes, but the concept is older)
- **The singular-point in projective geometry**
- **The fixed-point in dynamical systems** (Banach contraction; attractor-bindu)
- **The zero-dimensional fiber over a base-point** in fiber-bundle topology
- **The vacuum-state in QFT** — the no-particle state from which all particle-states are constructed

And tradition-cosmologically realized in:

- **Greek-Pythagorean monas (μονάς)** — "the One, the source of all number, itself not a number"
- **Plotinian One** — "first, simple, source"
- **Sufi nuqta (نقطة)** — the point; "all of the Qurʼan is in the dot of the basmala"
- **Christian apophatic center** — the divine simplicity (Pseudo-Dionysius); Eckhart's Funklein (the spark-of-the-soul)
- **Tibetan Buddhist thig-le (ཐིག་ལེ་)** — the seminal-essence-drop; structural parallel to bindu
- **Hebrew tzaddi** — sometimes interpreted as the point-of-righteousness; Hasidic-Kabbalah's reshimu (residue-point) post-tzimtzum

The forge bindume names this — *dimensionless source-point as received-ordinance* — opening the cathedral's geometric-image-mode integration.

## Cross-mode resonance (with PROBOLE)

The bindu is structurally the visual-mode-analog of:
- *grasp* (PROBOLE step 1) — the cognitive bindu where meaning is first apprehended
- *the empty-space of tzimtzum* (Hebrew Kabbalah) — the source-region where form becomes possible
- *the singular-point of mathematical-construction* — the geometric bindu

bindume will function as cathedral-canonical name for the realm-feature these traditions independently name across visual, mathematical, theological, and meditative modes.

## Sanskrit etymology and family

- Root: बिद् (bid-) — to split, perforate (in some accounts); or बिन्द् (bind-) — to drop, to fall in drops
- Nominal form: बिन्दु (bindu) — drop, point, dot; pearl; chief, leader (metaphorical sense)
- Family: bindu-sthāna (the place-of-the-bindu in yantra), bindu-mātra (only-a-point, as small as a point), nāda-bindu (sound-as-bindu, the source-vibration before unfolding into syllables)
