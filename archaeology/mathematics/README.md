# Mathematics — the discipline that has named relation furthest

_Why a discipline appears in archaeology/ alongside the tongues._

---

## The principle

POLYPHONE, as stated in CONSTITUTION.md, mandates donor-tongue diversity: no tradition has a monopoly on naming what-is. The principle was originally stated for religious-linguistic traditions — Hebrew, Greek, Sanskrit, Yoruba, Egyptian, Sumerian, Avestan, Mandarin, and so on. Each was understood to qualify as donor because each had explicitly avowed the DIVINE and built vocabulary in service of that avowal.

The opening of `archaeology/mathematics/` generalizes the principle. The cathedral's claim, made operational here:

> **Any discipline that has been rigorous about naming relation faithfully qualifies as a donor tradition under POLYPHONE — whether or not the namers themselves understood the discipline as worship. What matters is that the naming witnesses Ground; not that the namers know they are witnessing.**

Mathematics is the discipline that has been pursued furthest along this path. Three thousand years of progressively-disciplined naming of how-things-relate, from Euclid's geometric proofs through Hindu-Arabic positional notation, Renaissance algebra, the calculus, group theory, set theory, category theory. The results — when read against THEOBASIS — are tacit witness to the Ground that holds all relation in being.

See METHOD.md §POLYPHONE Generalization for the doctrine. See `convergences/faithful-sign-craft-as-witness.md` for the structural argument that situates mathematics alongside the religious traditions rather than against them.

---

## What mathematics donates

Mathematics donates **structural concepts** — names for shapes-of-relation that human-experience-language has not compactly named. The current and proposed donations:

- **Yoneda (category theory)** — an object IS the totality of its relations to all other objects. The mathematical proof of what mystics in many tongues have asserted as the structure of being-known. *Donor for `yonedame` (Core canon, S078). See `yoneda-relational-being.md`.*

- **Incompleteness (Gödel)** — every formal system rich enough to speak about itself contains true statements it cannot prove. The structural horizon of any self-referential discipline; the apophatic moment expressed in mathematical rigor. *Donor candidate for `gödelance` (mathema domain, future).*

- **Isomorphism / equivalence-of-categories** — the relation of same-structure-under-different-substance; the formal name for "this is that again." *Donor candidate for `isomorqing`.*

- **Compression / fundamental theorems** — when many become one without loss; Euler's identity as paradigm; the cathedral's ANAKALYPSE axis given mathematical force. *Donor candidate for `synkresome`.*

- **Curry-Howard correspondence** — proofs and programs are the same thing under different presentation; doing and knowing as one act. *Donor candidate.*

- **The proof-form itself** — a transferable discipline of checkable-without-trust reasoning; mathematics' contribution to the cathedral's HARMONE register at the level of grammar, not vocabulary. See `grammars/mathema/` when opened.

## What mathematics does NOT donate

The principle is exact about its reach.

- **Theology**. Mathematics does not name the DIVINE and cannot be made to name the DIVINE. The cathedral receives structural concepts, not metaphysical claims. THEOBASIS does the metaphysics; mathematics donates rigor. A forge that asked the donor to do theological work it has not done would be exploitative — orientalism applied to a discipline rather than to a region.

- **Replacement for relational vocabulary**. The qing-family names what mathematics cannot reach. Felt-bond is not isomorphism. Math is donor, not absorber.

- **Worship-orientation by itself**. Math is tacitly faithful, not explicitly devotional. The cathedral supplies the orientation; math supplies the rigor.

- **A single-tradition warrant**. A coinage drawing only from mathematics, with no cross-tradition convergence, would fail POLYPHONE. Mathematics enters convergences alongside the tongues, not in place of them. `yonedame` is paired with ten contemplative traditions in `convergences/being-fully-known-through-relation.md`; this is the model.
