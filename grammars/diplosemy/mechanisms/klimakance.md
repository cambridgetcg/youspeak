# Klimakance — the scalar-shift diplosemic mechanism

## The mechanism (summary)

A word whose meaning shifts systematically by the scale at which it is read — individual, dyadic, communal, structural, cosmic — with all scales simultaneously available and correlated.

**Natural examples (English):**

- *we*: intimate-dyad (two-person we), group (we as team), political-collective (we the people), royal (editorial we), cosmic (we as humanity). All scales live; context-choice selects primary-scale while secondary-scales remain latent.
- *home*: building, household, hometown, nation, felt-sense-of-belonging, cosmic-home. Five+ scales available simultaneously in poetic usage.
- *body*: individual-organism, body-politic, body-of-work, body-of-water. Scale-shifts are productive across philosophical domains.
- *family*: nuclear, extended, chosen, species, lineage, institution (Mafia). Scale-register selects meaning.
- *love*: personal-affection, romantic, familial, communal, divine. Greek had four words; English has one; scale-context disambiguates.

## The diplosemy

Klimakance is the structured availability of a word across multiple scales, where reading at one scale activates resonance with adjacent scales. Unlike pure polysemy (which has distinct meanings), Klimakance has ONE meaning read at different granularities; the meaning is scale-invariant, but what it names varies by the scale chosen.

## Engineering rules (provisional)

1. The word's meaning must scale-transform coherently — reading at individual-scale and at communal-scale must both yield the same semantic structure.
2. At least two scales must be *simultaneously* readable, not merely alternately.
3. The scales must belong to a coherent nesting (micro/meso/macro or individual/collective/cosmic).

## Use cases

- **Teaching:** naming a phenomenon at multiple scales simultaneously, revealing scale-invariance
- **Dual-delivery:** reader receives both personal and structural readings of a concept
- **Liturgy:** words that connect the beholder's interior experience to cosmic reality without loss of fidelity at either scale

## Relevance to YOUSPEAK

*Artiance* and *doxakallos* are potential Klimakance-candidates — they read at creaturely-scale (artisan's fit-to-craft) and at cosmic-scale (Ma'at's cosmic-order) simultaneously. The stress-test did not formally assess this, but the mechanism may already be operating latently in the Canon.

*Synophora* is NOT Klimakance — it is fixed at the between-beholders scale and does not transfer to larger collective-scales without losing structure.
