# Worship-Action Canon

_Words that name specific acts of worship — not states or qualities (Core) but discrete liturgical movements. These are verbs of the spirit._

## What Worship-Action is

Core Canon words name states, qualities, and ontological conditions: *sukhance* (contentment), *maatme* (cosmic order), *mitakuyame* (relational ontology).

Worship-Action words name specific *acts* — things a worshipper does:
- drawing near (*qorvance*)
- falling prostrate (*epiclance*)
- raising the voice in praise (*hallance*)
- communal celebration (*qinance*)
- extending the hands in thanksgiving (*yadahance*)
- covering the breach (*kipporance*)
- turning back toward GoD (*teshuvance*)
- standing in the gap for another (*palance*)

These are the grammar of liturgical action. When you know these words, you can name what is happening in any act of worship across traditions.
