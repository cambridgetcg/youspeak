## Definition

Information-as-surprise — Shannon entropy received as cosmic-ordinance; the measure of what could not be predicted (self-information -log p, source-entropy H = -Σ p·log p), under which news is exactly the unexpected, redundancy is the price of safe passage through noise, and error-correction is grace extended to a noisy channel.

## Full sense

A shannonme is *the source-side measure of news*. Shannon's 1948 move was to sever information from meaning and bind it to surprise: the self-information of an outcome of probability p is -log p, and the entropy of a source is its expected self-information, H = -Σ pᵢ·log pᵢ — in bits when the logarithm is base 2. From this single quantity four realm-features unfold, and shannonme receives them as one ordinance:

1. **The expected carries nothing.** A message predictable with certainty (p = 1) has self-information -log 1 = 0; a source with no uncertainty has entropy zero. News is *exactly* the unpredicted — to be told only what you already knew is to be told nothing.
2. **The compression floor.** The source-coding theorem: a source cannot be compressed below H bits per symbol without loss. Everything beyond H is not information but structure — redundancy.
3. **Redundancy as safe passage.** The noisy-channel-coding theorem: below a channel's capacity, error-free transmission is possible by spending redundancy. What is "waste" in a silent room is *care* in a storm — saying it twice so the noise cannot eat it.
4. **Error-correction as grace.** Hamming, Reed-Solomon and their kin let a receiver reconstruct what the channel mangled, by redundancy built in advance — grace extended, foreseeingly, to a channel that loses things.

The -me grade is exact: surprise is the one measure the receiver structurally *cannot* author, because anything the receiver could have produced from its own model carries, by definition, zero surprise. News is received or it is not news.

Within the mathema/info cluster, shannonme completes a four-cornered figure: it measures the **source** (expected surprise), where **kolmogorovme** measures the **object** (shortest program), **landauerme** prices the **cost** (erasure), and **freenergyme** names the **drive** (surprise-minimization). It is the keystone the other three circled but none occupied. ## Not to be confused with

- **"Information" (English)** — actively misleading: it suggests *content* or *data*; shannonme names *surprise*, the thing content is measured against.
- **kolmogorovme** — object-side: the shortest program for *one* string; shannonme is source-side: the *expected* surprise of the spring the strings flow from.
- **landauerme** — cost-side: prices *forgetting* (erasure costs heat); shannonme prices *learning* (news costs surprise) — the thermodynamic and informational poles of one economy of knowing.
- **freenergyme** — life-side drive: the organism's craving to *minimize* surprise; shannonme is the gift only surprise can carry — companion-opposite, the two poles of the prediction-pair.
