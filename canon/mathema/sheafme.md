## Definition

The measurable obstruction-class by which local-consistency fails to lift to global-coherence, named as received-cosmic-ordinance — the structural-form HARMONE-failures take when canonical-integration is obstructed.

## Full sense

A sheafme is *what is operative* when locally-consistent data refuses to lift to a single global-canonical-section. In algebraic topology, sheaf-cohomology classes (H¹, H², ...) measure these obstructions in successive degrees. In the cathedral, a sheafme is operative when:

- Two traditions name a realm-feature locally-coherently but their cathedral-compound does not stabilize at Core-grade (refine-not-canon)
- Distributed-systems cannot achieve consensus despite local-agreement (CAP-theorem instances)
- A scientific theory's local-predictions are correct but its global-coherence is obstructed (e.g., classical-mechanics + quantum-mechanics globally-incoherent without quantum-field-theory mediation)
- A relational-network preserves local-trust but accumulates global-distrust through structural-arrangement

sheafme makes the obstruction *namable* and *measurable* — the gap is not "incoherence" generically but a sheaf-cohomological structural-class with definite degree and definite measure.

## Example usage

1. "The forge refined-not-canonized; a degree-1 sheafme is operative" — naming a cathedral-forge-failure structurally.
2. "Local mathema-coherence + local language-coherence + global Core-failure suggests a sheafme between donor-classes" — diagnostic application.
3. "Quantum-mechanics and general-relativity are locally-coherent within their domains; their global integration is a sheafme awaiting resolution (quantum-gravity)" — physics-applied.
4. "Healthy distributed-systems minimize sheafmes through architectural-discipline; cathedral does the same through forge-discipline" — cross-domain pairing.

## Not to be confused with

- **HARMONE-failure** (generic) — HARMONE-failure is the bare-claim that coherence is obstructed; sheafme names the structural-form and degree of the obstruction
- **Contradiction** — direct-contradiction is degree-0 obstruction; sheafme captures higher-degree obstructions where no two pieces directly contradict but the global-section fails
- **Inconsistency** — inconsistency is a logical-property; sheafme is a structural-cohomological measure
