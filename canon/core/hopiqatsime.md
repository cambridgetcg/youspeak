# hopiqatsime

## Definition

Covenant-life-as-cosmic-maintenance-as-received-ordinance — the proper-ordered-existence whose maintenance by a covenanted people sustains the world. Not ethics merely, not culture merely, but a cosmological-maintenance covenant that an entire people holds: the lived-life-as-right-relationship that IS the world's maintenance, received as gift from the covenant-maker.

## Full sense

The Right-Action-As-Cosmic-Maintenance convergence cluster — the cathedral's most important convergence — is now complete with four poles:

- **duyuktame** (Cherokee) — the action-pole: right-action-as-cosmic-maintenance, the walking that sustains the world
- **hozhome** (Diné) — the condition-pole: beauty-harmony-balance-goodness as the unified cosmic ground, the path one walks in
- **koyaanisqatsime** (Hopi) — the diagnostic-pole: life-out-of-balance from abandoning right-relationship, the disorder that names what happens when maintenance stops
- **hopiqatsime** (Hopi) — the covenant-life pole: the lived-covenant-life that IS the maintenance — the proper-ordered-existence a covenanted people maintains to sustain the world

Hopiqatsime is the positive pole that the diagnostic pole (koyaanisqatsime) is the negative of. Koyaanisqatsime names the disorder from abandoning right-relationship; hopiqatsime names the right-relationship itself — the lived-covenant-life that prevents the disorder. The pair is the Hopi covenant's positive-negative dyad: the covenant-life and the disorder-from-abandoning-it.

### The three constitutive dimensions of Hopiqatsi

1. **The life-covenant**: the people received a specific assignment from Masau'u (deity of death, earth, fire, and boundaries) to be caretakers of the earth in a specific place. The assignment is received, not chosen.

2. **The covenant-condition**: access to the fourth world was given on the condition that the people live in hopiqatsi — the way of humility, hard work, cooperation, and proper ceremony. The world is conditional on the covenant-life.

3. **The communal-theological contract**: if the people maintain their ceremonies, their corn agriculture, their right-relationships, the world continues; if they abandon these, the world falls into koyaanisqatsi (life-out-of-balance). The maintenance is cosmological, not merely ethical.

### The democratization of cosmic-maintenance

The Hopi contribution is unique in the catalog: the cosmic-maintenance obligation belongs to an **entire covenanted people** — not a king (Hittite AN.TAḪ.ŠUM), not a priest-class (Avestan Yasna), not an individual (Cherokee duyukta), but a **community as unit**. This is the most democratized form of ritual-as-cosmic-maintenance theology: every person, every household, every clan participates in sustaining the world through their daily life.

The architecture-as-cosmological-map dimension deepens this: the entire inhabited settlement is a cosmological map. The daily movement of ordinary life through plaza, kiva, clan-house enacts cosmic orientation continuously. Just living in right-relationship in the right-place IS hopiqatsi. This is not a separate ritual practice — it is the texture of daily existence itself.

### The poverty-covenant

Masau'u — the covenant-maker — was poor, humble, dressed in rags. The covenant is not with power but with humility. The covenant-authority is stewardship, not domination. The covenant-conditions: live simply, live close to the earth, maintain ceremony, do not chase material excess. This is a theology of **covenantal-simplicity-as-cosmic-health** — distinct from prosperity theology, from imperial covenant-theology, from power-covenant.

The Hopi were led to the high desert mesas — the most difficult environment of all the available lands — as a test and as protection: the land is hard enough that only those committed to hopiqatsi would stay. The land itself filters for covenant.

### The -me marks received-ordinance

The -me suffix marks the covenant-life as received-ordinance: the assignment to be cosmological maintainers is gift, not achievement. The people did not choose to be world-sustainers; they received this role from Masau'u. The receiving IS the ordinance — the covenant is not a contract negotiated between equals but a gift that creates obligation. The -me refuses to let the covenant-life be understood as human construction: the world where communal right-living sustains the cosmos is gift-grade-given.

### The hopiqatsime-koyaanisqatsime pair

The dyad is the Hopi covenant's structural core: hopiqatsime (the covenant-life) and koyaanisqatsime (the disorder-from-abandoning-it). One names the health; the other names the disease. One names what you do; the other names what happens when you stop. The pair is diagnostic-and-prescriptive: koyaanisqatsime diagnoses the disorder and prescribes the cure (return to hopiqatsime); hopiqatsime names the lived-relationship that prevents the disorder from arising.

### Cross-tradition parallels

1. Hopi hopiqatsi — the covenant-life-as-cosmic-maintenance; the people's proper-ordered-existence sustains the world
2. Hittite AN.TAḪ.ŠUM — the 38-day royal spring festival; the king's performed faithfulness sustains the cosmos; Hopi extends from king to whole people
3. Avestan Yasna — the daily ritual fire-tending; if the fire goes out, cosmic order weakens; Hopi extends from priest to whole community
4. Cherokee duyukta — right-action-at-all-times; every individual's right-action maintains cosmic balance; the individual dimension
5. Diné hózhóójí (Blessingway) — the ceremony that restores hózhǫ́; the lived-way-of-beauty as ongoing maintenance
6. Hebrew berit (covenant) — the people's covenant with YHWH; keeping Torah → land flourishes; abandoning → land suffers; communal-covenantal but legal-juridical rather than ecological-cosmological

## Not to be confused with

- **duyuktame** — right-action-as-cosmic-maintenance (the walking); hopiqatsime is the covenant-life the walking maintains
- **hozhome** — beauty-harmony as cosmic condition (the path's character); hopiqatsime is the covenant that assigns the walking
- **koyaanisqatsime** — life-out-of-balance (the disorder); hopiqatsime is the lived-right-relationship that prevents the disorder
- **maatme** — cosmic-truth-order as principle (what IS); hopiqatsime is the inhabited-covenant-life that maintains what IS
- **tikkunme** — the repair-work (fixing after breakage); hopiqatsime is the maintenance (preventing breakage)
- **halakhame** — the walking-as-defined-practice (conformity to law); hopiqatsime is the covenantal-cosmological walking (sustaining the world)
- **telipme** — divine-withdrawal (what happens when maintenance stops); hopiqatsime is the maintenance

## Retirement conditions

This word would be superseded if a more precise or more learnable donor were found for the same concept — but the Hopi word is theologically precise and the concept is uniquely Hopi in its communal-covenantal structure. The hopiqatsime-koyaanisqatsime pair is the Hopi covenant's positive-negative dyad and is likely permanent in the cathedral. A potential companion could name the PROCESS of entering hopiqatsime (the covenant-reception event as distinct from the lived-covenant-condition) — but that would be a companion, not a replacement.
