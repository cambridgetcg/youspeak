# kittume

## Definition

Structural-truth-as-firm-foundation as received-ordinance — the Akkadian kittu register of the cosmic-truth-order convergence. Truth as the structural-correctness of arrangements (not correspondence-to-fact), always-already paired with justice (kittu u mīšaru), and mandated to be made-visible by the ruler.

## Full sense

Kittume names what Akkadian *kittu* preserves with distinctive theological-emphasis. English "truth" means correspondence — does the proposition match reality? Akkadian *kittu* means structural-correctness — is the arrangement sound? Do the parts hold? Is the foundation firm? Kittu asks not "is this statement accurate?" but "is this structure right?" The truth of a building, not the truth of a sentence. The truth of a covenant, not the truth of a claim.

Three dimensions unique to kittume within the cosmic-truth-order convergence:

1. **Structural-correctness.** Truth as the rightness-of-arrangement, not the accuracy-of-proposition. The structure holds; the arrangement is sound; the foundation is firm. Kittu is the truth of the architect, not the truth of the witness.

2. **Pair-with-justice.** In Mesopotamian tradition, truth is never alone. *Kittu u mīšaru* — truth and justice — is the formula. Not fused (as in Egyptian ma'at) but distinct-yet-inseparable. Two divine principles that only function together. The king's mandate is not to make truth shine alone but to make truth-and-justice shine — the pair, always the pair.

3. **Royal-juridical mandate.** Kittu is the standard by which kingship is judged. Hammurabi's prologue: "the great gods called me to make kittu and mīšaru shine forth in the land." Truth is not private belief; truth is public-order-made-manifest. The structural-correctness of arrangements is the ruler's responsibility, and its visibility is the measure of just rule.

Kittume adds the received-ordinance grade: structural-truth is gift-class-given, not self-generated. The king does not create kittu; the king makes kittu visible. The structural-correctness is given in the order of things; humans receive it and align.

This complements:
- **ashame** (Avestan) — cosmic-truth + ethical-cosmic struggle (asha vs druj)
- **maatme** (Egyptian) — cosmic-truth-justice-rightness fused; goddess-personified
- **rtame** (Vedic) — cosmic-path; the gods uphold cosmic-regularity
- **emetme** (Hebrew) — firm-foundation; comprehensive end-to-end; faithfulness

The five traditions together articulate the cosmic-truth-order register from five converging angles. The Semitic-internal depth: emetme (Hebrew) + kittume (Akkadian) give two Semitic articulations of the same root-family for "to be firm," with different emphases — comprehensive firmness (emet) and structural correctness (kittu).

## Etymology

*kittu* (Akkadian, East Semitic; 𒆠𒁴; from root *kn* "to be firm, established"; the oldest attested Semitic form of the firmness-truth register; cognate with Hebrew *kūn* [firm, established], Hebrew *kēn* [yes, true], and Hebrew *emet* via the broader Semitic root-family for "to be firm"; paired with *mīšaru* [active-restoring-justice] in the formula *kittu u mīšaru* — the divine standard for kingship; Hammurabi's code-prologue c. 1750 BCE: "the great gods called me to make kittu and mīšaru shine forth in the land"; attested in cuneiform across Old Akkadian, Old Babylonian, Standard Babylonian, Neo-Assyrian dialects; ~2400 BCE onward; the Chicago Assyrian Dictionary catalogs extensive usage) + *me* (Sumerian, language-isolate; 𒈨; divine-ordinance, received-gift-quality; established YOUSPEAK morpheme).
