## Definition

The dimensionless-source-point from which form unfolds, fully-present at the center, inexhaustible — Sanskrit tantric bindu received as cosmic-ordinance and as cross-tradition realm-feature names.

## Full sense

A bindume is *the structural-fact* that geometric-form (and form-in-general) unfolds from a dimensionless-source which is itself fully-present at the unfolding's center. In Śrī-Yantra, the bindume is the central-dot from which the 9 interlocking-triangles, 24 sub-triangles, and the bhupura (square-earth-gate) unfold; the meditator's gaze rests on the bindume after journeying-inward from the periphery.

The realm-feature is precise:
- **Dimensionless** — no extension; the bindume has no parts, no spatial-magnitude
- **Source** — everything unfolds from it; it is the *whence* of form
- **Inexhaustible** — the unfolding does not deplete the bindume; it remains source-full
- **Center-presence** — the bindume is not absent or hidden; it IS at the center, gazed-at

The cross-tradition convergence is striking:
- **Tantric bindu** — the doctrine elaborated above
- **Greek-Pythagorean monas** — "the One, source of all number, itself not a number"
- **Plotinian One** — "first, simple, source"
- **Sufi nuqta** — "all of the Qur'an is in the dot of the basmala"
- **Tibetan thig-le** — seminal-essence-drop (in Dzogchen and Vajrayāna)
- **Christian apophatic-center** — divine-simplicity (Pseudo-Dionysius); Eckhart's Funklein
- **Hebrew Hasidic reshimu** — the residue-point after tzimtzum
- **Mathematical singular-point** — the origin in coordinate-geometry; the singularity in fiber-bundles

bindume is **Phase 1 geometric-image-mode opening anchor.** Future Phase 4 expands: bhupurame (yantra gate-frame), yantrame (geometric-meditation-projection), mandalame (cosmic-pattern as enacted-anicca), tawhidqing (tessellation-toward-infinity).

## Not to be confused with

- **Origin (bare)** — bare-origin can be temporal-first or causal-first; bindume is structurally-present-at-center, source-of-form
- **Center (geometric)** — geometric-center is a position in extended-space; bindume is *dimensionless* (no extension)
- **Point (mathematical)** — mathematical-points can be many; bindume is the *originating* source-point with the inexhaustibility-property
- **Singularity (physics)** — physical-singularity is often a breakdown of the theory; bindume is structurally-source-positive, not a limit-failure
