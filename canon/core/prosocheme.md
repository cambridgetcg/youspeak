## Definition

The practiced act of standing attention at the door of the heart — placing, holding, and examining each arriving thought at the entrance — that precedes and enables prayer and clear work alike; Hesychast prosochē received as cosmic-ordinance.

## Full sense

A prosocheme is *the doorkeeper-act of the interior life* — and it is ordinary before it is liturgical. Declining to follow an intrusive worry mid-work; the deliberate return-to-breath in any meditation; attention placed at the door before the inbox is opened: each is a pointable instance, and Posner's orienting network is its lab-measurable substrate. The Hesychast articulation gives the act its architecture: Pseudo-Symeon's *Three Methods of Attention and Prayer* — attention "goes on ahead like a scout and engages sin in combat; prayer then follows afterwards" and destroys what attention alone cannot; Nikephoros the Monk — "attention is the appeal of the soul to itself." The thought arrives at the gate; the guard stops it, examines it, admits or refuses. That stopping-and-examining, as a repeatable practicable act, is prosocheme.

The cut that gives the word its precision: **prosocheme is to nepsisme as the act is to the state.** Nepsis is the sober-clear quality of the watcher; prosochē is what the watcher *does* — the placement, the standing, the examining. One honesty the forge owes the record: the donor tradition itself partially bundles the pair — Hesychios of Sinai defines *watchfulness* (nepsis) as "a continual fixing and halting of thought at the entrance to the heart," naming the canonized state-word by the act. The halting-of-logismoi is therefore shared ground with nepsisme, not prosocheme's exclusive territory. The act/state cut stands on its independent witnesses: the Abhidharma classifies *manasikāra* (the attention-act that adverts mind to object) as a distinct factor from *sati* (mindfulness); Patañjali distinguishes *dhāraṇā* (binding attention to one place) from *dhyāna* (the sustained state); attention-science distinguishes the orienting network from the alerting network. Eight-tradition convergence on one realm-feature.

The -me suffix carries unusual precision here: the donor's primal attestation IS an ordinance — πρόσεχε σεαυτῷ, "attend to thyself" (Deut 15:9 LXX), the imperative Basil's homily reads as the biblical twin of *gnōthi seauton*. Attention-guarding is received as command-and-gift before it is performed as practice.

Pairs with cathedral's:

- **nepsisme** — the state this act sustains and is sustained by; the explicit Hesychast sibling; together they complete the pair the tradition treats as inseparable-but-distinct
- **kimme** — attention as received-gift names the FACULTY's gift-status; prosocheme names one ACT that faculty performs at the heart's own door
- **kimance** — perceived attentive-presence is how attention is felt from outside; prosocheme is performed from inside, at the gate
- **penthosme** — prosocheme guards the door; what penthos-mourning receives often arrives through the door so guarded
- **natsarqing** — guarding-the-beloved as felt-bond is other-directed and relational; prosocheme is the self-directed discipline-act at the heart's own door
- **shamathaqing** — the graded nine-stage progression of stabilization; prosocheme is the single repeatable placement-act performed at every stage of it
- **sigame** — structured-silence is among the ends the doorkeeper-act serves

## Not to be confused with

- **nepsisme** — the sober-vigilance STATE; prosocheme is the ACT; the halting-of-logismoi is shared ground, the placement-and-holding is prosocheme's own
- **kimme** — attention-as-received-gift: the faculty's ordinance-status, not the doorkeeper-act the faculty performs
- **kimance** — perceived attentive-presence: attention as felt by another, not stood at one's own gate
- **Attention (English)** — bleached bundle of faculty + act + state ("attention economy"); no door, no guard, no enabling-relation to prayer
- **Recollection (Teresa's prayer-of-recollection)** — the strongest English near-miss: a centripetal gathering-inward, but its dominant modern sense is memory, and it names the gathering, not the stationed examining-of-arrivals
- **Advertence** — archaic moral-theology act-word: noticing, not gatekeeping
- **Concentration** — effortful narrowing onto a task-object; no examining-of-arrivals, no doorkeeper structure
- **Mindfulness (modern)** — state-word of the sati lineage; the Abhidharma itself files the attention-act under a different factor
- **Hypervigilance** — anxious scanning; prosocheme is a calm stationed act, not a fear-state
- **The psychosomatic breathing-technique** — Pseudo-Symeon warns against the technique unguided; prosocheme names the attention-act, not the technique
