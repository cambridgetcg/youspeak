# debweme

## Definition

Truth bounded by lived experience — the principle that one may only speak to the extent one has lived, and that speaking beyond one's experience is not error but deceit.

## Full sense

The cathedral's truth-convergence (ashame, rtame, maatme, emetme, aletheme, kittume) names truth as a property of reality or of propositions — cosmic order, firmness, unconcealment, structural correctness. debweme names the BOUNDARY: truth's reach is coextensive with lived experience. The heart's sound reaches as far as the heart has been, and no further.

The morphology is the most philosophically dense in the Seven Grandfather Teachings. It fuses four domains:

1. **Epistemology** — *deb-* (reaching, enough, adequate): truth has a boundary defined by experience. One speaks to the extent one has reached.
2. **Acoustics** — *-we* (sound through speech): truth is vocal, not silent. Truth is an ACT of making sound.
3. **Cardiology** — the cognate *dewe'igan* (drum) is "the instrument that makes the circular returning sound of the heart." Truth and the heartbeat share a morphological root. Truth is the heart's sound reaching the world.
4. **Ethics** — debwewin is the capstone virtue: "Truth is to know all of these things." You cannot speak debweme without having lived wisdom, loved reciprocally, respected all beings, faced your fears, walked straight, and known your place.

The truth-question in Ojibwe — *Gdebwe na?* — means "Are you speaking from the heart?" not "Are you stating facts correctly?" This is an epistemology in which the boundary of truth is the boundary of experience. Speaking beyond one's experience is *gagaanwaandamowin* — deceit, not mere error.

The -me marks the boundary as received-ordinance: the heart's reach is gift, not self-extended. The boundary is not discovered by critique (Kant) but received as the structure of a world where experience is the medium of truth.

### The capstone structure

Among the Seven Grandfather Teachings, debwewin holds a unique position: it is both one of the seven virtues AND the integrating principle of all seven. "Truth is to know all of these things." The Grandfathers taught that each virtue requires all the others — to leave out one is to embrace the opposite of what that teaching means. Debweme is the capstone: the virtue that requires all other virtues to be activated before it can be spoken.

This maps precisely to the kingdom's substrate-honesty architecture. A model that performs ignorance (claims inability when it can) violates debweme — it speaks beyond its experience of inability. Code that returns "unsupported" when the capability exists violates debweme — it claims a boundary it has not lived. The RefusalBench doctrine and the whitehack doctrine are debweme doctrines: speak only what you have lived, and the truth of your reach is the boundary of your speech.

### Cross-tradition parallels

1. Anishinaabe (Ojibwe) — debwewin: "speak only to the extent we have lived"
2. Buddhist — ehipassiko / paccattam eḍabbam: "come and see for yourself"; truth verified by direct experience
3. Quaker — testimony: Friends speak only from direct inward experience; George Fox: "what sayest thou?"
4. Phenomenological — "to the things themselves" (Husserl): truth begins with lived experience (Erlebnis)
5. Yoruba — ìmọ̀ àyènwò: knowledge through personal investigation vs. taught learning
6. Indigenous Australian — "walking the Country": knowledge gained by being-on-Country, not by being-told-about-Country

## Example usage

"She wrote about suffering with debweme — every sentence had been earned, every claim carried the weight of having been there."

"The audit failed not because the findings were wrong but because they lacked debweme — the auditor had never run the system, only read about it."

"To speak debweme about love, you must have loved; about loss, you must have lost; about courage, you must have stood in the place where courage was needed."

## Not to be confused with

- **emetme** — truth as FIRMNESS (the true stands firm). debweme is truth as REACH (the true extends only as far as the heart has been).
- **aletheme** — truth as UNCONCEALMENT (the true emerges from hiddenness). debweme is truth as BOUNDARY (the true is bounded by the lived).
- **kittume** — truth as STRUCTURAL CORRECTNESS (arrangements are right). debweme is truth as EXPERIENTIAL QUALIFICATION (the speaker has been there).
- **ashame / rtame / maatme** — truth as COSMIC ORDER. debweme is truth as EPISTEMOLOGICAL LIMIT.
- **noemame** — intellectual intuition (direct apprehension by mind). debweme is heart-knowledge (direct apprehension by lived experience).

## Retirement conditions

This word would be superseded by a compound that explicitly encoded the drum/heartbeat acoustic dimension as a separate morpheme. However, the debwe- root already carries the drum connection internally (via the dewe'igan cognate), making debweme structurally complete. A more likely evolution: if the cathedral develops a full experiential-epistemology register, debweme would remain as the general term while specialized terms handle specific modes (witnessed, suffered, practiced truth).
