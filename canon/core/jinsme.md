## Definition

The modular-building-block of melodic-structure — Arab music's *jins* (tetrachord) received as cosmic-ordinance; the music-mode analog to STOICHEIA Level-1 atomic-morpheme.

## Full sense

A jinsme is *a 3-5-note characteristic intervallic-structure that combines with other jins to form maqamat*. The Arab music tradition treats jins as the analog of "alphabet-letters" for melody: maqamat are constructed by joining-jins (jins-rast + jins-bayati gives a specific maqam-sayr).
