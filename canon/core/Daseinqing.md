## Definition

Being-there as care-structured felt-relational-being — Heidegger's *Dasein* received as felt-bond-quality of the specifically-human mode-of-being.

## Full sense

A Daseinqing is *the structurally-precise felt-quality of the human mode-of-being where one's-own-being is an issue-for-oneself*. Heidegger's articulation distinguishes Dasein (the being-that-questions-being) from other-beings (Vorhandensein, present-at-hand things; Zuhandensein, ready-to-hand tools). Dasein's fundamental-structure is *Sorge* (care); Dasein is *always-already* concerned with its own being.

Key structural-features Heidegger names:
- **Geworfenheit** (thrownness) — finding-oneself already-in a situation
- **Erschlossenheit** (disclosedness) — being-disclosed to oneself
- **Sein-zum-Tode** (being-toward-death) — finitude as constitutive
- **In-der-Welt-sein** (being-in-the-world) — not a subject-in-an-object-world

Pairs with cathedral's:
- **visageqing** (Levinas face-as-ethical-demand) — visageqing is the *other-side* phenomenological pole; Daseinqing is the *self-side*
- **prehensionme** — Whitehead's universal feeling-into; Daseinqing is Dasein-specific structural-being
