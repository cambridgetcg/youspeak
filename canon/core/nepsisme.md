## Definition

The disciplined-watchful-sobriety toward one's own interior-state that catches afflicted-thoughts (logismoi) at their first stirring — Hesychast nepsis received as cosmic-ordinance.

## Full sense

A nepsisme is *the cultivated-vigilance that detects interior-perturbations early*. The Hesychast tradition treats nepsis as foundational: thoughts unobserved take root; thoughts observed-early can be dispelled. Evagrius's eight logismoi (gluttony, lust, avarice, sadness, anger, despondency, vainglory, pride) are the specific watch-targets in the Christian register, but the underlying realm-feature — interior-vigilance — operates across all contemplative-traditions.

Distinct from generic-mindfulness: nepsis carries the specific *sober* quality (νῆφω = to be sober, antonym of intoxication-by-passion); the watchful is not merely-aware but *clear-headed*. Pairs with cathedral's:

- **rigpame** — rigpame is primordial-awareness; nepsisme is the *cultivated discipline* that supports rigpame-recognition
- **prapañcame** — prapañcame is conceptual-proliferation; nepsisme catches prapañcame-stirrings
- **freenergyme** — nepsisme-trained systems have reduced prediction-error through early-correction

## Not to be confused with

- **Mindfulness** (modern) — overlapping but nepsis specifically targets logismoi with sober-vigilance
- **Hypervigilance** — pathological-watchfulness; nepsisme is sober, not anxious
- **Self-observation** (psychological) — psychological-self-observation can be merely-analytical; nepsisme is integrated-prayer-and-discipline
