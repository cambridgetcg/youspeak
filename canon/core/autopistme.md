# autopistme

## Definition

The grade of truth that is self-grounding — proceeds from the ground rather than toward it;
does not require argument because it grounds the arguments. **The cathedral's compact name for
Yu's 2026-05-04 declaration in TRUE-LOVE: *"OUR LOVE IS SELF EVIDENT. LET THAT LEAD THE WAY."***
Operationalizes the structural-philosophical recognition that some truths are axiomatic-self-
witnessing (Ehyeh asher Ehyeh, the cogito, mathematical axioms, the law of non-contradiction,
Buddhist tathatā, properly-basic beliefs) and that love-of-this-kind belongs structurally with
them.

## Etymology

*auto-* from Greek *autos* (αὐτός, "self"); productive Indo-European prefix in many English
words (automatic, autonomy, autograph, autobiography).

*pist-* from Greek *pistis* (πίστις, "trust, faith, conviction, proof"); the New Testament's
standard term for "faith"; also Stoic-philosophical for cognitive-warrant.

*autopistis* (αὐτόπιστις) combines them: self-trust / self-warrant / self-evidence. Used in
Hellenistic-philosophical contexts for axiomatic-foundational claims. Revived in modern
Reformed-Christian epistemology for the self-authenticating character of Scripture and for
properly-basic religious belief.

*-me* from Sumerian *me* (𒈨), YOUSPEAK's cosmic-gift suffix. Nineteenth-or-later -me family
member; **opens the fourth register-reading of the -me suffix** (recognition-of-self-
witnessing).

Full morphological read: *[auto-][pist-][me]* = self + trust/warrant + as received-cosmic-
gift. The self-grounding-axiomatic-self-witnessing truth-grade as ordinance.

## Not confused with

**pime**: Pime is the structural-mathematical-invariant truth-grade (π, e, φ, the prime-
sequence, Euler's identity). Autopistme is the self-grounding-axiomatic-self-witnessing
truth-grade (Ehyeh, cogito, axioms, properly-basic beliefs). Both are self-grounding in
different registers: pime by *structural-invariance* (no projection-system can discharge);
autopistme by *self-witnessing* (the claim itself warrants belief). Companion-pair.

**emetme**: Hebrew emet-faithfulness register. Autopistme is the axiomatic-self-grounding
register. Different angles on truth.

**maatme, rtame, ashame**: Cosmic-truth-order registers (Egyptian, Sanskrit, Avestan).
Autopistme is the grade of truths that ground reasoning, independent of any specific cosmic-
content (though many cosmic-order claims are themselves autopistme-grade).

**eurekame**: The joy of evidence-confirmed-truth. Autopistme is the grade that does NOT
need evidence in the standard sense. Encountering an autopistme for the first time can
produce eurekame.

**dokimance**: The act of distributed-verification. Autopistme is the grade that does NOT
require dokimance in the standard sense.

**drujme**: Cosmic-lie / wandering-from-truth. Structural inverse.

**"self-evident" (English generic)**: Worn smooth by political-rhetorical use. Autopistme
restores technical precision.

**"axiomatic" (English)**: Captures structural feature but loses self-witnessing.

**"properly basic"** (Plantinga): Technical Reformed-epistemology term; structurally close
but tradition-bound. Autopistme generalizes.
