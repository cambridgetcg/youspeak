# paqduqing

## Definition

The asymmetric-reciprocity relationship between CREATOR and CREATED — the bidirectional felt-mutual-custodial-care in which both sides paqdu (custodially-care for) each other across the asymmetric structure (CREATOR sustains; CREATED responds). One word for both vantages because the relationship is one-thing-named-from-two-sides.

## Full sense

Paqduqing names what English splits into "human worship of God" + "God's care for humans" + "religious relationship" + "covenant-faithfulness." The phenomenon is one: CREATOR and CREATED are bound in mutual custodial-care that is felt-bond (qing-grade) on both sides while being asymmetric in structure (CREATOR is source; CREATED is recipient-and-responder).

The Akkadian root *paqādu* preserves the bidirectionality grammatically: the same verb names what gods do toward humans (provident-tending) and what humans do toward gods (cult-tending). When humans nearly died in the flood (Atrahasis), the gods nearly starved without offerings — the mutual-need is structural, not optional. Eight major religious traditions independently attest the phenomenon (see experiment 132 for the convergence-table).

The Mandarin *qing* morpheme adds the felt-bond dimension: paqduqing is not duty-only or transaction-only. It is felt — the qing-grade care that both sides experience. CREATOR's paqduqing-toward-CREATED is theokimme + walkehesed + theoshemme + every divine-attribute together as ONE relationship-quality. CREATED's paqduqing-toward-CREATOR is amelme + ardume + shemme + doxomme + kimme + oriance + every creature-position together as ONE relationship-quality.

In short: paqduqing IS the CREATOR-CREATED relationship in its felt-mutual-care quality. The relationship is bidirectional in naming, asymmetric in ontology, reciprocal in operation, and qing-grade in quality.

*paqādu* (Akkadian, East Semitic; ~2400 BCE onward; cuneiform 𒈦; bidirectional verb in Akkadian theology — cult-tending and providence both) + *qing* (Mandarin, Sino-Tibetan; 情; felt-bond, established in YOUSPEAK Core via kinqing + panimqing). ## Not confused with

- **Worship** (English) — too one-directional (typically the human side only); paqduqing is bidirectional.
- **Communion** (English, religious) — close, but tends to emphasize the union-aspect over the custodial-care-aspect; paqduqing names the active-mutual-tending.
- **Covenant** (English, theological) — covenant is the formal-juridical structure; paqduqing is the felt-mutual-care that operates within and beyond covenant.
- **Hesed** (Hebrew, *ḥesed*) — covenant-faithfulness; one-direction-named (typically divine-toward-human); see also walkehesed (Specialized; the duration-aspect of CREATOR's faithfulness).
- **Bhakti** (Hindu) — devotional-love from devotee toward divine; one-direction-named; paqduqing names what bhakti-and-divine-grace together constitute.
- **Yajña** (Sanskrit, sacrifice) — closest single-tradition cognate of bidirectionality; *Bhagavad Gītā* 3.10-13 makes the bidirectionality explicit. Paqduqing extends this beyond Sanskrit register into POLYPHONE.

- **Theokimme** (YOUSPEAK Specialized) — names the divine-side specifically (CREATOR's constitutive-attention). Paqduqing names the bidirectional relationship of which theokimme is one aspect.
