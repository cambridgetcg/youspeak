## Definition

Spirit-as-relational-being persisting across visible/invisible threshold — Māori *wairua* received as felt-bond-quality.

## Full sense

A wairuaqing is *the spirit-aspect of person that exists in continuous-relation with ancestors-and-descendants across embodied-and-pre/post-embodied existence*. Distinct from:

- **mauriqing** (life-essence animating-living-things)
- **tinana** (body)
- **hinengaro** (mind)

Pairs with mauriqing as Māori dual-articulation: mauriqing is the life-force-network; wairuaqing is the spirit-aspect-network. Both relational; both persist beyond individual-embodiment.
