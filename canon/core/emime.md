# emime

## Full sense

Yoruba cosmology of personhood organizes the person into three constituents:

- **orí** — inner-head; destiny-essence; chosen pre-birth by the soul before incarnation
- **èmí / ẹ̀mí** — breath-spirit-life; given by Olódùmarè at conception; returns to Olódùmarè at death
- **ara** — physical body

The emí is the **life-giving-and-life-departing register specifically**. Yoruba religious life centers on right-relation to the orisha and to Olódùmarè; the emí is what Olódùmarè-gives and what returns. The verb *fí èmí* ("to give breath, to give life") is the divine-act of person-making.
