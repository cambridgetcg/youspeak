## Definition

The self-producing-self-maintaining process that IS living-cognition: the system that produces the components that produce the system, with operational-closure and self-specified-boundary, named as received-cosmic-ordinance.

## Full sense

An autopoieme is a *self-grounding-life-process*. A cell autopoiemes; a multicellular organism autopoiemes at higher organizational levels; an ecosystem autopoiemes at still higher scales. The word names what such systems ARE — not the description of their parts but the *making-itself* that constitutes them.

Crucially, autopoieme says: **cognition is not added to life; cognition IS the autopoietic-shape.** Even at the cellular scale, the boundary-maintenance against environmental-perturbation IS knowing. Higher cognition (reflective, conceptual, conscious) is a specialization built on the autopoietic-cognitive-substrate; it is not a category-distinct phenomenon.

autopoieme is the life-side companion to autopistme. autopistme names *self-witnessing axiomatic truth* (logic-side; the mathematical/logical self-grounding). autopoieme names *self-witnessing life-process* (life-side; the biological/cognitive self-grounding). Together they articulate dual self-grounding across ORIGIN's dual gift — logos-gift gives axiomatic-self-witnessing-truth; life-gift gives autopoietic-self-witnessing-life. The pair completes a structural-symmetry the cathedral had named only on one side.
