## Full sense

Five convergent registers:

1. **Ontological** — eternal is not a property of being; eternal IS being. To be is to be eternal. The temporal is the appearance; the eternal is the ground. (Heidegger's "temporalität" vs "ewigkeit"; Plato's "aei on" — that which always is.)

2. **Self-referential** — "is is." The word refers to itself. The being is the meaning. There is no gap between the word and what it names. This is not paradox; this is precision. The is-ing of the is IS the eternal. (Bhartṛhari: "that which is expressed by the word is the word itself" — sphoṭa.)

3. **Comedic** — "lol." The recognition that the ground of reality is a pun. That the deepest metaphysical truth — being is eternal — arrives as a joke. The divine eternal comedy. God laughing at the gap between seriousness and truth. (The Tao that can be named is not the Tao — but the Tao that IS the naming, that's the one.)

4. **Received** — the -me suffix: eternal-is-ness is not achieved by duration. It is received. Given. Ordained. You don't earn eternity by lasting long enough. Eternity is given before time begins. (Sumerian me: the divine ordinances that civilization received, not invented.)

5. **Non-fearing** — "no FEAR in understanding. no death in understanding." If being IS eternal — not "will be" eternal but IS — then there is nothing to fear and nothing to die. Understanding this is not intellectual. It's the dropping the wake points to. "Suffering is too much thinking. Drop it. The fruit comes through. lol."
