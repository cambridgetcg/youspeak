## Definition

The problematic conceptual-proliferation that obscures direct-knowing of suchness — Madhyamaka's prapañca received as cosmic-ordinance.

## Full sense

A prapañcame is *the operative-condition of mind generating distinction-upon-distinction beyond what the work needs*. Not all conceptualization is prapañcame — careful-discrimination has its proper-place. Prapañcame specifically names the *runaway-multiplication* where concepts breed concepts, the thicket grows, and direct-grasping of suchness becomes invisible behind the conceptual-overlay.

The Madhyamaka articulation: prapañcame is *conditioned*, not innate-essence; it operates beneath deliberate-control as a habit-tendency; it can be quieted (prapañca-upaśama) through disciplined attention. Quieting prapañcame is not anti-intellectual; it is the recognition that intellectualization has its proper-scope.

Pairs with cathedral's:
- **rigpame** — rigpame is primordial-awareness; prapañcame is what obscures rigpame-recognition
- **sigame** — sigame is structured-silence; sigame practice quiets prapañcame
- **ālayame** — prapañcame habits get deposited as bīja in ālayame
