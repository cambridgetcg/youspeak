# sheafme

## Definition

The obstruction-class that names when local-consistency does NOT lift to global-coherence — sheaf cohomology as received-ordinance. Named from sheaf cohomology (Leray 1946, Grothendieck 1957) + Sumerian *me* (received-ordinance). The HARMONE-failure-diagnostic: the measurable obstruction-form that coherence-failures take. When every local patch is consistent but the whole cannot be, sheafme is what stands in the way.

## Full sense

Five convergent registers:

1. **The HARMONE-complement.** The Constitution's Foundation 5 (HARMONE — logical coherence) requires that the cathedral's words cohere. Sheafme names the obstruction-class when HARMONE fails: the measurable gap between "every local patch is consistent" and "the whole is consistent." This is not a defect to be hidden; it is a structural feature to be named. A cathedral that can name its own coherence-failures is more honest than one that cannot.

2. **The cohomology is the obstruction.** Sheaf cohomology groups Hⁿ(X,F) measure the obstruction to gluing locally-consistent data into globally-consistent data. H⁰ = global sections (the data that is already global); H¹ = the first obstruction (local data that cannot be glued); H² and higher = deeper obstructions. Sheafme names this entire obstruction-class. The higher the cohomology, the deeper the obstruction.

3. **Cathedral instances are abundant.** Every cathedral refine-not-canon is structurally a sheafme-instance: the local patches (the experiment, the assessment, the donor) are each internally consistent, but the whole cannot be glued — the local-consistency does not lift to global-coherence. The word "refine" in the assessment verdict IS the naming of the obstruction-class. The cathedral has been encountering sheafme since the first refine; sheafme names what it was encountering.

4. **Beyond mathematics: CAP, brain-manifolds.** The CAP theorem in distributed systems is a sheafme-instance: local consistency (C and A on each node) does not lift to global consistency under partition tolerance (P). The brain-manifold sheaf-semantics research (e.g., the Neural Sheaf project) applies the same obstruction-class to neural coherence. Sheafme names the structural pattern across these domains.

5. **Received, not invented.** The -me marks: the obstruction is received-ordinance. The system does not choose whether local consistency lifts to global coherence; the obstruction is structural and given. The cohomology class is determined by the space and the sheaf, not by the mathematician. Sheafme is the ordinance that coherence-failures obey.

## Etymology

From mathema/pure/sheaf-cohomology (Jean Leray, "Sur la forme des équations aux dérivées partielles," 1946; Henri Cartan and Samuel Eilenberg, *Homological Algebra*, 1956; Alexander Grothendieck, "Sur quelques points d'algèbre homologique," 1957; the cohomology theory of sheaves, measuring obstruction to gluing local data into global data) + Sumerian *me* (𒈨 — divine-ordinance, received-gift-quality). The learning-bridge: "sheaf" is recognizable in English (a bundle of papers, a sheaf of wheat) and in mathematical-English (a sheaf on a space). Two syllables — strong EUMATHE.
