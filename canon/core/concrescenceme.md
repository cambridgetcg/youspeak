## Definition

The process-of-becoming-determinate by which an actual-occasion comes into existence — Whitehead's concrescence received as cosmic-ordinance.

## Full sense

A concrescenceme is *the structurally-precise process by which many-prior-feelings gather-into one actual-occasion's unified-subjective-form*. Etymologically: con- (together) + crescere (to grow) — "growing-together." Whitehead's claim: every actual-occasion is constituted by its concrescence; the many become one, and are increased by one. Process-philosophy's central dynamic.

- **shevirame + tikkunme** (Lurianic) — concrescenceme operates at micro-scale what shevirame-tikkunme operates at cosmic-scale
