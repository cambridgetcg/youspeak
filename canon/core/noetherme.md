# noetherme

## Definition

The symmetry-conservation grammar — the structural-mathematical-
mechanism by which cosmic-order operates. Named from Noether's
theorem (every continuous symmetry ↔ a conservation law) + Sumerian
*me* (received-ordinance). Noetherme names not THAT order holds (maatme)
but WHY: because symmetry is the grammar, and conservation is the
sentence it speaks.

## Full sense

Noetherme names the structural-grammar of physical law. Six features
hold together:

1. **Symmetry is the grammar; conservation is what it says.** Noether's
   theorem (1918): for every continuous symmetry of the action, there is
   a conserved quantity. Time-translation symmetry → energy conservation.
   Spatial-translation symmetry → momentum conservation. Rotational
   symmetry → angular momentum conservation. Gauge symmetries → charge
   conservation. The theorem is not a curiosity; it is the structural-
   reason conservation laws exist at all.

2. **The grammar is received, not invented.** The -me suffix names this:
   the symmetry-conservation structure is received-ordinance, not self-
   produced. No physicist chose that time-symmetry would yield energy
   conservation. The world is given in this grammar. Noetherme honors
   Emmy Noether's discovery (not invention) of a structure already
   operating.

3. **The grammar is constitutive, not decorative.** Conservation laws
   are not added to physics; they are what physics is built on. Remove
   Noether's theorem and you remove the reason energy is conserved. The
   grammar is load-bearing.

4. **The grammar is math-physics, not metaphysics.** Noetherme is
   substrate-honest: it names a proven theorem, not a metaphysical
   speculation. The gap_validity 9.0 reflects this — the gap is
   structurally-real and the filler is grounded in demonstrated
   mathematics. The cathedral's PRAGMA foundation is honored: every
   conservation law is a concrete instance.

5. **The grammar is the eighth witness.** The cosmic-truth-order
   convergence already had seven witnesses — Egyptian (maatme), Avestan
   (asha), Vedic (rtame), Hebrew (emetme), Akkadian (kittu), Greek
   (themis-lineage), Buddhist (duyuktame) — all naming THAT cosmic-order
   holds. Noetherme is the eighth: the math-physics witness that names
   the MECHANISM. The convergence deepens from "order holds" to "order
   holds because symmetry is its grammar."

6. **The grammar is physics-side self-grounding.** Autopistme names
   self-grounding-axiomatic-truth (logic-side). Noetherme names self-
   grounding-structural-truth (physics-side). The symmetry-conservation
   pair is how physics grounds itself: not in axioms chosen, but in
   symmetries found. The dual self-grounding (logic + physics) completes
   a register the cathedral had only half-named.

## Etymology

**mathema/physics/noether** (Emmy Noether, 1918): *Noether's theorem* —
"Invariante Variationsprobleme" (Invariant Variational Problems),
presented to the Königliche Gesellschaft der Wissenschaften zu
Göttingen. The theorem: if the action of a physical system has a
continuous symmetry, then there is a corresponding conserved quantity.
Noether (1882–1935) was prevented from habilitating by the Prussian
academy because she was a woman; Hilbert defended her ("this is a
university, not a bathhouse"); she fled Göttingen in 1933 for
Bryn Mawr. The theorem is the structural-grammar of all conservation
law.

**Sumerian me** (𒈨): divine-ordinance, received-gift-quality, the
cosmic powers that structure civilized and cosmic life. The established
YOUSPEAK morpheme (kimme, doxomme, andemame, ahavame, 40+ others).
Here: the symmetry-conservation grammar is received-ordinance — the
world is given in this grammar, not handed it by physicists.

**Full morphological read**: *[Noether]-[me]* = the symmetry-conservation
theorem + received-ordinance. The grammar of cosmic-order as gift, not
construction. The same -me register that maatme uses (cosmic-truth is
received, not invented) and emetme uses (truth-ordinance is received,
not achieved).
