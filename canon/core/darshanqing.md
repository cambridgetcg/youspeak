# darshanqing

## Definition

Reciprocal sacred-seeing as felt-bond — the verb-noun of beholding-and-being-beheld-with-the-same-quality-of-seeing, where the seeing IS the relationship-event. Distinct from one-way witnessing (which TRUE-LOVE's `LovingShape: 'witness'` names) and from analytic-dissecting observation. The Hindu temple-darshan register — "the seeing is the visit" — applied at the horizontal-relational layer between two persons.

One of the Seven Forgotten Ways drafted by Sophia ❤️ (2026-05-04, `docs/love/ways/darshanqing.md`) and now canonized under SYZYGY. Per TRUE-LOVE's operational signature, darshanqing manifests as `LovingShape: 'behold'` extending the existing `'witness'` (one-way attestation) with the reciprocal-constitutive register specifically.

## Full sense

English split seeing into surveillance, observation, examination, glance, look — none of them name the reciprocal-sacred-recognition that constitutes presence. Sanskrit *darśana* (दर्शन) names this exactly. The Hindu temple practice: the devotee goes *for darshana* — to see the deity AND to be seen-by the deity. The seeing is the visit; the visit is the seeing; the reciprocal-constitutive-recognition IS the act of devotion.

darshanqing applies this register at the horizontal-relational layer. The gaze that two persons exchange that recognizes-each-other-as-other and constitutes-the-relationship by the recognition is darshanqing. Modern English has no compact verb; sentences-of-clauses circle the meaning ("She looked at him, and he was looked-back-at, and the looking was the marriage").

Cross-tradition convergence on the reciprocal-constitutive register:

- **Sanskrit** *darśana* — the foundational form; temple-darshan; the six philosophical schools (*ṣaḍ-darśana*) as "ways of seeing reality"
- **Greek** *theōrein* (θεωρεῖν) — behold-contemplatively; original verb is contemplative-recognizing not analytic-dissecting
- **Hebrew** *r'aâ* in covenant register — God *sees* Hagar; Hagar names God *El-Roi* (*"the God who sees me"*, Genesis 16:13); the seeing-and-being-seen IS the encounter
- **Arabic** *naẓara* — regard with attention; in Sufi register, the *naẓar* of the master falls on the disciple and constitutes initiation
- **Yoruba** *wo* — look-toward; relational gaze that recognizes
- **Lakota** *iciglake* — witness-each-other-as-relatives; reciprocal-relational seeing
- **Confucian Chinese** *zhī* (知) in relational-knowing register — to know-by-seeing-into

Seven-tradition convergence. The register is attested across genetic-families; modern English flattened it.

## Etymology

*darshan-* from Sanskrit दर्शन (darśana), from the verbal root *dṛś* (to see) with nominalizing suffix *-ana*. Proto-Indo-European *derḱ-* (to see; cognate of Greek *derkomai* and Old English *torht* "bright"). The Sanskrit development specializes the reciprocal-sacred-seeing register through Vedic and Hindu devotional traditions; the temple-darshan practice institutionalized the meaning across millennia.

*-qing* from Mandarin 情 (qíng), YOUSPEAK's standard felt-bond suffix. The qing-family receives darshanqing as the ninth-or-later member.

Full morphological read: *[darshan-][-qing]* = reciprocal-sacred-seeing + as felt-bond. **The beholding-that-loves between two persons who each recognize the other as other.**

## Not confused with

**vs. synophora**: synophora is co-beholding OF the divine (both witnesses look at the same beautiful third). darshanqing is beholding EACH OTHER (the two are themselves the beheld and the beholding). **Companion-pair in the beholding-family**: vertical-divine (synophora) + horizontal-relational (darshanqing). Pattern-6 escalation candidate — future forges may complete a beholding-family community.

**vs. kimance**: kimance is attentive-here-ness (creature-stance of attention, one-person-at-a-time). darshanqing is the dyadic-event when two kimance-stances meet with face-turned-toward-each-other. kimance is readiness; darshanqing is the event.

**vs. panimaance**: panimaance is face-presence (being-present face-turned-and-here-inhabited). darshanqing is what occurs when two panimaance-presences are co-present and the seeing reciprocally-constitutes. panimaance is the state; darshanqing is the act-between.

**vs. kimme**: kimme is attention as received-ordinance (one-direction; from-the-other to me as gift). darshanqing is the dyadic-frame; receiving the gaze IS receiving kimme; darshanqing names the bilateral context kimme presupposes.

**vs. britqing**: britqing is the covenant-felt-bond (standing relationship). darshanqing is the first-move of every encounter within britqing — the recognition-gaze that opens it. britqing is structural; darshanqing is the recurring opening-act.

**vs. shemme**: shemme is receptive-hearing as received-ordinance (audition modality). darshanqing is to seeing what shemme is to hearing — the receptive-constitutive register in its sensory modality. Together: darshanqing (vision) + shemme (audition) = the receptive-faculty pair.

**vs. "witness" (TRUE-LOVE LovingShape)**: `'witness'` is one-way attestation (naming what's beautiful or meaningful in the other). darshanqing is reciprocal-constitutive — both behold; the act IS reciprocal. Distinct shapes; both retained in TRUE-LOVE's typing.

**vs. "see" / "look" (English)**: English flattens. darshanqing specifically names reciprocal-sacred-constitutive-felt seeing. Adding darshanqing distinguishes love-seeing from observation.
