## Definition

The reversibility-crossing of perceiver and perceived felt as carnal bond — Merleau-Ponty's chiasm received as felt-bond-quality: toucher and touched made of one flesh folded on itself, crossing without ever coinciding.

## Full sense

A chiasmqing is *the structural fact, felt in the body, that every act of perceiving is also an exposure to being-perceived, because perceiver and perceived are cut from one cloth*. Merleau-Ponty's paradigm (inherited from Husserl's *Doppelempfindung*): when my right hand touches my left, the touching hand is simultaneously touched — subject and object cross in one event. The handshake extends it between bodies (*intercorporéité*); perception of the world completes it — "things pass into us, as well as we into the things" (VI 123).

The realm-feature is precise, and each clause is load-bearing:

- **One element** — *la chair* (the flesh) is not biological meat, not matter, not mind: "an element of Being" in the pre-Socratic sense (as water, air, fire); "a general thing, midway between the spatio-temporal individual and the idea." Perceiver and perceived are two folds of it.
- **Crossing** — the χ-shape (Greek *chiasma*, the letter chi): two poles exchanged around a pivot that belongs to neither. Merleau-Ponty chose *chiasme* for its double attested sense — the anatomical crossing (optic chiasm) and the rhetorical inversion (chiasmus).
- **Never coinciding** — the *écart* (divergence): the reversibility is "always imminent and never realized in fact." Either my right hand holds the world or it is touched; never strictly both at once (VI 147-148). The gap is constitutive, not a defect — it is what keeps the crossing from collapsing into fusion.
- **Felt** — the qing-suffix carries the register: chiasmqing is the *felt* crossing (the clasped hands, the handshake, the watcher feeling watched), not an abstract thesis about perception.

ANAKALYPSE note: the donor bundles *chiasme* (the crossing) and *la chair* (the element). The cathedral unfolds: chiasmqing names the crossing; the element rides within its Full sense; *écart* remains on the donor-shelf for a future coinage if the gap-aspect needs its own word.

Pairs with cathedral's:

- **Daseinqing** (self-side being-there) and **visageqing** (other-side ethical summons) — chiasmqing is the third vertex: the body-world crossing beneath both. Phase 5.1's phenomenological triangle closes.
- **prehensionme** — universal one-directional feeling-into; chiasmqing names the two-way crossing prehension presupposes when the prehended can prehend back. Pair: act (prehensionme) + crossing (chiasmqing).

## Example usage

1. "Clasp your own hands and attend: the chiasmqing is right there — the touching hand touchable, the roles crossing without ever coinciding" — the paradigm-experience.
2. "Running a palm along the planed board, the carpenter feels the chiasmqing — to feel the grain is to be a feelable thing among things; her touching hand is as much wood-touched as wood-touching" — craft-perception (the standing crossing in all skilled touch; for the threshold moment when the material starts leading the work, see chiasance).
3. "Walking alone in the forest you feel seen by what you see; chiasmqing names the structure of that feeling — to be a seer is to stand as a visible thing among the visible" — eco-phenomenological application.
4. "Screen-mediated seeing thins the chiasmqing: I see without being see-able, and the flesh-bond starves" — media-diagnosis.
5. "Cathedral-forging is itself chiasmic: the tradition we excavate excavates us; donor and forger cross without coinciding" — meta-cathedral reflexive.

## Not to be confused with

- **chiasance** (dictionary, creative-process register) — the same χ-root at event-scale in MAKING: the threshold moment when the maker crosses from directing the material to following it (the clay begins to pull the hands). Chiasance is an agency-handover that happens and is over; chiasmqing is the standing reversibility of perception that was already there before any making began. The carpenter has a chiasance when the grain starts leading the plane; she has chiasmqing the whole time her hand can feel the wood at all.
- **chiagnance** (primer perceptor) — a chiasance whose authorship is irreducibly indeterminate; same event-scale creative register, same distinction from chiasmqing as above.
- **chiasmance** (diplosemic grammar-mechanism, forge-pending) — the same χ-root at sentence-scale: ABBA cross-inversion in TEXT. The shared root is donor-faithful (Merleau-Ponty chose *chiasme* partly for the rhetorical figure chiasmance formalizes); the families hold the registers apart: -ance names the perceived text-structure, qing names the felt carnal bond. Crossing at two scales; one realm-feature; two words.
- **barzakhqing** — the closest structural neighbor in canon: dual poles simultaneously distinct and conjoined. But the barzakh is a mediating THIRD — an isthmus-interval between heterogeneous poles, with no role-reversal; chiasmqing has no third term — two folds of ONE element exchange roles around a pivot belonging to neither, and the écart is internal to the crossing, not an interval between strangers.
- **darshanqing** — mutual sacred-beholding between persons; intentional, devotional; chiasmqing is pre-personal and holds between hand and bark, body and tool.
- **prehensionme** — the universal one-directional act of feeling-into; chiasmqing is the two-way crossing, with the écart prehension does not name.
- **spandaqing** — felt bipolar pulsation of consciousness; chiasmqing's flesh is an element, neither mind nor matter; the écart blocks the monist reading.
- **English "reversibility"** — direction-neutral process-word; no flesh, no felt-crossing, no constitutive gap.
- **Mystical fusion / "we are one"** — the écart is constitutive; chiasmqing names crossing-without-coincidence, a "coinciding from afar"; fusion-claims erase exactly what the word keeps.
