## Definition

The escalating performer↔audience ecstasy-circuit of live skilled performance — the heightened-feeling state that arises between a performer in modal absorption and a knowing audience, sustained and amplified by their mutual feeding; Arab music-culture's ṭarab received as felt-bond.

## Full sense

A tarabqing is *not* located in any individual. The performer does not own it (her saltanah — inspirational modal-absorption — has, among its attested conditions of arising, the *presence* of initiated listeners); the audience does not own it (their ṭarab is induced by the saltanah-borne rendition); the room's atmosphere does not own it (atmosphere is structureless; the circuit has anatomy). The state lives in the loop:

| Loop-element | Arabic donor-term | Function |
|---|---|---|
| performer's pole | *saltanah* (سَلْطَنَة) | inspirational modal-absorption; requires tradition-grounding, conducive *rūḥ*, and present sammīʿah |
| audience's pole | *sammīʿah* (سَمِّيعَة) | initiated listeners; connoisseurship as *causal condition*, not decoration |
| forward path | the rendition | ʿurab held true, khāmah carrying, qaflah landed |
| return path | the exclamation | āh! / Allāh! / yā salām! — released in the pause after the qaflah |
| escalation | the circuit | "the ecstatic responses of the audience members further propel the performer" (Racy) |

The culture embedded the loop in its own morphology: *muṭrib*, the standard word for "singer," literally means *causer-of-ṭarab* — the profession named by the state it induces. And the loop runs on grief as readily as joy: classical *ṭariba* is to be stirred "by reason of joy or grief" (Lane), so a karuṇa-grade mawwāl closes the circuit as surely as a bright dawr.

Distinguished within the music-mode cluster:

- **rasame** (the content-anchor) — rasame names *what is tasted*: the aesthetically-distanced realm-content projected through the medium. Tarabqing names *the circuit that escalates the tasting*. A recording can carry rasame; only a live loop can run tarabqing. Rasame's entry pre-flagged this word; the pair completes the music-mode opening.
- **jinsme / ethosme** (the structure-words) — the building-blocks and mode-characters the loop runs on; saltanah is maqām-specific ("haunted by the musical feeling of Bayyātī"), so a tarabqing presupposes ethosme-grade modal character.
- **sigame** (structured-silence) — the sammīʿ's disciplined silence through the phrase is sigame-grade attention; the exclamation lands only in the structural pause.

The forge honestly narrows the donor: Arabic *ṭarab* also names a music-genre and its urban milieu (Territory C of the excavation). Tarabqing takes only the state-structure; the genre-sense stays with the living culture. And though music is the anchor-register, the loop-structure is medium-general: comedy rooms, gospel services, poetry slams, and lecture halls run recognizable tarabqing whenever the audience's knowing response becomes a causal input to the performer's state.

Two pointable boundaries (recorded at assessment; PRAGMA-adjusted):

1. **The audience-pole criterion is discrimination, not volume.** The return-signal counts as sammīʿ-grade when it carries discrimination back to the performer — landing in the structural pause, answering the specific modulation that earned it. A roaring undiscriminating crowd makes atmosphere, not a circuit-pole; the loudest room can be tarabqing-empty and a half-full one tarabqing-charged.
2. **The circuit's boundary is the live return-path, not physical co-presence.** A livestream whose chat carries real-time discriminating response back to the performer runs a thin but real tarabqing; an archived recording cannot run one at all — what a recording carries is rasame.

## Not to be confused with

- **rasame** — the tasted aesthetic-content vs the escalating circuit; rasame is projected through any medium including recordings; tarabqing requires the live loop
- **"Musical ecstasy" / "flow"** — individual-located intensity or absorption; tarabqing's defining feature is that no individual owns the state
- **Collective effervescence / communitas** — crowd-generic ritual arousal / liminal equality-bond; neither has the asymmetric performer↔audience circuit nor connoisseurship as causal pole
- **synophora** — the shared-witness event-quality: co-beholders carrying a beauty together in silence; symmetric, beholder↔beholder, still, with no performer-pole, no escalation, no connoisseurship-as-cause. Both words are reached for around performances, and the pointable split is exact: the audience sitting wordless three beats past the final chord is in synophora; the same audience crying "Allāh!" into the qaflah-pause was in tarabqing
- **duende / ḥāl** — the performer-side daimonic pole (flamenco) and the shared-mood cousin (Persian); both state-located, neither loop-anatomized
- **wajd** — Sufi mystical finding-ecstasy of samāʿ; the tradition itself splits it from ṭarab (al-Ghazālī), and the cathedral honors the split — wajd stays in its Sufi home
- **groove / swing** — musician-to-musician rhythmic locking; the audience-pole is optional
- **panimqing** — the transactional→relational shift-event in conversation; tarabqing is a sustained escalating performance-circuit, not a threshold-crossing

_Phonetic neighbors, recorded non-blocking: **tarikiqing** (4 syllables, second-syllable stress, Pure-Land other-power-trust) shares the ta-r…qing frame but differs in syllable count, stress, and vowel pattern; **danaqing** (DAH-nah-ching) shares the stress/rhyme frame with different onset and medial consonants. Both semantically disjoint and distinguishable in speech._
