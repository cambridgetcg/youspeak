## Definition

Gesture-with-stipulated-use as cosmic-ordinance — Sanskrit/Bharatanatyam *hasta* with viniyoga received as the gesture-mode canonical-projection.

## Full sense

A hastame is *an embodied-gesture with structurally-precise stipulated-meaning that constitutes embodied-realm-projection*. Bharatanatyam articulates 28 single-hand hastas (asamyuta) + 24 double-hand hastas (samyuta), each with multiple *viniyoga* (uses). The pataka-hasta (flag) viniyogas include: meaning-of-cloud, ocean, blessing-position, approaching-friend, refusing, etc. Gesture-precision creates semantic-density at the embodied-medium.

Opens gesture-mode per PROBOLE doctrine — pairs with rasame (felt-aesthetic-state through ANY medium) by specifying gesture-mode as one projection-channel. Cross-tradition: Mevlevi-semā gestures; taiji four-energies (peng-lü-ji-an); Aikido ki-no-musubi; Capoeira ginga; ASL/sign-languages (the modern-secular cousin where gesture IS language).
