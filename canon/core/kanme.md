# kanme

## Definition

Best-approximation-along-projection-when-direct-extension-fails — the categorical-mathematical-mechanism that names what the cathedral does when forging cross-tradition compounds. Named from Kan extension (Daniel Kan, 1958; Mac Lane: "All concepts are Kan extensions") + Sumerian *me* (received-ordinance). When the cathedral extends a Sumerian me along a Mandarin qing, or projects a Greek noēma into the cathedral's vocabulary, the resulting compound IS a Kan extension — the canonical best-approximation along the projection-mapping.

## Full sense

Five convergent registers:

1. **The meta-cathedral entry.** Kanme names what the cathedral does at the categorical-mathematical level. Every forge that compounds two traditions is a Kan extension: the source tradition is extended along the projection-mapping (the semantic bridge between the traditions) to produce the best-approximation compound. The cathedral has been doing this since doxalgia (experiment 000); kanme makes the operation explicit.

2. **Left and right, the dual form.** Kan extensions come in left and right variants — the left adjoint (most free / least constrained) and the right adjoint (most cofree / most constrained). The cathedral's compounds exhibit both: some compounds lean toward the source tradition (left-Kan, the extension that preserves colimits), some toward the target (right-Kan, the extension that preserves limits). The dual form IS the word's coverage: kanme names both.

3. **"All concepts are Kan extensions."** Mac Lane's dictum (Categories for the Working Mathematician, §VII.6, attributed to Kan). Limits, colimits, products, exponentials, ends, coends — all are special cases of Kan extensions. If all concepts are Kan extensions, then all YOUSPEAK compounds are kanme-instances. The meta-cathedral claim is not that the cathedral uses category theory as a metaphor; it is that the cathedral's forge-operation IS the categorical construction, whether or not the forger knows the math.

4. **Received, not chosen.** The -me marks: the best-approximation is received-ordinance. The universal property of the Kan extension determines the best approximation uniquely (up to natural isomorphism). The cathedral does not choose how to translate between traditions; the universal property determines the translation, and the cathedral receives it. This is the deepest meaning of the -me suffix in this context: the cross-tradition translation is not invented but discovered, and the discovery is determined by structure.

5. **Honest about math-dominance.** The polyphone-balance is honestly 7.5: the entry is math-class dominant. The cross-tradition resonance (apophatic theology's best-approximation-to-the-inexpressible, Madhyamaka upāya as skillful-means-as-projection, Vedanta neti-neti as indirect-approximation) is real but secondary. The entry does not overclaim.

The learning-bridge: "Kan" is recognizable to anyone who has taken category theory (2 syllables — the most EUMATHE mathema-donor entry in the cathedral).
