# pime

the irreducibly-irrational-and-everywhere quality of realm-features that no projection-system
can fully discharge but every sufficient cognizer encounters from many angles. **The cathedral's

## Full sense

Pime names a *grade* of truth, not a particular truth. The grade is characterized by:

1. **Structural-mathematical-invariance**: the truth has the form of a mathematical structure
   that is consistent across all valid reasoning. π is the paradigm: every cognizer reasoning
   about flat-Euclidean geometry arrives at the same value of π.

2. **Irrationality and transcendence**: the truth cannot be discharged as a finite-rational
   description. π's decimal expansion 3.14159... continues without repetition; no finite
   sequence of ratios captures it. The truth EXCEEDS rational projection in a precise
   mathematical sense.

3. **Multi-angle ubiquity**: the truth appears from many independent angles. π is not only a
   geometric constant; it appears in probability (Gaussian normalization), number theory
   (Basel problem), wave mechanics, complex analysis (Euler's identity), statistics, and
   countless other contexts. The truth is "everywhere" structurally.

4. **Cross-civilization-discovered**: independent civilizations arrived at the same truth. π
   has been computed independently by Babylonian, Egyptian, Greek, Chinese, Indian, Arabic-
   Islamic, and modern multinational mathematical traditions. No single tradition owns it.

5. **Cross-cognition-class-decodable**: any cognizer (human, machine, ETI) that can recognize
   the appropriate symbols decodes pime-grade truths without prior linguistic agreement. π is
   the canonical SETI-handshake content for this reason.

6. **Received-as-cosmic-ordinance**: under THEOBASIS, the realm is GoD-grounded; pime-grade
   truths are recognized rather than invented; the recognition is witness-of-given.

Pime names this *grade*. Particular pime-grade truths include π itself, e (Euler's number), φ
(the golden ratio), Euler's identity (e^(iπ) + 1 = 0), the prime-sequence, Kolmogorov complexity,
the Pythagorean theorem, the Gaussian distribution, the conservation laws of physics, the
fundamental constants. Each is a pime-grade truth; pime is the grade-class.

## Etymology

*pi-* from the Greek letter π (lowercase pi; Unicode U+03C0). The letter has served as the
mathematical symbol for the circumference-to-diameter ratio of a flat-Euclidean circle since
William Jones's 1706 *Synopsis Palmariorum Matheseos*; Leonhard Euler made the convention
standard in his 1736 *Mechanica* and 1748 *Introductio*. The choice of the Greek letter was
originally abbreviated from *περιφέρεια* (periphereia, "periphery / circumference") but its
modern function is as a *symbol* for the invariant itself, not as an abbreviation.

**The donor-status in pime is mathema-class, not archaeology-class.** The donor is the
mathematical invariant 3.14159..., which has been independently discovered across seven
civilizational mathematical traditions. The Greek letter π is the standardized notation by
which contemporary mathematics records the invariant; the invariant itself is donor-agnostic.
This distinction is important: pime is not "the Greek-letter-pi-as-Greek-tradition-content";
it is "the mathematical-invariant-pi as cross-civilization-discovered-cosmic-given."

See `mathema/pure/pi.md` for the full donor file with discovery-history, invariance-class, and
decoder-hints for EXTRUSION (human → non-human) and INDUCTION (non-human → human) transmission.
