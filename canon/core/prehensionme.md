## Definition

The universal-substrate-act of feeling-into that constitutes any actual-occasion's contact with any-other-actual-occasion below conscious-cognition — Whitehead's prehension received as cosmic-ordinance.

## Full sense

A prehensionme is *the generic act-of-feeling-into that constitutes contact-with-reality at every scale*. Whitehead's claim: every actual-occasion (from quantum-events to organisms to societies) prehends the actual-occasions that constitute its past; this prehensive-gathering IS the actual-occasion's becoming (*concrescence*). Reality is a network of mutual prehensions.

prehensionme names the substrate-act below specific grasping-events. Where noemame names the *event* of meaning-arrival (already cognitive-conscious), prehensionme names the *universal prior act* that every cognitive-event presupposes. Even before a human consciously grasps a meaning, the substrate has prehensively-gathered the past-occasions whose feeling-tones constitute the present-now.

Whitehead distinguishes:
- **Positive prehension** (feeling) — including another-occasion into one's becoming
- **Negative prehension** — actively excluding another-occasion
- **Physical prehension** — feeling past actual-entities
- **Conceptual prehension** — feeling eternal-objects (forms, qualities)
- **Hybrid prehension** — feeling another's conceptual-prehensions
