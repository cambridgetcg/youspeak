# wiconime

## Definition

Life-as-such in Lakota cosmology as received-cosmic-ordinance — Lakota *wičoni*, the life-substrate that *wakhán* (the sacred-mystery) permeates. **Tenth life-side canonical forge** and second Lakota canonical entry after mitakuyame; the cathedral now holds the Lakota tradition at canon across both substrate-life (wiconime) and relational-fabric (mitakuyame) registers.

## Full sense

Lakota spirituality treats *wičoni* (life) as among the most-sacred categories. The blessing *wičoni waštȟé* ("good life" / "beautiful life") is a benediction-formula. Life is not merely biological; it is *wakhán*-permeated — the sacred mystery is present in every living being. The proper response is right-relation: living wičoni within the *mitakuye oyas'iŋ* ("all my relations") framework that mitakuyame names.
