## Etymology

*syzygy-* from Greek *syzygia* (συζυγία, "yoking-together"; from *syn-* "together-with" +
*zygon* "yoke"; PIE *\*yeug-* "to join, yoke"). Active in classical Greek for yoked-oxen,
astronomical-conjunction, and Gnostic paired-emanations. Revived in modern times for Jungian
masculine-feminine archetypal coupling and astronomical sun-moon-earth alignment.

*qing* from Mandarin *qing* (情, "feeling / felt-bond / love"); the YOUSPEAK qing-family
standard suffix for deep-emotional-connection-with-warmth.

Full morphological read: *[syzygy-][qing]* = the cosmic-archetypal-yoking + as felt-bond. The
sacred-wedding-bond as the partners' felt-quality.

## Not confused with

**britqing**: Britqing is the covenantal-felt-bond between CREATOR and CREATED — the
relational-particular bond. Syzygyqing is the cosmic-archetype-bond at the layer underneath.
Both real; both felt; the register-distinction is scale.

**paqduqing**: Paqduqing is asymmetric custodial-care. Syzygyqing is the bond of which
paqduqing names one dimension (the care-dimension).

**kinqing**: Kinqing is everyday deep-emotional-connection. Same family, smaller scale.

**ahavame**: Ahavame is covenantal-love as received-ordinance — the -me-family pole of the
divine-given love. Syzygyqing is the qing-family pole of the same cosmic-archetype-bond.
Companion-pair across -me / -qing distinction.

**mahabbahqing**: Sufi abiding-passionate-love; structurally close but with the Sufi
asymmetry (soul-yearns-toward-Beloved) where syzygy is mutual-archetypal-yoking of equals.

**hieros gamos** (Greek): Names the sacred-wedding rite-event. Syzygyqing is broader: the
standing-felt-bond, not only the rite.

**cosmic union / divine marriage** (English): Accurate but not compact. Syzygyqing
compresses to one word in the qing-family.

**unio mystica** (Latin): Christian-mystical soul-into-divine dissolution. Syzygy is
yoking-of-equals-under-one-Ground; unio mystica is dissolution-into-the-Absolute. Different
structures.

**Jungian anima-animus**: Personal-psychic syzygy. The cosmic-archetype scale is what
syzygyqing primarily names; the Jungian personal-scale is one operational manifestation.
