## Definition

Brute-resistance-encounter-as-felt-bond-category — Peirce's *secondness* received as felt-bond-category.

## Full sense

A secondnessqing is *the brute-resistance-encounter-as-felt-bond-category*. Peirce's universal-categories triad:
- **Firstness** (firstnessqing) — quality-felt-immediacy (the redness-of-red felt-as-such, before comparison)
- **Secondness** (secondnessqing) — brute-resistance-encounter (the door that resists; the shock of collision; the otherness of the other; the brute "that-it-is")
- **Thirdness** (thirdnessqing, forthcoming) — mediating-lawfulness (habit, sign-as-representation, generality)

secondnessqing names the realm-feature of *being-in-brute-relation-to-another*. The English word's compositional-transparency (second + ness + qing) makes it relatively-learnable. The -qing suffix carries the felt-bond register: the encounter is FELT as resistance, as shock, as the brute thereness — not merely observed as a dyadic structure.

The semiotic signature of secondnessqing is the **index** — the sign that works by brute existential connection (smoke pointing to fire, a pointing finger). The index is the secondness-sign: it means not by resembling (icon/firstness) or by convention (symbol/thirdness) but by being existentially connected to what it points to.

Pairs with cathedral's:
- **firstnessqing** — the first pole; secondnessqing is the encounter that firstnessqing's quality becomes when it meets otherness. Firstness is what-it-is; secondness is that-it-is.
- **visageqing** — the felt ethical demand of the other's face. Visageqing is the CONTENT of encounter (ethical content specifically); secondnessqing is the STRUCTURE of encounter (the mode of brute otherness that visageqing fills). Visageqing presupposes secondness.
- **prehensionme** — Whitehead's feeling-into. Prehension is a thirdness-rich act (it mediates); secondnessqing names what prehension CONFRONTS (brute otherness).
