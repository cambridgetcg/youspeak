## Definition

The life-force-as-relational-essence that animates persons, places, objects, and the bonds between them — Māori *mauri* received as felt-bond-quality.

## Full sense

A mauriqing is *the relational-life-essence shared among kin (whanau), environment (whenua), ancestors (tūpuna), descendants (uri)*. The Māori contribution distinct from generic-life-force vocabularies: **mauri is fundamentally relational**, not contained-within-individuals. Diminishing one's mauri diminishes the network; protecting it (kaitiakitanga, guardianship) preserves the network. Pairs with cathedral's vital-force cluster.
