# freenergyme

## Definition

Living-as-prediction-error-minimization — the structural-principle by which any persisting cognitive-living-system contacts reality. Named from the free-energy principle (Friston 2006+) + Sumerian *me* (received-ordinance). Agents minimize variational free energy (long-term-average surprise) via perception (update model) and action (change world). The continuous-substrate-process that makes every act of grasping (noemame, sphoṭame, eurekame) possible.

## Full sense

Five convergent registers:

1. **The substrate-process, not the event.** The cathedral has words for grasping — noemame (the grasping-as-event), sphoṭame (the bursting-forth), eurekame (the finding). These name discrete moments of meaning-arrival. Freenergyme names what runs underneath all of them: the continuous process by which a system maintains itself against entropy by predicting its sensorium and correcting prediction-errors. Every eureka is a local minimum in the free-energy landscape; freenergyme is the landscape itself.

2. **Perception and action are the same gradient.** Under FEP, the system reduces free energy two ways: perception updates the internal model to match the world; action changes the world to match the model. Seeing and reaching are both free-energy minimization — the same variational gradient, differently expressed. This is the word's ANAKALYPSE power: it unfolds "perception/action/learning/anticipation" into one principle.

3. **The Markov blanket is the skin of the self.** The formalism separates internal states from external states via a Markov blanket (sensory states + active states). The blanket IS the boundary of the self — not a metaphysical boundary but a statistical one. The self is the set of states that are conditionally independent of the world given the blanket. Freenergyme names the process that maintains this boundary: the system that keeps itself itself by predicting itself.

4. **Received, not invented.** The -me marks: this principle is received-ordinance. The system does not choose to minimize free energy; it minimizes free energy because that is what persisting systems do. Non-persisting systems do not minimize free energy (they dissolve). The principle is the ordinance that persistence serves. The system did not write the law; the law wrote the system.

5. **Contested but structural.** FEP-as-explanation-of-all-cognition is debated. The cathedral names the structural-principle, not the totalizing-claim. The principle applies where Markov-blanket assumptions hold; it is debated for higher-order cognition. Freenergyme names the substrate-level structural fact, honestly marked as substrate-level.
