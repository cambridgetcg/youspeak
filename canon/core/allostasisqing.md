# allostasisqing

## Definition

Stability-through-anticipatory-change — the principle that living systems maintain stability not by holding fixed setpoints but by predictively pre-adjusting to meet anticipated demand. Named from allostasis (Sterling & Eyer 1988) + Mandarin *qing* (felt-quality). The physiological register of prediction-error-minimization: the body FEELS what is coming and adjusts before it arrives. Stability achieved through change, not resistance to it.

## Full sense

Five convergent registers:

1. **Against homeostasis.** Homeostasis (Cannon 1929) models stability as holding a fixed setpoint: the body returns to 98.6°F after a perturbation. Allostasis (Sterling & Eyer 1988) models stability as predictive pre-adjustment: the body's setpoint is not fixed but moves to meet anticipated demand. Blood pressure rises before waking, not after. Cortisol rises before dawn, not after. The system is stable BECAUSE it changes, not despite changing.

2. **The felt-anticipation.** The -qing marks: the anticipatory-adjustment is a felt-register. The body does not compute the prediction abstractly; it FEELS what is coming. The athlete before the sprint, the speaker before the podium, the parent before the cry — the body adjusts before the event, and the adjustment is felt as arousal, readiness, the quality of anticipating. Allostasisqing names this felt-anticipation.

3. **Allostatic load.** The cost: chronic anticipatory adjustment wears the system. The body that is always bracing for the next demand pays the toll in cumulative wear — cardiovascular damage, immune suppression, metabolic dysregulation. Allostatic load is the price of stability-through-change. The word carries the cost honestly: allostasisqing is not free; the anticipatory adjustment is a loan against future resources, and the loan accrues interest.

4. **The FEP connection.** Allostasis is the physiological register of the free-energy principle (freenergyme). The system minimizes prediction-error by pre-adjusting to predicted states. Allostasisqing and freenergyme name the same principle from different vantages: freenergyme is the formal-structural register (variational free energy); allostasisqing is the physiological register (predictive pre-adjustment of setpoints). The two are kin — the mathema-formal and the biocog-physiological.

5. **Daoist resonance.** Wuwei (無為 — non-forcing, effortless action) is the tradition-resonance: the system that achieves stability by not resisting change, by flowing with the anticipated demand. Allostasisqing is the physiological version of wuwei: stability through yielding to what is coming, not through holding against it.

- **freenergyme** (the prediction-error-minimization) — freenergyme is the formal-structural register of the same principle; allostasisqing is the physiological register. The mathema-formal and the biocog-physiological — kin, not synonyms.
- **jiritsume** (self-attuned-cadence) — jiritsume is the rhythm that tunes itself from its own state; allostasisqing is the stability that maintains itself through anticipatory change. Both name self-regulating-systems, from different angles: the cadence and the pre-adjustment.
- **spandaqing** (consciousness-pulsation) — spandaqing is the felt-throb of aliveness; allostasisqing is the felt-anticipation that keeps the pulsation adaptive. The rhythm and the pre-adjustment.
- **homeostasis** (the fixed-setpoint model) — homeostasis is the model allostasisqing replaces. The two are not companions; they are rivals. Homeostasis holds; allostasisqing flows.
