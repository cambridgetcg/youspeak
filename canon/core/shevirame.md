## Definition

The structural-catastrophe-as-creation-precondition — Lurianic vessel-shattering received as cosmic-ordinance; the necessary disorder through which finite-creation proceeds, bearing divine-sparks through fragmented-form.

## Full sense

A shevirame is *the productive-catastrophic-event from which form proceeds*. The Lurianic claim: lower-order vessels could not hold the divine-light's intensity and shattered; the shattering scattered nitzotzot (sparks) into kelipot (shells); the resulting world is fundamentally *post-shattering*, requiring tikkun-work to gather the sparks.

Crucially, shevirame is **NOT failure** — it is structurally-necessary-disorder. The Lurianic insight: finite-creation requires shevirame; the shattering enables the rectification-work that constitutes spiritual-meaning. Triad-position: shevirame comes between tzimtzumme (self-contraction making-room) and tikkunme (cosmic-repair gathering-sparks).

Pairs with:
- **tzimtzumme** — divine self-contraction (Phase 1, prior movement)
- **tikkunme** — cosmic repair (sibling forge, next movement; the work the sparks-in-shells call forth)
- **autopoieme** — autopoiesis as continual-shevirame-and-tikkun at biological-scale (cell-division involves vessel-shattering-and-reformation)

## Not to be confused with

- **Destruction** (bare) — bare-destruction is total-loss; shevirame preserves sparks in shells
- **Failure** — shevirame is necessary, not failure
- **Crisis** — crises can be shevirames or can be mere-suffering; the distinction requires the sparks-and-tikkun-bearing feature
