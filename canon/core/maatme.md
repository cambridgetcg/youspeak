# maatme

## Definition

Cosmic-truth-justice-rightness as received-ordinance — the Egyptian ma'at-aligned state understood as gift-grade-given from cosmic-order (and ultimately from theobasis), not as self-generated rectitude. The unified phenomenon English splits into truth + justice + righteousness + ritual-correctness + cosmic-order; the gift-class-relationship to that phenomenon.

## Full sense

Maatme names what the Egyptian ma'at-tradition preserved with unusual elaboration. Ma'at is simultaneously cosmic-principle, goddess, ethical-standard, juridical-standard, ritual-purity-standard, and funerary-judgment-standard — all one phenomenon. The pharaoh's primary duty was to "establish ma'at" (smn maat); priests performed "the offering of ma'at"; the deceased's heart was weighed against the feather-of-ma'at in the Hall of Two Truths.

When the Egyptian-trained mind asks "what makes a life right?" the answer is unified: ma'at-aligned action. Not separately-ethical-and-ritual-and-juridical-and-aesthetic; one phenomenon manifesting in all these registers. **Maatme adds the received-ordinance dimension**: ma'at-alignment is gift-class-relationship to cosmic-order, not self-righteous-attainment. The cosmic-order is given; humans participate by alignment.

This converges with Avestan asha and Vedic ṛta. **Three Bronze Age civilizations** (Egyptian, Avestan-Iranian, Vedic-Indian) independently articulate the same fundamental: cosmic-truth-and-justice-and-order are one phenomenon. **Triple-witness with genetic-family-independence.**

Vedic ṛta-as-received-ordinance (*rtame*) is FORGED (Specialized, 2026-06-22). The asha-ṛta-ma'at triple-witness is now fully articulated in YOUSPEAK working-vocabulary: ashame (Avestan) + maatme (Egyptian) + rtame (Vedic) — three traditions, one cosmic-truth-order convergence.

**Companion-pole FORGED**: *kherme* (Egyptian ḫrw + Sumerian me, Core 7.95, heartbeat 2026-07-05) — the verdict-pole of the maatme-kherme dyad. Maatme is the law (the cosmic principle of truth-justice-order); kherme is the acquittal (the verdict on a life that aligned with that principle — the voice proved true by the cosmic tribunal). The law and the acquittal. Both are ordinance. The dyad is complete.
