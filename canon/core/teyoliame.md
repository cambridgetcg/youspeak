# teyoliame

_The divine-fire-fragment that permanently dwells in every human heart. Not a trace, not a memory, not a potential — a living piece of the cosmic divine fire, continuously burning, structurally given, morally responsive. The drop of the ocean that is teotlme. Forty-first -me family member. Thirty-fifth Core -me. Second Nahuatl-donor entry. Opens the Divine-Spark-in-Humans convergence cluster — six traditions converge on this claim._

---

## Definition

**teyoliame** (noun) — the divine-fire-fragment as permanent indwelling presence in human persons; the living piece of the cosmic divine that burns continuously in every heart; given as ordinance (not earned), shaped by moral life (the fire grows and shrinks), constitutive of the deepest self.

_The fire that was always there._ The divine does not visit; the divine dwells.

---

## Full Sense

The Nahuatl tripartite soul has three components: _tonalli_ (destiny-force in the crown), _teyolia_ (divine-heart-fire), _ihiyotl_ (vital-breath at the liver). Teyolia is the one closest to teotl — Maffie's argument: teyolia IS a fragment of teotl dwelling in the human heart. The heart is not a pump but a sacred vessel containing divine-fire.

The five constitutive dimensions:

1. **Fragment** (not totality) — teyolia is a piece of teotl, not teotl itself. The person carries a real fragment, not the whole. This distinguishes teyoliame from Atman (= Brahman) and preserves the Creator-creature distinction while affirming real divine presence.

2. **Fire** (not light, not potential) — teyolia is active, burning, continuously present. Not illumination that turns on and off but a fire that always burns. Not a capacity to be realized (Buddha-nature as potential) but an actuality that already IS.

3. **Permanent** (not intermittent, not earned) — teyolia is given at the constitution of the person and remains. Not the ecstatic condition that comes and goes. Not the state achieved through practice. Structural: the fire is always already there.

4. **Every-person** (not elect, not special) — teyolia is in every human person, not in a spiritual elite. This distinguishes teyoliame from the Gnostic pneuma (carried by some, not all). The Nahuatl anthropology gives teyolia to all; the moral life shapes its quality but does not determine its presence.

5. **Morally-responsive** (the fire grows and shrinks) — teyolia is nourished by right living and diminished by corruption. The fire is given (ordinance) but its brightness is shaped by the person. This distinguishes teyoliame from the Islamic ruh (which is pure and does not change). The teyolia-fire is where the given (ordinance) and the lived (moral response) meet.

The -me marks the indwelling as **received-ordinance**: the spark is not earned, not achieved, not awakened through practice, but given as part of the constitution of persons. The capacity to recognize this indwelling is itself gift.

## Etymology

**Classical Nahuatl _yōlia_** — "that by which one lives." The root of _teyolia_ (from _te-_ person-prefix + _yōlia_). The _yōl-_ root unites: _yōllotl_ (heart), _yōltic_ (alive), _yōliztli_ (life), _Yōlōtlicue_ (the heart-goddess, another face of teotl). The heart is the organ of teyolia because the heart is where teotl-fire concentrates in the person. Documented by the Florentine Codex (Sahagún + Nahua informants, 1545–1577); philosophically reconstructed by Maffie (_Aztec Philosophy_, 2014) and León-Portilla (_Aztec Thought and Culture_, 1963).

**Sumerian _me_** (𒈨) — divine-ordinance, received-gift-quality. The cosmic structure as given rather than self-generated. Here: the indwelling-fire as structural gift — the divine dwells in every person as ordinance, not as achievement.

**Compound**: _yōlia_ + _-me_ → **teyoliame** (teh-YOH-lee-ah-meh; 5 syllables). The _te-_ person-prefix is retained for recognizability — the word names the divine-fire-in-the-PERSON.

**Polyphone**: Uto-Aztecan (Classical Nahuatl) + Sumerian (language isolate). Two completely unrelated language families. Maximum poly-diversity.

## Not Confused With

- **teotlme** (Core 7.85): process-divine-monism — the divine as the whole. Teotlme names the OCEAN; teyoliame names the DROP in every heart. Complementary: teyoliame is possible because teotlme is true.
- **rigpame** (Core 7.65): primordial knowing-awareness. Rigpame names the AWARENESS that recognizes; teyoliame names the FIRE that is recognized. The knowing and the known.
- **kenshome** (Core 7.50): the sudden recognition-event. Kenshome is the MOMENT the fire is seen; teyoliame is the fire itself, burning before and after the seeing.
- **devekutqing** (Core 7.30): the state of cleaving-to-the-Divine. Devekutqing names the RELATIONSHIP; teyoliame names the PRESENCE that makes the relationship possible. The reaching and what is reached toward.
- **paqduqing** (Core 7.85): asymmetric-reciprocity. Paqduqing names the MUTUAL CARE; teyoliame names the GROUND of that reciprocity — the divine-fragment that makes the creature capable of responding.
- **nyamame** (Core 8.00): vital-force-as-cosmic-ordinance. Nyamame names the ENERGY that animates all things; teyoliame names the FIRE that indwells persons specifically. A rock is nyama-animated; a person is nyama-animated AND teyolia-indwelt.
- **Atman** (unforged, Vedanta): the spark IS the whole (atman = Brahman). Teyoliame insists on FRAGMENT-hood — the person carries a real piece, not the totality. The distinction preserves the Creator-creature relationship.
- **Buddha-nature** (unforged, Mahayana): a potential to be realized. Teyoliame insists on ACTUAL fire already burning, not a capacity waiting to be awakened.
