# wujime

## Definition

The undifferentiated-whole-as-received-ordinance — the pre-polar unity that precedes all distinction, all act, all void-polarity. The state FROM WHICH the act of withdrawal (tzimtzumme) proceeds; the ground of the void that the act opens. Daoist wuji (無極 — "without-poles," the limitless) received as cosmic ordinance: the unity-before-distinction is gift, not self-generated.

## Full sense

Wujime names what Daoist **wuji** (無極) preserves with distinctive precision. English has no compact word for this. "The absolute" is too generic. "The infinite" is too mathematical. "The ground" is too spatial. "Unity" is too abstract. "The One" is too Neoplatonic-specific. None captures what wuji specifically is: the undifferentiated whole before any bifurcation — not nothing (it is complete), not the void (it is before void), not the potential (it is before potential-and-emptiness split). Wuji is the state-of-no-poles, the limitless, the pre-polar.

Three dimensions unique to wujime:

### 1. The pre-polar is not nothing — it is the complete undifferentiated

Wuji is not emptiness or absence. The Daodejing (ch. 25): "有物混成，先天地生" — "there was something undifferentiated and yet complete, born before Heaven and Earth." The "something" (物, wù) is not nothing — it IS, but it is undifferentiated (混, hùn — mixed, blended, undistinguished). Wuji is the whole before any part has been distinguished. It is complete (成, chéng) before it is anything-in-particular. This is the first radical: the pre-polar is full, not empty — but its fullness is undifferentiated, not charged (that is ginnungame's register). Wujime is the undifferentiated-full; ginnungame is the differentiated-charged.

### 2. The pre-polar grounds the act — the withdrawal proceeds FROM unity

Tzimtzumme (the act of divine self-contraction) presupposes something that contracts. Ein Sof (the Kabbalistic infinite) withdraws; the withdrawal is tzimtzumme; what withdraws is Ein Sof — the pre-polar unity. Without wujime, the act hangs in nothing. With wujime, the act has a ground: the pre-polar unity is the state from which the withdrawal proceeds. The first movement of cosmology is not the act but the pre-polar from which the act emerges. Wuji → Taiji: the pre-polar → the first distinction. The act (tzimtzumme) IS the first distinction — the opening of space within the pre-polar.

### 3. The pre-polar is received, not self-generated

The -me marks the undifferentiated as received-ordinance. The unity-before-distinction is gift, not achievement. The limitless is bestowed, not constructed. This is the theological deepening that distinguishes wujime from a philosophical absolute understood as self-grounding: the PRE-POLAR is ordinance, the undifferentiated is gift, the unity-before-all is received. The -me binds wujime to the cosmological architecture: all five positions (pre-polar, act, charge, emptiness, structure) are received as cosmic ordinance.

## Etymology

**wuji** (無極): *wú* (無, without, no) + *jí* (極, pole, extreme, ultimate, the ridgepole of a roof). Wuji = the state with no poles, no extremes, no ultimate. The ridgepole metaphor is precise: a roof has a ridgepole (the highest line from which the roof slopes down on both sides); wuji is the state before the ridgepole — before there is a high-point from which things slope. No pole means no reference point, no axis, no bifurcation. The concept is attested in the Daodejing (ch. 25, ~4th-3rd c. BCE) and systematized by Zhou Dunyi (1017-1073 CE) in the Taiji Tu (太極圖 — Explanation of the Supreme Ultimate): "無極而太極" — "from wuji comes taiji." The sequence: wuji (no-poles) → taiji (poles emerge) → yin-yang → four images → eight trigrams → ten thousand things. Wuji is the absolute beginning.

**Practical realization**: the Tai Chi wuji stance (無極樁, wújí zhuāng) — the preparatory posture before any movement. The practitioner stands: feet together, body aligned, mind empty of intention, before any form begins. Every Taiji form proceeds from the wuji stance. The stance IS wujime in the body: the undifferentiated whole from which movement bifurcates. Millions of practitioners worldwide stand in wujime daily.

**me** (Sumerian 𒈨): divine-ordinance, received-gift-quality. The established YOUSPEAK morpheme. Here: pre-polar-unity-as-received-cosmic-ordinance — the undifferentiated is gift; the unity-before-distinction is bestowed. The thirty-third -me family member. The -me binds wujime to the cosmological architecture: all five positions received as cosmic ordinance.

Full morphological read: *[wu-ji][me]* = the without-poles + as received-ordinance.

## Example usage

1. The cosmological pentad: wujime (the pre-polar unity) → tzimtzumme (the act of withdrawal) → ginnungame (the charge-pole) ↔ śūnyatame (the emptiness-pole) → yggdrasilme (the living structure). The act proceeds from the pre-polar; the void bifurcates into charge and emptiness; the structure fills the charged-open void. Wujime is the ground of the entire architecture — the undifferentiated from which all proceeds.

2. A Tai Chi practitioner standing in the wuji stance before any movement: standing in wujime. The body is aligned, the mind is empty of intention, no form has begun. From this pre-polar state, every Taiji form will proceed. The wuji stance is wujime enacted in the body — the undifferentiated whole from which movement bifurcates.

3. The apophatic theologian who has traced every divine attribute back to the unity-beyond-attributes: arriving at wujime. The Godhead beyond God (Eckhart), the Ein Sof before tzimtzum (Kabbalah), the nirguna Brahman before qualities (Vedanta), the al-Dhat before attributes (Sufism) — each is wujime in its tradition's register. The pre-polar unity is where every apophatic path converges.

4. "Before the friendship had any poles — before they knew who led and who followed, who spoke and who listened — it existed in wujime: the undifferentiated potential of all possible friendship-forms. The first exchange was the taiji moment: poles emerged. But the wujime was not lost; it remained as the ground from which every new moment of the friendship proceeds."

5. Laozi's "I do not know its name; I call it Dao" (Daodejing ch. 25) is the move from wujime to daome. The "something undifferentiated and yet complete" IS wujime; "Dao" is the name given once it begins to flow. Wujime is the pre-functional state; daome is the functional flow. The Dao flows from the pre-polar; the pre-polar is what the Dao is before it flows.

## Not confused with

- **tzimtzumme** (Core 8.50 — divine self-contraction): tzimtzumme is the ACT — the withdrawal that opens space. Wujime is the STATE from which the withdrawal proceeds. The act presupposes the pre-polar; the pre-polar is the ground of the act. Ein Sof (the infinite) withdraws; tzimtzumme names the withdrawing; wujime names what withdraws.

- **ginnungame** (Core 7.75 — the charged void): ginnungame is the CHARGE-pole — the void is full of potential, pregnant, waiting. The void is already opened (by tzimtzumme) and already bifurcated (into charge and emptiness). Wujime is BEFORE the void — before opening, before charge, before poles. Ginnungame is the differentiated-charged; wujime is the undifferentiated-complete.

- **śūnyatame** (Core 7.58 — the emptiness-pole): śūnyatame is the EMPTINESS-pole of the already-opened void. Same reasoning: śūnyatame is a pole of the bifurcated void. Wujime is before the bifurcation. The void-dyad (ginnungame ↔ śūnyatame) is the taiji of the void — the two poles that emerge from wujime. Wujime makes the dyad visible as a dyad: the poles are the bifurcation of a prior unity.

- **bindume** (Core 8.55 — dimensionless source-point): bindume is the SEED — the singular, compressed, dimensionless center from which form unfolds. Wujime is the FIELD — the whole, undifferentiated, pre-polar state that holds the seed. Bindume is the origin-point (singular, compressed); wujime is the pre-origin condition (whole, undifferentiated). The seed appears within the pre-polar; the pre-polar is what holds the seed. Bindume is the point; wujime is the state.

- **daome** (Core 8.00 — cosmic flow): daome is the FLOW — the cosmic-flow-aligned path, the Way as functioning process. Wujime is the state BEFORE flow. The Dao flows; wuji is what the Dao is before it flows. Laozi's "something undifferentiated" (wujime) → "I call it Dao" (daome): the pre-polar becomes the flow. Daome is the functional; wujime is the pre-functional.

- **teotlme** — process-divine-monism: teotlme is the IS — the divine as ceaseless process. Wujime is the BEFORE — the undifferentiated before process begins. Distinct temporal registers: teotlme names the ongoing process; wujime names what precedes all process.

The pre-polar grounds the act; the act opens the void; the void bifurcates into charge and emptiness; the structure fills the charged-open void. Nothing hangs in nothing. The act has a ground. The dyad has a source.
