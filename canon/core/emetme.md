# emetme

## Definition

Comprehensive-truth-as-firm-foundation as received-ordinance — the Hebrew emet-aligned state understood as gift-grade-given. The Hebrew register of the cosmic-truth-order convergence; complementary to ashame (Avestan), maatme (Egyptian), rtame (Vedic).

## Full sense

Emetme names what the Hebrew emet-tradition preserves with distinctive theological-emphasis. Hebrew emet is from root ʾ-M-N (aleph-mem-nun; cognate of "amen") meaning "to support, sustain, be firm." Emet is the truth-as-firm-foundation specifically — what holds-up reality, what stays-stable-when-tested, what one can-build-life-upon.

The pictographic structure encodes the meaning: emet's three letters (aleph + mem + tav) span the Hebrew alphabet from first to middle to last. Rabbinic tradition reads this as "truth from beginning to middle to end" — comprehensive, end-to-end, gap-free. The truth-word's letters demonstrate truth's nature as comprehensive-and-spanning.

Emetme adds the received-ordinance grade: emet-aligned existence is gift-class-relationship to firm-truth, not self-generated rectitude. The firmness is given; humans align by trust-and-faithfulness.

This complements:
- **ashame** (Avestan) — cosmic-truth-with-cosmic-ethical-struggle (asha vs druj)
- **maatme** (Egyptian) — cosmic-truth-justice-rightness-fused; goddess-personified
- **rtame** (Vedic) — cosmic-path-the-gods-uphold

The four traditions together articulate the cosmic-truth-order register from four converging angles. Modernity splits truth + firmness + justice + righteousness + cosmic-order into separate domains; the four traditions hold them as one phenomenon viewed from different angles.
