# doxakallos

## Definition

The uncreated beauty-quality of GoD — the ontological beauty-in-itself of the divine, the beheld-pole whose rightful beholding produces doxalgia in the beholder.

## Full sense

Doxakallos names GoD's own beauty as an attribute of the divine Being, not as event, aspect, or beholder-reaction. It is the beauty that IS in GoD — anterior to any creature's beholding, uncreated, incomparable, yet ordered-to being beheld. The appearing of doxakallos in creation is **tajalliance** (the descending arc). The ascending return is **anakallance**. The rightness-made-visible of a created thing participating in doxakallos is **orthophanes**. The ache in the beholder who rightly beholds orthophanes-of-GoD is **doxalgia**. The unity that doxakallos names is manifest in three faces — **glorividence** (the creative-perception), **gloricedence** (the creative-withdrawal), **glorichorence** (the gathered-choral-overflow) — and by any other face yet to be named.

Doxakallos is the anchor. Every word in the Liturgy refers to it, as every ray refers to its source.

## Etymology

**δόξα (doxa)** + **κάλλος (kallos)**.

*Doxa*: Greek, "glory, weight, opinion." In pre-Septuagint Greek, *doxa* primarily meant opinion or seeming. The Septuagint translators of the Hebrew scriptures chose *doxa* as the standard rendering of Hebrew *kavod* (כָּבוֹד — weight, glory, manifested divine presence). This single lexical decision bound *doxa* to Hebrew weight-theology for the next two millennia. In Christian Greek *doxa* is: the manifested weight of divine presence, the visible glory of GoD, the quality that fills sacred space when GoD is present. *Doxa* inherits kavod.

*Kallos*: Greek, "beauty" as substantive noun (neuter, 3rd declension). From the adjective *kalos* (beautiful), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*kal-* (beautiful, fair). Kallos names beauty-as-quality, not beauty-of-X; it is the substantival name for the property of being beautiful.

*Doxakallos*: Greek-morphology compound, *doxa-* modifying *-kallos*, with *kallos* as the head-noun. Literally: "the beauty of doxa," "beauty-glory." Semantically: the beauty-quality specifically of GoD's manifested presence — the beauty-side of divine glory, distinguished from glory's other moments (weight, severity, holiness).

## Example usage

- *The Standing Liturgy is the search for doxakallos. Every beholding is a step toward the word; the word is the arrival.*

- *She had read Psalm 27:4 for forty years — "to behold the no'am of YHWH" — before she recognized what the verse was asking for. It was doxakallos, by another name. The recognition broke her, as doxakallos broke any beholder rightly ordered to it.*

- *Orthophanes is a ray; doxakallos is the sun. The rays are of the sun. The orthophanes of any creature, rightly beheld, opens into doxakallos.*

- *Augustine's Pulchritudo tam antiqua et tam nova and Diotima's auto to kalon and the Sufi's jamāl al-ḥaqq and Psalm 27's no'am YHWH — five traditions' circling-phrases. Doxakallos names their common centre.*

## Not to be confused with

- **doxalgia** — the ache in the beholder at beholding doxakallos. The two are a pair: *beholding doxakallos produces doxalgia in the beholder rightly ordered to see it.* Doxakallos is in GoD; doxalgia is in the beholder.

- **orthophanes** — the rightness-made-visible of any ordered thing. All orthophanes of GoD-ordered things participate in doxakallos; doxakallos is not exhausted by any of them. Orthophanes is how doxakallos appears; doxakallos is what orthophanes is-of, when the ordered thing is GoD.

- **tajalliance** — the event of doxakallos's self-disclosure into creation (the descending arc). Event, not quality. Doxakallos is the quality of which tajalliance is the event.

- **anakallance** — the ascending arc, creation's beauty returning to doxakallos.

- **glorividence, gloricedence, glorichorence** — three faces of doxakallos: the seeing-that-makes-real, the yielding-that-makes-room, the gathered-choral-overflow. Doxakallos is their unity; each of them is one mode-of-showing of the one beauty.

- **gloriandre** (DICTIONARY) — an earlier YOUSPEAK attempt at this same gap (status 3/7 qualities). Gloriandre settled the beauty-quality on the creature-side (generating beauty in-what-it-touches). Doxakallos keeps the beauty-in-GoD-side.

- **pulchritudo Dei** (Augustinian Latin phrase) — the same target, phrase-construction. Doxakallos is the single-word-substantive that Latin reached only by phrase.

- **jamāl** (Sufi Arabic) — the closest single-word cross-linguistic cognate. Jamāl is bundled with *jalāl* (divine majesty) as a dyad. Doxakallos is *not* dyadic; it is unitary. If *jamāl* is needed in English as a loan, it names the *intimacy-beauty-side* of divine self-presentation; doxakallos names the *beauty-in-itself-qua-ontological-attribute*.

- **tiferet** (Kabbalistic Hebrew) — a sefira-position; structural. Doxakallos is the quality that tiferet is one position within.

- **kavod** (Hebrew) — the manifested weight of divine presence. Kavod includes beauty as one moment; doxakallos *names that moment* as its own word.
