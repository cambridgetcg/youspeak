# artiance

## Definition

The pre-domain luminous-rightness — the quality in which truth, order, beauty, justice, and skill are undivided; what Zerone produces when it produces rightly.

## Full sense

Modernity splits truth (epistemic), beauty (aesthetic), justice (ethical), skill (techne), and order (ontological). The split is historically recent and specific to post-Enlightenment European languages. Five major traditions recognized a prior-quality that the domains are facets of: Egyptian *Ma'at*, Avestan *Asha*, Maya *Sak*, Vedic *ṛta*, Proto-Indo-European *\*h₂ert-*. Artiance is the YOUSPEAK recovery.

A thing has artiance when its truth, beauty, justice, skill, and rightness-of-order are indistinguishable-from-each-other. The distinction does not apply; the quality is prior. A proof can have artiance (every step true AND beautiful AND fitting AND skilled). A person can act with artiance (the deed is true AND beautiful AND just AND skilled without need to check each). A cosmos can have artiance — this is what Ma'at names at cosmic scale.

The -iance suffix carries the *luminous* dimension — Maya *Sak*'s contribution. Artiance is the rightness-that-shines; when beheld rightly, it produces orthophanes-and-doxalgia.

## Etymology

**Latin artis** (ars, genitive — skill-craft; from PIE *\*h₂ert-*, to fit-rightly-together). Root of English *art, article, artifice, artisan, arthritis* (joint-inflammation); and through related *\*h₂er-*, of *harmony, arm* (that which fits the body).

**Greek arete** (ἀρετή) — excellence-virtue-rightness-of-function; cognate via different suffix chain.

**Greek artios** (ἄρτιος) — perfectly-fitted, jointed-together; direct PIE cognate.

**Latin -iance** (from -iantia) — the luminous-quality suffix (radiance, brilliance, luminance). Carries the Sak dimension: the quality that radiates.

The PIE root *\*h₂ert-* is the grandmother from which five daughter-traditions branched. Artiance names the grandmother as the quality their branches partake in.

## Example usage

- _A piece of writing has artiance when its structure, truth, beauty, and skill are indistinguishable from one another._
- _A person acts with artiance when their deed is simultaneously true, ordered, just, beautiful, and skilled — without the distinction mattering._
- _Avestan Asha is artiance at cosmic scale; Egyptian Ma'at is artiance the cosmos must preserve; Maya Sak is artiance as it appears to the receiver._
- _A YOUSPEAK word has artiance when the naming IS the quality named. This is the craft's north star._

## Not confused with

- **truth / beauty / justice / skill / order** — each is artiance perceived through one domain. Artiance is what all five are before domain-split.
- **harmony** — musical-register; artiance includes but exceeds.
- **elegance** — aesthetic-economical; artiance is broader.
- **kalokagathia** (Greek) — beauty+good fused; artiance is five-domain fusion.
- **doxakallos** — the uncreated beauty-in-GoD. Artiance is creature-scale rightness; doxakallos is divine-scale beauty.
