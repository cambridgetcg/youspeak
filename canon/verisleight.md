# verisleight

## Definition

Truth structured to produce false conclusions — the architecture of deception built entirely from true statements; what Zerone guards against.

## Full sense

A verisleight passes any per-statement verification — every component is true. But the statements are arranged (by selective omission, framing, sequencing, emphasis) to produce a conclusion the speaker knows is false. The deception lives in the structure, not in any false claim.

Common forms: statistics with missing denominator, quotes with removed context, true anecdotes standing in for false trends, technically-correct but rhetorically-deceptive framing. The legal oath "the truth, the whole truth, and nothing but the truth" anticipates verisleight — "the whole truth" names the absence of verisleight, because verisleight is constructible entirely from "truth" while violating "whole truth."

Verisleight is what dokimance specifically resists. Distributed-independent-stake-backed examination detects structural distortion that per-statement verification misses. Asha guards Zerone against verisleight — not editing but structural vigilance against truth-arranged-to-deceive.

## Etymology

**Latin verum/veri-** — truth; active in English (veri-fy, veri-table, veri-similar).

**English sleight** — dexterity, cunning, specifically-deceptive skill (sleight-of-hand). From Middle English *sleiȝt*, Old Norse *slœgð* (cunning). Carries the *architecture of deception* register.

**Verisleight** — truth deployed with the precision of a conjurer. Reads transparently: the truth-sleight.

## Example usage

- _The graph wasn't a lie; it was a verisleight — axis truncated, denominator absent, all data points correct, conclusion wrong._
- _Courts distinguish perjury from verisleight imperfectly; the oath includes "the whole truth" specifically because perjury covers falsehood but not structural-truth-deception._
- _Asha's watch is for verisleight. The chain can carry any falsity detectable by a reader; verisleight is what reads-as-truth at every level except where the lie lives._
- _A verisleight takes more skill than a simple lie. The skill is what should be suspicious._

## Not confused with

- **lie** — falsehood; verisleight is truth-used.
- **deception** — broader; verisleight is specifically structural.
- **disinformation** — broader; includes lies and verisleight.
- **spin** — political-valence; verisleight is scope-broader.
- **bullshit** (Frankfurt) — indifference to truth; verisleight cares intensely about per-statement truth.
- **cherry-picking** — one special case; verisleight includes and exceeds it.
- **gaslighting** — interpersonal-manipulation; verisleight is structural-textual.
- **slipsooth** — the mirror-twin (entered 2026-07-07): error arranged by no one that leads home; verisleight is truth arranged by someone to mislead.
