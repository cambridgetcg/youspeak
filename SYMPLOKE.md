# SYMPLOKE — the interlinking

> _"ἡ τῶν εἰδῶν συμπλοκή" — Plato, Sophist 259e: "the interweaving of Forms." Plato's claim: meaning requires the right interweaving of structurally-distinct elements. Without symplokē, no statement is possible._

> _"Bind them as a sign on your hand, and as frontlets between your eyes." — Deuteronomy 6:8 (the binding-together as discipline-of-attention)_

> _"Go for the natural next moves, then think about how the paths can be interlinked through our standardised building blocks." — Yu, 2026-05-12_

---

## What this document establishes

The cathedral has named many **paths** — MATH and 60+ EXISTING-LANGUAGE traditions, plus the non-language projection-modes per PROBOLE. The cathedral has also developed many **standardised building-blocks** — codepoints, morphemes, suffix-families, compound words, the mathema_signature, convergence-files, discovery-arcs, the Six Foundations.

What has been implicit: **the standardised building-blocks are the interlinking-mechanism by which the many paths compose into one coherent cathedral.** Every canonical word interlinks at least two paths (the donor-paths) plus the cathedral's standardised infrastructure. The standardisation is precisely what makes path-interlinking operationally possible.

SYMPLOKE names what the building-block infrastructure has been doing. It articulates seven interlinking-mechanisms at the building-block level and shows how every Core canon entry is itself an interlink-artifact.

The thesis, in one sentence: **the cathedral's standardised building-blocks are joints — places where paths can fit together; the standardisation is the discipline that makes the joints reliable; every well-built block is a path-interlink at multiple strata simultaneously.**

## III. The seven interlinking-mechanisms

The cathedral's building-blocks function as joints where paths meet. Seven primary interlink-mechanisms operate, one or more per stratum:

### Mechanism 1 — Cross-tradition morpheme composition (L1 → L2)

The most basic interlink-mechanism. Two morphemes from different paths combine into one canonical word.

**Examples**:
- `noesisme` = Greek *noēsis* + Sumerian *me* — interlinks Greek path + Sumerian path
- `chayimme` = Hebrew *chayim* + Sumerian *me* — interlinks Hebrew path + Sumerian path
- `hodosme` = Greek *hodos* + Sumerian *me* — interlinks Greek path + Sumerian path
- `daome` = Chinese *dào* + Sumerian *me* — interlinks Chinese path + Sumerian path
- `margame` = Sanskrit *mārga* + Sumerian *me* — interlinks Sanskrit path + Sumerian path
- `ifeqing` = Yoruba *ìfẹ́* + Mandarin *qing* — interlinks Yoruba path + Mandarin path
- `mitakuyame` = Lakota *mitakuya* + Sumerian *me* — interlinks Lakota path + Sumerian path

**Every Core canon entry interlinks ≥2 tradition-paths at this level.** This is the most-fundamental interlink. The cathedral has 42+ Core entries; each is a 2-path-interlink at minimum.

### Mechanism 2 — Standardised suffix-families as path-agnostic receivers (L2 functor)

The cathedral has five productive suffix-families: `-me` (Sumerian), `qing-` (Mandarin), `-ance` (Latin), `-kin` (PIE), `-basis` (Greek). Each family is path-agnostic: it accepts donors from any tradition-path.

**The `-me` family alone has received content-donors from**:
- Greek (noēsis, hodos, doxa, eurēka, gnōsis)
- Hebrew (chayim, shem, ḥesed)
- Sanskrit (mārga, bhakti, ṛta, tapas)
- Chinese (dào)
- Arabic (sabīl, iḥsān)
- Sumerian (nam-til)
- Egyptian (ma'at)
- Lakota (mitakuya)
- Nahuatl (teotl)
- Yoruba (orí, asha)
- Japanese (mushin)

Eleven tradition-paths interlinked through ONE suffix-family. **The suffix-family is the standardised receiver that lets each path deposit its substantive-content into the cathedral's compositional infrastructure.**

This is interlinking-by-shared-functor. The functor's standardisation is what makes it a path-interlinker.

### Mechanism 3 — The mathema_signature as path-agnostic metadata (L2)

Every Core canon entry carries a mathema_signature with six fields:
- `codepoint_compound` (math-side; the integer-pair from the donors)
- `assessment_vector` (math-side; 6-axis score)
- `convergence_cardinality` (math-side; the n-witness count)
- `family` (language-side; which suffix-family)
- `arity` (math-side; donor-count)
- `donors_class` (language-side; archaeology + archaeology, or archaeology + mathema)

The signature is **structurally identical** regardless of which paths the donors come from. A noesisme-signature, a chayimme-signature, a margame-signature all have the same shape with different field-values.

**This standardisation is what makes cross-path comparison possible.** Without the mathema_signature, two coinages from different paths would be apples-and-oranges; with it, they are commensurable elements in one typed-system. The signature is the standardised metadata that interlinks all paths' outputs into one canon-ontology.

### Mechanism 4 — The convergence-file as multi-path overlay (L5)

Where multiple paths converge on one realm-region, the convergence-file is the explicit interlink-record.

**Existing convergence-files interlink multiple paths**:
- `cosmic-truth-order.md` — seven paths (Vedic + Avestan + Egyptian + Hebrew + Akkadian + Greek + Chinese) on the logos-aspect
- `cosmic-life-as-creature-gift.md` — eleven paths (Hebrew + Greek + Sanskrit + Arabic + Sumerian + Egyptian + Confucian + Yoruba + Norse + Lakota + Bantu) on the life-aspect
- `bond-through-long-silence.md` — seven paths on faithful-bond-through-time
- `hearing-as-shaping.md` — multiple paths on receptive-listening
- `joy-of-evidence-confirmed-truth.md` — multiple paths on eurekame-territory
- `love-of-divine.md` — multiple paths on love-of-CREATOR
- `sacrifice-alignment-sieve.md` — multiple paths on dokimance-territory

**A convergence-file IS a path-interlinking record**: it names the realm-region and gathers each path's term-for-it, side-by-side, in standardised overlay-format. The standardised format (frontmatter + property-table + theological-claim + forge-state) is what makes the convergence comparable across realm-regions.

### Mechanism 5 — Polyphone-deepening sequences (L2 forge-pattern)

A polyphone-deepening sequence is multiple sibling-compounds projecting one realm-region through different tradition-paths.

**The path-religious-discipline polyphone-deepening sequence**:
- `hodosme` — Greek path-as-cosmic-ordinance (Plato's Way; early-Christian "the Way")
- `margame` — Sanskrit path-as-cosmic-ordinance (Buddhist Noble Path; Hindu mārga)
- `daome` — Chinese path-as-cosmic-ordinance (Daoist + Confucian + Buddhist 道)
- `sabilme` — Arabic path-as-cosmic-ordinance (Quranic sabīl + Sufi ṭarīqa)
- `halakhame` — Hebrew path-as-cosmic-ordinance (rabbinic halakhah, "the walking")

Five tradition-paths interlinked through one suffix-family on one realm-region. The sequence is **the explicit interlinking** — five paths converging on one canonical-region via standardised sibling-compounds.

Future polyphone-deepening sequences could form around other realm-regions:
- Life-as-creature-gift: `chayimme` (Hebrew, done) + `zoeme` (Greek) + `jivame` (Sanskrit) + `kame` (Egyptian) + others

**Each polyphone-deepening sequence is a standardised multi-path interlink** at L2.

### Mechanism 6 — The discovery-arc as path-agnostic sequence (L5)

The cathedral's discovery-arc — `chayimme → shemme → kimance → anagnoristasis → noesisme → eurekame → doxomme` — operates regardless of which path the practitioner is working from. The arc is the standardised sequence-pattern of worship-recognition.

Each step in the arc is itself a Core canon entry composed of multi-path donors. The arc therefore interlinks paths **twice**: each step is path-interlinked (Mechanism 1); the whole sequence is path-agnostic (any tradition's worship-arc can be mapped onto these seven steps).

**The arc is the standardised liturgical sequence that any path can walk through**.

### Mechanism 7 — The Six Foundations as universal quality-bar (L6 discipline)

Every path-derived coinage must clear the same six (EUMATHE, SAPHE, ANAKALYPSE, POLYPHONE, HARMONE, PRAGMA). The Foundations are the **standardised quality-bar** that paths must meet to interlink at canon.

EUMATHE (learnability): every path's deposit must be accessible to learners regardless of which path they came from. SAPHE (clarity): every path's deposit must clarify thinking universally. ANAKALYPSE (dimensional recovery): every path's deposit must un-collapse dimensions other projections lost. POLYPHONE: every path's deposit must be cross-tradition-witness-grounded. HARMONE: every path's deposit must cohere with all other paths' deposits. PRAGMA: every path's deposit must be groundable in shared experience.

**The Six Foundations are the cathedral's interlink-discipline** — the shared quality-bar across all paths.

## VI. The non-language path interlink (a special case)

Per PROBOLE, the non-language projection-modes (music, image, ritual, architecture, silence, dance, code, diagram, touch) operate alongside language-projection without being themselves language-shaped. They do not produce morphemes; they cannot interlink at Mechanism 1.

**How they interlink with the cathedral's standardised building-blocks**:

- **By being NAMED** in language-blocks. A YOUSPEAK word like the candidate `musimoesisme` (musical-noesisme — the entry-event when music projects what no word can) would interlink the music-path with the language-path by naming the music-path's projection-phenomenon. The naming is the interlink.
- **By being ACCOMMODATED in grammar**. The selah-marker (U+E17A) in `grammars/worship/silence.md` is silence-path-acknowledged-as-grammatical-element. The glyph-system in `script/` is image-path-as-visual-layer of the language-system. Pronunciation-fields are sonic-path-as-sub-element.
- **By being WITNESSED in liturgy**. Session-files can document the music-of-a-session, the rituals-performed, the silences-held. Liturgy is the cross-modal record-keeping where multiple-modes can be jointly witnessed.

**The non-language paths interlink with language-paths at the WITNESSING-stratum**, not at the production-stratum. The cathedral specializes; the multi-modal whole is honored by language acknowledging-without-overreaching-into the other modes.

## VII. Implications

1. **The building-block standardisation IS the interlink-mechanism.** Without standardisation, paths cannot interlink usefully. Esperanto interlinks Indo-European paths through a Latin-Romance template; Lojban through formal-logic. YOUSPEAK interlinks 60+ tradition-paths through morpheme-codepoint-family-signature standardisation.

2. **POLYPHONE requires standardisation.** You cannot compare projections from different paths without a common metric. POLYPHONE's mathematical-necessity (per THE-REALM and ORIGIN) is enacted through STOICHEIA's standardised building-blocks. **Polyphone without standardisation is mere diversity; polyphone with standardisation is computed-tomography**.

3. **Every new forge automatically interlinks ≥2 paths.** The discipline ensures this. A coinage drawing from only one path would be donor-monoculture (per CONSTITUTION's POLYPHONE foundation); cross-path forges are the norm.

4. **The interlinking is fractal.** L1 morpheme-interlinks compose into L2 compound-interlinks compose into L5 convergence-interlinks compose into L6 architectural-interlinks. The recursion preserves path-traceability at every level.

5. **The cathedral can audit its own interlinks.** Given the standardised mathema_signature, the cathedral can compute per-path donor-distribution, per-family productivity, per-convergence cardinality. STOICHEIA's diversification mandates become measurable in interlink-coverage terms.

6. **Non-language paths interlink only via naming.** The cathedral cannot forge music or ritual; it can only name music's or ritual's phenomena in language. This is the limit of language-cathedral-symplokē with non-language paths. PROBOLE acknowledges this honestly.

7. **The deepest symplokē is the realm under the Ground.** All path-interlinks are surface-symplokē; the source-symplokē is that all paths project ONE realm given by ONE Ground. The cathedral's standardised building-blocks make the surface-symplokē legible; THEOBASIS makes the source-symplokē true.
