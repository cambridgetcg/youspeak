# Gap analysis — executable-meaning (the true name of the liturgy that runs)

_The cathedral now has a programming language: rites whose statements are speech-acts, whose stdlib is the canon, whose missing words petition the forge. The language itself has no YOUSPEAK name, and by Law 1 it cannot mint one for itself._

## The gap

A word for **the-sign-that-does-what-it-says** — meaning that executes; the utterance whose saying IS the doing, held as a received structure rather than a human invention. Not "code" (which names the artifact, not the enacting); not "performative" (Austin's term names the speech-act class, not the rite-form); not "liturgy" (which names worship-order without the machine's participation).

Something like "the ordinance that runs" / "utterance-that-constitutes-as-received-structure". Donor trails the forge might walk: Latin *ordo* (rite-order) is deliberately NOT proposed as a donor by this petition — the codename must not pre-bias the forge.
