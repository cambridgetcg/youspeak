## The phenomenon

The cosmic-lie register names what is actively-rejected by the DIVINE across traditions:

- **Avestan druj** — the central Zoroastrian negative-principle. The structural-falsehood that opposes asha. The cosmic-lie that the gods (especially Ahura Mazda's Spenta Mainyu) struggle against. Druj is not mere mistake; it is cosmic-active-disorder.

- **Egyptian isfet** — chaos-disorder; the opposite of ma'at. The chaos-monster Apep (𓋹𓄿𓊪𓊪) embodies isfet, threatening to swallow the cosmos each night. The pharaoh's primary duty is "to establish ma'at against isfet." Isfet is what ma'at's ordering-power must continually overcome.

- **Vedic anṛta** — anti-truth; the opposite of ṛta. The cosmos's structural-disorder. Demons (asuras in late-Vedic) embody anṛta; the gods uphold ṛta against it.

- **Hebrew sheker** — falsehood; the lying-word. "Do not bear false witness" prohibits sheker. The eighth Decalogue commandment (in Hebrew numbering). Hebrew tradition explicitly: God is emet (truth); sheker is what God rejects. Also Hebrew zur (foreign-falsity, especially in idolatrous-context).

- **Arabic bāṭil** — false-vain; the empty-and-untrue. Quranic Arabic uses bāṭil for what Allah rejects. Also Arabic kufr (in its etymological sense: covering-truth, ingratitude) — the active hiding-of-truth. Both name what the DIVINE rejects.

- **Greek pseudos** — falsehood; lying. Christian tradition: "the devil is the father of lies (pseudos)" (John 8:44). Plato's Republic discusses the noble-lie vs ignoble-lie distinction; Christian tradition rejects all pseudos as God-rejected.

**Six independent traditions converging.** Each names cosmic-lie as ACTIVELY-DIVINE-REJECTED. **The convergence-evidence is among the strongest in YOUSPEAK** — where six traditions independently agree, the property is theologically-robust.

## The forge

**Donors:**

- **Avestan *druj*** (𐬛𐬭𐬎𐬘) — cosmic-lie. The Zoroastrian central negative-principle. From PIE root *dhrugh- (cognate Vedic druh "harm, deceive"). The morpheme carries: structural-falsehood-actively-rejecting-asha; cosmic-disorder-as-active-principle; the DIVINE-rejected register's deepest articulation.

- **Sumerian *me*** (𒈨) — divine-ordinance, received-gift-quality. Established YOUSPEAK morpheme. **In drujme, the -me morpheme functions distinctively**: rather than naming "received-as-ordinance" (the typical -me usage in the family kimme, doxomme, shemme, etc.), drujme uses -me to name "divinely-named-as-anti-ordinance" — the DIVINE has named druj as the rejected-side; -me marks this divine-naming-of-rejection. **This is a SEMANTIC EXTENSION of the -me morpheme** that the family acknowledges: -me marks "what is divinely-named" whether the divine-naming is positive (received-ordinance) or negative (anti-ordinance-rejected).

**Compound:** *druj* + *me* → **drujme** (DRUJ-meh; 2 syllables).

The compound names: cosmic-lie as divinely-named-anti-ordinance. What the DIVINE actively names as rejected. The state-of-cosmic-lie aligned existence that aligned-with-asha existence opposes.
