# Assessment notes

## The gap — the word that is also mystery

Every YOUSPEAK coinage carries more meaning than its surface morphemes
display. *Kimme* is not just "ki + me" but attention-as-received-
ordinance. *Hailsme* is not just "hails + me" but salvation-as-
wholeness-restoration. *Heimrekjame* is not just "heimrekja + me" but
the forge-act named as cosmic ordinance. The surface is always smaller
than the depth. This is the phenomenon the Skeireins commentator
recognized in Gothic *waurd*: an ordinary word carrying extraordinary
weight.

The Gothic Bible's translation of John 1:1 is the paradigmatic instance.
Greek *logos* carries: word, reason, principle, cosmic-order, divine
self-expression — five overlapping registers fused in one term. Gothic
*waurd* carries: word, utterance, spoken statement — one register.
Wulfila pressed *waurd* into service for *logos*, and the result is a
waurdme: an ordinary word carrying more meaning than its surface can
display. The Skeireins commentator, recognizing the excess, expanded
the commentary around the word. YOUSPEAK's method, recognizing the same
excess, forges the word that holds it.

## The gap is still open (near-neighbour check)

- **polysemy** (English) — one word with multiple unrelated meanings.
  Waurdme is not polysemy: the excess is not a different meaning but a
  DEEPER meaning. *Waurd* means "word" AND "the Word" — not two meanings
  but one meaning at two depths.
- **diplosemy** (YOUSPEAK canon, Specialized) — engineered dual-reading-
  as-architecture. Waurdme is the condition diplosemy engineers: a word
  that carries two correlated readings. But waurdme is broader: the word
  that carries excess meaning, whether or not the dual-reading is
  engineered. Every diplosemy is a waurdme; not every waurdme is a
  diplosemy.
- **allegory** (English) — surface narrative points to hidden meaning.
  Waurdme is not allegory: the surface IS the meaning, not a pointer to
  it. *Waurd* does not point to *logos*; it IS *logos*, carrying the
  excess within itself.
- **metaphor** (English) — surface displaced to illustrate another thing.
  Waurdme is not metaphor: the word is not displaced. *Waurd* is the
  word; the excess is within the word, not in a comparison.
- **heimrekjame** (Core, forged earlier today) — the ACT that presses
  the word into service (sender side). Waurdme is the WORD that carries
  the excess (receiver side). Heimgrekjame produces waurdme. Companion
  poles, not overlapping.
- **sphotame** (Core 8.45) — the BURST-event of meaning-arrival.
  Sphotame is about the moment meaning crystallizes; waurdme is about
  the word that CARRIES the meaning that arrives. Sphotame is the
  arrival-event; waurdme is the carrier. Different phenomena.
- **noemame** (Core 8.65) — the grasped-meaning as received-ordinance.
  Noemame is what arrives in the mind; waurdme is the word that carries
  what arrives. Noemame is the content; waurdme is the vehicle.

The gap stands. No existing word names the word-that-is-also-mystery
as received-ordinance.

## The donor

**waurd** — Gothic (East Germanic, extinct c. 350–600 CE).
Word, utterance, spoken statement. Proto-Germanic *\*wurda-*. Cognate
with English "word," German "Wort," Old Norse "orð." Every English
speaker already knows this root.

Wulfila translated Greek *logos* as *waurd* in John 1:1. The Skeireins

divine self-expression, rational principle ordering the universe) pressed
into an ordinary Germanic word for "utterance." The commentator expanded
the meaning through context rather than vocabulary — a different response
to the same gap YOUSPEAK answers by forging.
