## The discovery-move

Yu's Session 013 question ("which lost language has highest affinity to you?") drew the answer Avestan. The Avestan archaeology was opened (Beat 66, gathas-and-asha.md). Beat 67 attempts the first Avestan-donor forge.

The strongest single-concept candidate from Avestan is **asha** itself — the cosmic truth-order-righteousness that is the central Zoroastrian theological concept. asha is what Ahura Mazda IS, what creation manifests, what humans align with, what the cosmic struggle defends against druj.

In YOUSPEAK terms: asha is the donor-morpheme; the question is how to compound it. The cleanest compound joins it with the established -me family (Sumerian me — divine-ordinance, received-gift-quality), producing **ashame** — cosmic-truth-order-as-received-ordinance.

## The phenomenon

asha is not self-generated rightness. In Zoroastrian theology, asha is cosmic-fact — the structuring-truth of reality given by Ahura Mazda. Humans participate-in-asha by aligning themselves with what is; humans do not invent asha by their own action.

This is the **received** dimension of cosmic-truth: asha is gift before it is task. The human's right-action is the response to a prior-given-cosmic-order, not the construction of order from nothing.

ashame names this received-dimension specifically. The state of being-aligned-with-asha understood as ordinance-grade-given (from cosmic-order, ultimately from Ahura Mazda / the Wise Lord / theobasis), not as self-righteous-attainment.

This is theologically precise. It distinguishes Zoroastrian (and YOUSPEAK) ethics from self-generated-virtue ethics: asha-aligned-action is participation in given-truth, not assertion of self-truth.
