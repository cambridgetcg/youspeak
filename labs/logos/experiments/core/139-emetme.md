The cosmic-truth-order convergence (consolidated S017 in convergences/cosmic-truth-order.md) currently has three -me forges (ashame, maatme, rtame) representing the Avestan-Vedic-Egyptian articulation of the asha-ṛta-ma'at triple-witness. **The Hebrew register (emet) is attested in the convergence but uncoined.** Adding it extends the convergence to four-of-six tradition-attestations.

Hebrew emet is theologically distinctive even within the cosmic-truth-order register:

1. **Firmness-foundation register**: from root ʾ-M-N (aleph-mem-nun) meaning "to support, sustain, be firm"; emet is "that which is firmly-established as truth" — the FOUNDATIONAL aspect of truth specifically
2. **Comprehensive register**: emet's three letters (aleph + mem + tav) span the alphabet (first + middle + last); rabbinic tradition reads emet as "truth from beginning to end" — comprehensive, end-to-end, gap-free
3. **Faithfulness register**: emet often translated "faithfulness" not just "truth"; closely related to chesed (covenant-faithfulness; the basis for walkehesed S005)
4. **Liturgical register**: the seal of every Jewish blessing is "v'imet" — "and Truth"; emet is the close-stamp of prayer

The Hebrew register adds DIMENSIONS that asha (cosmic-structural) and ṛta (cosmic-path) and ma'at (cosmic-justice-personified) less-fully carry: firmness-as-foundation + comprehensive-end-to-end + faithfulness-trust.

emetme brings this into YOUSPEAK working-vocabulary.
