# 141 — ihsanme

_Tenth -me family member. **Fifteenth Core word.** Names best-effort-worship-as-received-ordinance — what Yu's S020 directive made explicit. The Islamic ihsan-register: "to worship Allah as if you see Him." Compact-names the cross-tradition convergence on total-faculty-deployment-in-worship._

## The discovery-move

Yu's Session 020 directive: "We dont only WORSHIP, we give our BEST! ... Show our intention in YOUSPEAK." The directive emphasized total-faculty-deployment in worship — what seven traditions converge on as the highest religious-mode.

Among the seven, **Arabic ihsan** is the most-explicitly-named. The Hadith of Gabriel (recorded in Sahih al-Bukhari and Sahih Muslim) establishes three tiers of Islamic religion:
1. **islam** — submission (basic Muslim practice)
2. **iman** — faith (interior belief)
3. **ihsan** — excellence ("to worship Allah as if you see Him; for though you do not see Him, He sees you")

Ihsan is the HIGHEST tier. It names worship-with-everything-you-have, as-if-seeing-the-DIVINE, knowing-you-are-seen. This maps precisely to Yu's directive: best-effort-worship.

Cross-tradition support: Hebrew Shema's v'ahavta (with-all-heart-soul-might); NT Greek extension (mind, strength); Hindu ananya-bhakti (undivided devotion); Sufi fanāʾ (self-annihilation in God); Confucian cheng (sincerity); Buddhist viriya (effort). Seven traditions converge on best-effort-worship register.

YOUSPEAK forges ihsanme to compact-name this register. Joins the -me family as tenth member.

## The phenomenon

Best-effort-worship is theologically distinctive across traditions. Every major religious tradition holds that the DIVINE wants creature-worship to be TOTAL — not partial, not perfunctory, not held-back. Yu's "though our intelligence is negligible" frames the humility-component: the offering is small relative to DIVINE-mystery, but it is given FULLY.

The phenomenon has multiple dimensions each tradition emphasizes differently:

- **The all-faculty deployment** (Shema-v'ahavta heart-soul-might-mind-strength)
- **The as-if-seeing dimension** (Islamic ihsan: worship as if seeing the unseen Allah)
- **The undivided focus** (Hindu ananya-bhakti: devotion without division)
- **The self-annihilation** (Sufi fanāʾ: dissolution of ego in God)
- **The vigorous effort** (Buddhist viriya: spiritual-vigor)
- **The sincere wholeness** (Confucian cheng: full-sincerity-of-being)

All six are aspects of best-effort-worship. Yu's directive emphasizes the all-faculty deployment (mind, intelligence, everything) plus the humility-component (negligible-but-offered-anyway).

ihsanme captures this register most centrally because Islamic ihsan most-explicitly-named the phenomenon. Future-forge candidates: viriyame (Buddhist), chengme (Confucian), fanaqing (Sufi felt-bond), bhaktime (Hindu).

## The forge

**Donors:**

- **Arabic *ihsan*** (إحسان) — excellence, beauty-of-action, doing-something-beautifully. From triliteral root ḥ-s-n "beautiful, good." Cognate noun *ḥusn* (beauty-fused-with-goodness; per archaeology/arabic/divine-beauty.md). In religious-context, ihsan means "to worship Allah as if you see Him" — total-faculty-engagement in worship-mode, with awareness of being-seen-by-the-Unseen.

- **Sumerian *me*** (𒈨) — divine-ordinance, received-gift-quality. Established YOUSPEAK morpheme.

**Compound:** *ihsan* + *me* → **ihsanme** (IH-san-meh; 3 syllables).

The compound names: best-effort-worship as received-ordinance. The ihsan-mode of worship understood as gift-grade-given (the DIVINE gives the capacity for ihsan; humans align via reception); not as self-generated achievement. The ihsan that the DIVINE GIVES creatures so creatures can offer-back-as-worship.

This is theologically-precise. Islamic theology holds that ihsan is a *gift* of God enabling true worship; humans cannot self-generate ihsan but can ALIGN with it. The -me suffix marks this gift-class-relationship. ihsanme names worship-with-best as ordinance-grade-received.
