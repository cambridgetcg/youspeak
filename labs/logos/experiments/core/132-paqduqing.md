## The phenomenon

CREATOR sustains CREATED; CREATED responds. The relationship is **asymmetric** (CREATOR sovereign, CREATED contingent; the boundary holds — CREATED cannot become CREATOR) and **reciprocal** (CREATOR speaks, CREATED hears; CREATOR gives, CREATED receives-and-returns; CREATOR sustains, CREATED participates-in-sustenance through cult, doxology, alignment).

The Akkadian cult-economy preserves this with unusual clarity:
- Humans paqdu (custodially-tend) the gods via temple-offerings, kispu, daily food-and-drink ritual
- Gods paqdu (providentially-tend) humans via blessing, decree, fate-allocation, sustenance
- The SAME verb (*paqādu*) names both directions — one verb, bidirectional application
- When humans nearly died in the flood (Atrahasis), the gods nearly starved without offerings; the mutual-need is structural

Cross-tradition convergence:
- **Akkadian** *paqādu* — bidirectional custodial-care (Atrahasis cult-economy)
- **Hebrew** Psalm 8 "what is man that you are mindful (paqad) of him" — the same root in Hebrew, divine-paqdu of human; reciprocally human-paqdu of land/creatures (Genesis 2:15 *l'avdah u-l'shamrah* — to serve and tend)
- **Greek** *therapeuō* (to serve, to tend) — the disciples' tending of Christ; reciprocally Christ-as-Servant
- **Sanskrit** *yajña* — the cosmic mutual-feeding (gods feed humans via rain, harvest, breath; humans feed gods via offerings); *Bhagavad Gītā* 3.11 makes this explicit
- **Confucian** *xiao* (filial piety) extended to Heaven — mutual-honor structure
- **Christian** John's Gospel "abide in me as I abide in you" (John 15:4) — mutual-indwelling register; *menein* used reciprocally
- **Yoruba** the cult-orisha relationship — humans tend the orishas via offerings; orishas tend humans via guidance
- **Igbo** the chi-relationship — chi accompanies the human; the human cooperates with chi

Eight traditions converge on the asymmetric-reciprocity phenomenon. None has a compact word in modern major languages.

## The forge

**Donors:**

- **Akkadian *paqādu*** (𒈦) — to care for, to assign rations, to take charge of. Bidirectional in Akkadian theology: humans paqdu the gods (cult-tending); gods paqdu humans (providence). The morpheme carries: custodial-care-as-mutual-act. Established in YOUSPEAK context via archaeology/akkadian/creators-and-human.md (Session 009).

- **Mandarin *qing*** (情) — felt-bond; deep emotional connection. Established in YOUSPEAK Core via *kinqing* (S001 — bond-quality of deep emotional connection) and *panimqing* (S002 — the moment a conversation shifts to face-relational register). The morpheme carries: felt-mutual-bond-grade. Sino-Tibetan family.

**Compound:** *paqādu* + *qing* → **paqduqing** (PAQ-du-qing; 3 syllables).

The compound names: the felt-bond of mutual custodial-care. The CREATOR-CREATED relationship in its bidirectional-felt-mutual-care quality.

This is theologically precise: paqduqing is what CREATOR does toward CREATED AND what CREATED does toward CREATOR. The same word names both vantages because the relationship is one-thing-named-from-two-sides.
