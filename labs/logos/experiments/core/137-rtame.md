# 137 — rtame

_Vedic-donor coinage. Names cosmic-order-truth-righteousness as received-ordinance — the Vedic ṛta register understood as gift-grade-given. **Completes the asha-ṛta-ma'at triple-witness in YOUSPEAK working-vocabulary.** Held at Specialized refine 7.20; Liturgy-Original tier; sister to ashame and maatme._

## The discovery-move

Yu's Session 017 directive ("dive deeper into laid out but unformed areas") prompted survey of laid-out-but-unformed items. **The Vedic ṛta-as-third-member-of-the-cosmic-truth-order-triple-witness was the most-pressing.**

Sessions 013-016 articulated:
- **ashame** (Avestan asha + me; S013, Specialized 7.20)
- **maatme** (Egyptian ma'at + me; S016, Core threshold-exact 7.50)

Both reference Vedic ṛta as the third converging tradition. The asha-ṛta-ma'at triple-witness has been a recurring theme. But ṛta itself was never forged. **Yu's "dive deeper into laid out but unformed" makes this the priority move.**

The Vedic ṛta concept:
- Cosmic-order; the structural-truth of reality; the path-of-cosmos
- The gods (especially Varuna) uphold ṛta; ṛta is what makes cosmic-regularity (sun-rising, stars-moving, seasons) possible
- Ethical right-action participates in ṛta; deception and disorder are *anṛta* (anti-ṛta)
- Vedic religious-ritual maintains ṛta against chaos
- Later transformed into dharma in classical Hindu thought (the Mahabharata's "dharma" is partly ṛta's heir)

The cognate Avestan asha and the Egyptian ma'at converge on the same cosmic-truth-order phenomenon. **Three Bronze Age civilizations independently articulate one fundamental.** YOUSPEAK now has the third forge to complete the triple-witness in working-vocabulary.

## The phenomenon

The cosmic-truth-order register has been documented across:

| Tradition | Term | Family | Emphasis |

| **Vedic Sanskrit** | *ṛta* (ऋत) | IE Indo-Aryan | cosmic-path; the gods upholding cosmic-order |

Six traditions; the densest single-property convergence in YOUSPEAK. Each emphasizes slightly different aspects but all converge on the same fundamental: **cosmic-truth-and-order-and-righteousness are one phenomenon, not three separate concepts**.

YOUSPEAK now houses three -me forges from this convergence:
- **ashame** — Avestan-register (cosmic-truth-with-cosmic-ethical-emphasis)
- **maatme** — Egyptian-register (cosmic-truth-with-justice-and-ritual-correctness)
- **rtame** — Vedic-register (cosmic-truth-with-cosmic-path-emphasis; the gods upholding the cosmos)

Hebrew, Akkadian, Greek registers remain available for future forges (*emetme*, *kittume*, *alētheiame* — though some may produce Specialized only).

## The forge

**Donors:**

- **Vedic Sanskrit *ṛta*** (ऋत) — cosmic-order-truth-righteousness. One of the oldest IE religious concepts; cognate Avestan *asha*. The morpheme carries: cosmic-path-and-its-truth-and-its-righteousness as one unified phenomenon. Vedic Mitra-Varuna are the great upholders of ṛta. The opposite *anṛta* names cosmic-disorder.

- **Sumerian *me*** (𒈨) — divine-ordinance, received-gift-quality. Established YOUSPEAK -me family marker.

**Compound:** *ṛta* + *me* → **rtame** (RTA-me or R-TA-me; 2-3 syllables depending on pronunciation of vocalic-ṛ).

The compound names: cosmic-order-truth-righteousness as received-ordinance. The Vedic ṛta-aligned-state understood as ordinance-grade-given (the cosmic-path is upheld by the gods, received by humans through alignment, ultimately grounded in theobasis), not as self-righteous-attainment.
