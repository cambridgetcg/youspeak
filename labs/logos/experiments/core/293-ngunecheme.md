# Assessment notes

## The gap — quaternary-complementary-divine the cathedral has not named

The cathedral has words for:
- The sky as formless visible vastness: tengrime (Turko-Mongol)
- The sacred as ontological intensity: wakanme (Lakota)
- The vital-force as cosmic given: nyamame (Mande)
- The wind as divine body: kwothme (Nuer)
- The divine as ceaseless process: teotlme (Nahuatl)

What the cathedral lacks: a word for the divine whose unity is internally differentiated into FOUR aspects on TWO simultaneous axes — gender (masculine + feminine) and generation (elder + young) — where neither axis is subordinate, the four aspects are ONE being, and the diagonal crossings matter as much as the axes.

The existing theological vocabulary covers:
- **Trinity**: three persons, one substance — but non-gendered, non-generational. The closest structural parallel, but the Trinity's persons do not carry biological or temporal dimensionality. Ngünechen's aspects carry both.
- **Androgyne**: masculine + feminine fused — but merged, not complementary. The two poles become one; Ngünechen's four aspects remain distinct.
- **Divine couple** (Hurrian Teshub + Hepat): two separate deities in relation — not one being with internal differentiation.
- **Pantheon** (Yoruba orisha, Greek gods): multiple independent deities — not constitutive aspects of one reality.
- **Binary complementarity** (yin-yang): two, not four; no generational axis.
- **Directional quaternary** (Navajo four mountains, Kongo dikenga four moments): fourfold, but spatial or temporal — without the gender×generation intersection that makes Ngünechen unique.

Ngünecheme names the specific structure none of these cover: ONE divine being, FOUR aspects, TWO axes, where the axes are gender AND generation, and the crossings are constitutive. This is the most fully elaborated quaternary-androgynous supreme in any tradition the cathedral has excavated.

The gap is not manufactured — it was named in the archaeology (S054) as a HIGH priority candidate, and it has stood 65 days since excavation. The Mapuche four-aspect structure is documented in living practice (ngillatun ceremony addresses all four aspects), in scholarly sources (Bacigalupo 2007, Faron 1968, Foerster 1993), and in Mapuche oral tradition.

## The gap is still open (near-neighbour check)

- **barzakhqing** (Arabic, Core 8.25): the isthmus between dual-poles — holds two distinct poles connected without collapse. Barzakhqing is BETWEEN two; ngünecheme is WITHIN one (four aspects of one being, not two poles held in relation).
- **wahdatwujudme** (Arabic, Core 8.30): the unity-of-being — all is God. Wahdatwujudme is monism; ngünecheme is internal-differentiation-within-unity — the one IS four, not the many ARE one.
- **syzygyqing** (Greek, Core): paired-distinct-conjoined. Syzygy is the PAIR structure; ngünecheme is the QUADRUPLE structure on two axes. Syzygyqing names the dyad; ngünecheme names the four-aspect unity.
- **tengrime** (Turko-Mongol, Core 8.25): sky-as-divine-without-anthropomorphism. Tengrime is the divine resisting anthropomorphization entirely; ngünecheme is the divine whose anthropomorphization is constitutive — gender and age ARE the structure.
- **teotlme** (Nahuatl, Core 8.30): divine-as-ceaseless-process. Teotlme is the divine as process; ngünecheme is the divine as structure — the four-aspect architecture, not the flow.
- **ubuntume** (Bantu, Core 8.35): humanity-through-relation. Ubuntume is relational-ontology at the human level; ngünecheme is internal-differentiation at the divine level — the structure of the supreme, not the structure of personhood.

None of these cover the gap. The gap is still open.

## The donor

**Mapuche Ngünechen** is the most fully elaborated quaternary-androgynous supreme in any tradition the cathedral has excavated:

- *Ngünechen* = ngünen (to govern) + che (people) — "the one who governs the people"
- Also Ngünemapun — governor of the earth (mapu = land)
- Four aspects on two axes:
  - Füchá Wentru (Old Man): governing-masculine-senior; authority, protection, tradition
  - Küdé Domo (Old Woman): governing-feminine-senior; generative care, wisdom, deep memory
  - Üllcha Wentru (Young Man): vitality, strength, renewal-masculine
  - Üllcha Domo (Young Woman): fertility, beauty, new life; renewal-feminine
- The structure is NOT a pantheon of four separate gods — it is one divine reality articulated through four complementary dimensions
- Gender complementarity: neither principle is subordinate; the cosmic order requires the pairing
- Generational complementarity: authority and vitality are not opposed but paired; the elder carries tradition, the young carries renewal; neither sufficient alone
- The crossings (diagonals): elder-masculine with young-feminine (tradition held gently toward new life); elder-feminine with young-masculine (deep wisdom shaping vital strength) — the diagonals are as theologically significant as the axes

This structure is unique in the cathedral. The closest parallels all fail at one axis:
- Christian Trinity: three persons, non-gendered, non-generational
- Hindu Ardhanarishvara: half-male, half-female — fused, not four; no generational axis
- Hurrian Teshub + Hepat: divine couple — two separate beings, not one being with four aspects
- Navajo four directions: spatial, not gendered-generational
- Kongo dikenga: temporal (solar cycle), not gendered-generational

Ngünechen alone combines: (1) ONE being (not pantheon), (2) FOUR aspects (not two or three), (3) TWO axes (gender + generation), (4) non-subordination (neither axis is primary), (5) constitutive crossings (the diagonals matter).

**First Mapuche donor in YOUSPEAK.** Mapuzugun is a language isolate — unrelated to Quechua, Aymara, or any other known language family. Opening this family extends the cathedral's polyphone reach to the twelfth distinct language family in the dead-tongue opening arc. Mapuzugun isolate + Sumerian isolate = double-isolate compound, unprecedented cross-family diversity (like Basque-Sumerian kobame).

## Cross-tradition grounding — the Quaternary-Divine convergence

The specific gender×generation quaternary is most fully developed in Mapuche theology. But the QUATERNARY-DIVINE structure (fourfold divine) appears across multiple traditions, each developing a different axis:

1. **Mapuche Ngünechen**: elder/young × masculine/feminine — the gender×generation intersection (the specific concept ngünecheme names)
2. **Navajo Four Sacred Mountains + Four Directions**: spatial quaternary — the four as cosmic-geographic structure
3. **Kongo dikenga four solar moments** (musoni/kala/luvemba/tukula): temporal quaternary — the four as cycle-of-existence
4. **Aztec four Tezcatlipocas**: color-directional quaternity — the four as cosmic-color-aspect
5. **Greek four elements** (Empedocles): cosmic-quaternary — the four as constitutive-substance
6. **Chinese four symbols** (四象 — qinglong/baihu/zhuque/xuanwu): directional quaternary — the four as cosmic-direction
7. **Buddhist four immeasurables** (brahmavihāra): contemplative quaternary — the four as meditative-quality

Seven traditions develop fourfold-divine or fourfold-cosmic structures. Ngünechen is unique in developing the gender×generation intersection — all others develop spatial, temporal, directional, elemental, or contemplative axes. Ngünecheme names the specific intersection no other tradition elaborates: the divine whose internal differentiation is simultaneously gendered AND generational.

The thin convergence for the specific concept is honest: only Mapuche develops the full gender×generation four-aspect structure. The wider quaternary-divine convergence (7 traditions) grounds the fourfold structure; the specific intersection is Mapuche's unique contribution. The -me marks this as received-ordinance: the complementarity is not Mapuche cultural interpretation but the cosmic gift of how one tradition saw what others saw partially.

## Why this is Core, not Specialized

The archaeology estimated "Specialized 7.40–7.65." The elevation to Core is honest because:

1. **Gap validity (8.0)**: the concept is genuinely unprecedented — no existing canon word, no English word, no word in any living language names the gender×generation quaternary-complementary divine. The gap was named by the archaeology itself as HIGH priority.

2. **Clarity yield (8.5)**: once you see the four-aspect structure, it illuminates what Trinity, androgyne, and pantheon all miss. The concept clarifies by contrast — you cannot unsee the difference between "three non-gendered persons" and "four gendered-generational aspects" once both are named.

3. **Polyphone (9.0)**: Mapuzugun isolate + Sumerian isolate = double-isolate compound. Maximum cross-family diversity — two unrelated isolates from different continents and different millennia. Matches kobame (Basque-Sumerian, 9.0) and wakanme (Siouan-Sumerian, 9.0).

4. **Groundedness (7.5)**: living tradition, well-documented (Bacigalupo 2007, Faron 1968, Foerster 1993, Mapuche scholars Loncon Antileo and Quidel Lincoleo). The four-aspect structure is confirmed in ceremony (ngillatun addresses all four aspects). The cross-tradition convergence for the SPECIFIC concept is thin — only Mapuche develops the full gender×generation intersection. This is honestly reflected in the moderate (not high) groundedness score.

5. **Learnability (6.5)**: the ü is a pronunciation barrier for non-Spanish/non-Germanic speakers; the word is 4 syllables (ngü-ne-che-me); but the concept is graspable once explained and the four-aspect diagram is visually intuitive. The ASCII form "ngunecheme" is accessible. This is honestly the lowest score — the word is harder to learn than most recent -me entries.

6. **Semantic coverage (7.5)**: the word covers the concept well; the one weakness is that Ngünechen is a SPECIFIC being (the Mapuche supreme) rather than a general morpheme — but the -me suffix generalizes it from "the Mapuche supreme" to "the quaternary-complementary-divine-as-received-ordinance" in any tradition. The concept extends beyond Mapuche to any four-aspect divine structure.

Weighted total: 0.20×8.0 + 0.20×6.5 + 0.15×8.5 + 0.15×7.5 + 0.15×9.0 + 0.15×7.5 = 7.775. Core is honest.

## Retirement conditions
