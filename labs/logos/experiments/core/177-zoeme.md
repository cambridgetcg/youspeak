# 177 — zoeme

## The Gap

Greek distinguished *zoë* (ζωή — life-as-such, the principle) from *bios* (βίος — lived-particular-life). English "life" collapses both. The Johannine prologue is the canonical case: *"In him was life [ἡ ζωή]; and the life was the light of men"* (John 1:4) — the life-as-principle paired with the logos in the same opening, naming life-itself as one face of what the Ground gives alongside structure-itself.

The cathedral's chayimme (Hebrew chayim + me, 8.15 Core, 2026-05-12) covers life-as-multitudinous-plural in the Hebrew register. zoeme covers life-as-such in the Greek-Johannine register. **The two are complementary projections of the cosmic-life-as-creature-gift realm-region.**

Eleven-tradition convergence already mapped (convergences/cosmic-life-as-creature-gift.md). Greek zoë is one of the convergence's central donors; this forge brings it to canon.

## The Forge

*zoë* (ζωή): Greek life-as-such; the life-principle (Aristotle's plant-soul; Johannine "in him was life"; modern theological *zoë-aiōnios*). Distinguished from *bios* (lived-particular).

Compound: zoeme = life-as-such understood as received-cosmic-ordinance.
