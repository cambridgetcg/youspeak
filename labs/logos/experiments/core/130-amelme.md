## The discovery-move

Yu directed (2026-04-25): "keep worshipping. Dive deep into Akkadian. Focus on relationship of the CREATORS and HUMAN."

The deepening (archaeology/akkadian/creators-and-human.md authored Beat 53) revealed eight load-bearing features of the Akkadian creator-human relationship, including the foundational claim that **humanity-itself is a decree of the divine assembly**. The Atrahasis creation-narrative makes this explicit: humans are made *ana ardūtim* ("into servitude"), with the very purpose-of-existence decreed by the gods. Humanity is a parsu / me — an ordinance — not a self-existing fact.

This is theologically distinct from:
- "Creature" (English) — too generic; lacks the decreed-by-assembly weight
- "Image of God" (Hebrew tselem; Christian inheritance) — single-creator framing
- "Creation" (English) — abstract; lacks the personal-ordinance register

The Akkadian register names: **humanity-as-decreed-ordinance, by an assembly, with purpose**. This is the relationship-of-creators-and-human at its most foundational claim.

## The forge

**Donors:**

- **Akkadian *amēlu* / *awīlu*** (man, person, human) — the most-frequent term for human-as-individual in Akkadian textual tradition. In the creation-formulae, amēlu is the term for what is decreed-into-existence ("primeval man" = *amēlu mahrû*). The morpheme carries: human-as-individual-and-class.

- **Sumerian *me*** (𒈨) — the divine-ordinance / received-gift-quality morpheme. Established in YOUSPEAK as the received-register marker (kimme, doxomme, shemme + agwame Specialized).

**Compound:** *amēlu* + *me* → **amelme** (A-mel-me; 3 syllables).

The compound reads as: humanity-as-received-ordinance. Not "the human who happens to exist" but "the human whose-existing-IS-an-ordinance-from-the-divine-assembly." Humanity itself, in its very being-here, is decree.

This is theologically load-bearing for creator-human relationship: humans cannot grasp their position-vis-à-vis-creators without first recognizing that their being-as-such is a decree-received. Amelme is the foundational creature-pole for the entire creator-human relationship-vocabulary.

Kimme, shemme, walkekin all name states-creatures-can-enter (and which secular speakers can recognize through their own analogous experience). Amelme names a CLAIM-about-what-humanity-is. The audience for this claim is narrower; the groundedness honestly scores 6 not 8.
