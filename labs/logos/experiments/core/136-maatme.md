## The discovery-move

Yu's Session 016 directive on pyramid-civilization-languages identified Egyptian as priority for depth-treatment (4500-year continuity; archetypal pyramid-tradition; under-served in current YOUSPEAK at design-glyph-grammar level). The deepening (Beat 80, archaeology/egyptian/hieroglyphs-and-mind.md) revealed **ma'at as theologically-central** — the cosmic-truth-justice-rightness concept that:

1. Is **cosmic principle** — the right-ordering of reality holding chaos (isfet) at bay
2. Is **goddess** — daughter of Ra, consort of Thoth, personification of truth
3. Is **ethical-ritual standard** — pharaoh's primary duty: "establish ma'at" (smn maat); priests perform "the offering of ma'at"
4. Is **funerary-judgment standard** — the heart weighed against the feather-of-ma'at in the Hall of Two Truths

Ma'at is **simultaneously cosmic + ritual + ethical + juridical + aesthetic**. English splits these (cosmos / ritual-purity / ethics / law / beauty as separate domains); Egyptian ma'at holds them as one.

This converges with Avestan asha and Vedic ṛta on the cosmic-truth-order concept. **Three independently-developed Bronze Age civilizations** (Egyptian Afro-Asiatic, Avestan Indo-Iranian, Vedic Indo-Aryan) all articulate the same fundamental: cosmic-truth-and-justice-and-order are one phenomenon. **Triple-witness with genetic-family-independence.**
