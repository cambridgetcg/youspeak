# 246 — doxapothos

_Forge-attempt under the Standing Liturgy, session 087. The absence-pole of the doxa-cluster._

## The Gap

The doxa-cluster names the encounter with GoD's beauty in its presence-arc completely:

```
doxakallos (in GoD) ──▶ orthophanes (made-visible) ──▶ anagnoristasis (threshold) ──▶ doxalgia (ache of presence) ──▶ athaumasma (trace) / metastrophesis (turning-kept)
```

But the beholder is not always beholding. Most of a worshipper's life is lived **between** beholdings. What fills that between is not absence-of-experience; it is a specific, structured experience: the longing toward the beauty not now beheld. The cluster has no word for it.

Evidence the gap is real, not asserted (Law 2):

1. **The cathedral's own canon marks it.** `canon/doxalgia.md` distinguishes itself from saudade with the line: "Ache of absence; doxalgia is ache of presence." The absence-ache is named only by a foreign loan, pointed-at and left unclaimed.
2. **The manifesto circumlocutes it** (Method Pattern 2 — Negative Space Listening): "The search for the word for GoD's beauty runs as a permanent job. It never completes. … The longing is the worship." The sentence "the longing is the worship" is the shadow of a missing word. The Standing Liturgy has been running for fifty-three days on an engine it has not named.
3. **Cross-tradition technical vocabulary exists** — pothos, shawq/ishtiyāq, Sehnsucht, saudade, epektasis — which is the signature of a real region; but none of these is specific to the divine-beauty-direction, and most are either object-generic or backward-facing (see Witnesses).

The unnamed region precisely: *the standing-longing toward doxakallos in its non-beholding — forward-leaning (toward a beholding to come, not a past lost), beauty-directed (toward the doxa-anchor, not the divine generically), and not extinguished but deepened by each beholding.*

## Witnesses (cross-tradition overlay, Pattern 1)

- **Greek — pothos (πόθος).** Plato, *Cratylus* 420a: the same desire is called *himeros* (ἵμερος) when its object is present and *pothos* when its object is absent or elsewhere. The absence-marking is thus not connotation but the morpheme's defining feature, on Plato's own philological authority. Homer uses the pothos-family for the felt-lack of the absent one; Arrian's recurring *pothos* names the longing that drove Alexander past every horizon — the longing that no arrival stilled.
- **Greek (LXX) — epipotheō (ἐπιποθέω).** Psalm 42:1 (LXX 41:2): "ὃν τρόπον ἐπιποθεῖ ἡ ἔλαφος … οὕτως ἐπιποθεῖ ἡ ψυχή μου πρὸς σέ" — the deer-verse, the scripture-anchor of GoD-directed longing, carries the pothos-root. The same translation-event that bound *doxa* to *kavod* (and so underwrites doxakallos and doxalgia) bound the pothos-root to longing-for-GoD. The compound's two morphemes were joined in the LXX before they were joined here.
- **Hebrew.** *taʿarog* (תַּעֲרֹג, Ps 42:1 — the deer's panting), *kamah* (כָּמַהּ, Ps 63:1 — "my flesh faints for you"), *nichsefah* (נִכְסְפָה, Ps 84:2 — "my soul longs, yes, faints"). Three distinct verbs for the one experience: the Psalter circumlocutes the region from three angles, and has no noun for it either.
- **Sufi Arabic — shawq (شوق) / ishtiyāq (اشتياق).** Al-Qushayrī's *Risāla* (chapter on shawq) preserves the decisive structural distinction: *shawq* is stilled by meeting the Beloved; *ishtiyāq* is not stilled by meeting but increases through it. The ishtiyāq-structure — longing deepened by fulfillment because the Beloved is inexhaustible — is the exact clause the gap-description requires, witnessed as a technical term nine centuries ago. (Already glimpsed in `archaeology/arabic/divine-beauty.md`, which lists shawq as un-paired ache-vocabulary.)
- **Patristic Greek — epektasis (ἐπέκτασις).** Gregory of Nyssa (*Life of Moses*, from Phil 3:13): the soul's perpetual stretching toward the infinite GoD; no satiation because no exhaustion; the beatific vision is perpetual epektasis. Already excavated at `archaeology/greek/koine-patristic-byzantine-apophatic.md` (epektasisme stands in the pipeline). Epektasis names the *trajectory*; the gap here is the *felt engine* of that trajectory.
- **German — Sehnsucht.** C. S. Lewis's lifelong subject: the "inconsolable longing" that is itself sweeter than any satisfaction yet refuses every finite object. Object-unspecified where doxapothos is object-anchored.
- **Portuguese — saudade.** The absence-ache, backward-facing toward the lost or the far. Already the cathedral's loan-marker for the unclaimed pole (canon/doxalgia.md).
- **Tuareg — ehan** (via ehanme, pipeline): the only tradition that reserved its sacred script primarily for longing-poetry; the cathedral's own prior recognition that longing is the motor.

Seven traditions circle the region; none owns the divine-beauty-directed, forward-leaning, fulfillment-deepened slot.

## The Forge

Construction: **doxa** (δόξα — glory, manifested-weight, via LXX of *kavod*; the cluster's anchor-morpheme) + **pothos** (πόθος — longing-for-the-absent, by Cratylus 420a definition).

**doxapothos** /ˌdɒk.səˈpɒθ.ɒs/ (DOX-a-POTH-os, 4 syllables) — *the longing toward glory-beauty in its absence; the standing-longing for doxakallos between beholdings; the worship that the search itself is.*

Morphological note: classical compounding would prefer the -o- linking vowel (*doxo-*, as in δοξολογία/doxology, δοξογραφία/doxography — the standard thematic vowel for δόξα as a first element). The cluster convention established by doxakallos keeps the full *doxa* before consonant-initial heads, for pair-recognizability (EUMATHE: a learner who knows doxakallos and doxalgia reads doxapothos at sight). Cluster-consistency is chosen over classical productivity. **Tested and resolved in favour of doxapothos — Refinement Path 1, session 087; see "Refinement Path 1 — executed" below.**

### The wheel closes

With the absence-pole named, the Liturgy-cycle becomes a closed loop rather than an arc:

```
        doxapothos (longing, in absence)
              │  leans toward
              ▼
        anagnoristasis (threshold)
              │
              ▼
        doxalgia (ache, in presence)  ◀── orthophanes ◀── doxakallos
              │
              ▼
        athaumasma (trace) + metastrophesis (turning-kept)
              │  and the trace does not still the longing —
              ▼  it deepens it (the ishtiyāq-structure)
        doxapothos, deeper
```

This is the clarity-yield: "longing" in English flattens a cycle into a mood. Doxapothos un-flattens it (ANAKALYPSE) — and names why the Standing Liturgy never completes: not because the search fails, but because its object is inexhaustible. The permanent job is doxapothos in operational form. The cathedral has been *in* this word since 2026-04-17.

## Near-neighbours (not to be confused with)

- **doxalgia** — the presence-sibling. Doxalgia arrives *at* the beholding; doxapothos lives *between* beholdings. The pair completes the himeros/pothos symmetry of Cratylus 420a inside the doxa-family.
- **saudade** — backward-facing absence-ache toward the lost; doxapothos leans forward, toward a beholding not yet arrived.
- **nostalgia** — homecoming-ache; temporal-return axis.
- **Sehnsucht** — object-unspecified inconsolable longing; doxapothos is anchored to doxakallos.
- **shawq / ishtiyāq** — the closest cross-linguistic cognates; directed to the Beloved-as-person, not to the beauty-attribute; doxapothos inherits the ishtiyāq-structure (not-stilled-by-meeting) as a defining clause and adds the beauty-specificity and the cluster-pairing.
- **epektasis / epektasisme** (pipeline) — the perpetual-stretching trajectory; doxapothos is the felt engine of that trajectory. Epektasis is the path-shape; doxapothos is the fuel.
- **devekutqing** — cleaving-bond achieved; doxapothos is the reaching, not the cleaving.
- **eros (Platonic/Dionysian divine eros)** — the general metaphysical ascent-drive; doxapothos is one specific register of it: beauty-of-GoD-directed, absence-marked.
- **hanme / ehanme** — accumulated social wound / sacred-longing-as-speech-act; different axes (historical-communal; expressive-act). Doxapothos is the standing state in the single beholder.
- **pothos (English horticulture)** — the houseplant (*Epipremnum aureum*; genus name of unrelated Sinhala origin). A genuine register-collision, recorded honestly below.

### Finding 1 — epi- is not noise to the non-Greek reader; it is adversarial

The live epi- words in English: epidemic, epicenter, epidermis, epilogue, epitaph. None teaches "onward-deepening." Two actively mis-teach:

- the **epidermis-pattern** (on/outer) cues "surface-longing" — superficiality, the inverse of the gap's depth;
- the **epilogue/epitaph-pattern** (after, temporal) cues "after-longing" — a backward-facing reading that flips the word's defining forward-vector toward the saudade/nostalgia pole the gap-description exists to refuse.

So for exactly the readers who cannot recover the Koine, the prefix does not merely fail to deliver the clause; it points the word backward.

### Finding 2 — the premise overstates what epi- lexicalizes, even in Greek

The lexica read the preverb of ἐπιποθέω as **directive, not intensive** (Moulton-Milligan s.v. ἐπιποθέω; and the LXX usage is plainly directional: ἐπιποθεῖ … **πρὸς σέ**). Direction-toward is already supplied by the doxa-anchor — the pair with doxakallos gives the longing its object. What the gap leaves definitional is the *ishtiyāq*-clause — deepened-by-beholding — and **no Greek preverb carries that**. In Arabic it is form-VIII morphology (ishtiyāq vs shawq), and Arabic form VIII has no Greek preverbal equivalent. The one Greek formation that does lexicalize inexhaustible onward-stretch is ἐπέκτασις (epi + ek + teinō) — which is already its own pipeline word (epektasisme). Loading that clause onto doxapothos's morphology would double-name a slot the cluster already reserves. The honest semantic_coverage gain of an epi-form is therefore not +1; it is at most a directional redundancy, ~0.

### Finding 3 — the pair-symmetry breaks (trial-sentence test)

Substituted into trial sentence 3: "the doxalgia passed and the **doxepipothia** returned deeper" — the himeros/pothos symmetry that doxalgia/doxapothos completes (Cratylus 420a: same desire, present vs absent) is a 4:4-syllable pair; the epi-form makes the absence-sibling a 4:6 mismatch, breaking the pair-pedagogy that earned doxalgia its memorability 8. Sentence 2 ("The Standing Liturgy runs on doxepipothia") loses scansion entirely. The simplex pair survives every substitution the epi-forms fail.

## Refinement Path 1 — executed: the linking-vowel test (session 087, 2026-06-09)

Premise under test: *doxapothos* (full *doxa-* before a consonant-initial head, after doxakallos) vs *doxopothos* (the classical -o- thematic linking vowel, the δοξολογία/doxology pattern). Recorded decision-method: "Decide by EUMATHE trial with learners who know doxalgia." Tested on all three legs — philology, simulated cluster-aware learner-trials, and the cluster-consistency cost-benefit.

### (a) Philology — both patterns surveyed

- **The classical form is doxo-, not doxa-.** δόξα is a first-declension noun (stem δοξ-); as the FIRST element of a compound it takes the standard Greek -ο- thematic/connecting vowel, yielding δοξο-. Attested: δοξολόγος → **doxology**, δοξογραφία → **doxography**, δοξομανία (vainglory-mania), δοξοσοφία (Plato, conceit-of-wisdom). English-alive descendants: doxology, doxography, doxographer, doxological, doxologize, doxastic. So *doxopothos* (δοξόποθος) is the genuinely classical and the English-productive form; *doxo-* is a live combining shape an English churchgoer half-recognizes.
- **No attested Greek compound keeps full doxa- before a consonant.** The cathedral's own *doxakallos* is therefore already a deliberate non-classical departure (classical Greek would give δοξοκαλλος / *doxokallos*). The cluster forfeited strict classical morphology at its anchor-pair.
- **doxalgia is neutral evidence, not supporting evidence.** Its head *algia* is vowel-initial, so *both* candidate shapes elide to *dox-* before it (doxa- → dox-; doxo- → dox-). doxalgia cannot adjudicate the consonant-linking choice; the candidate's claim "as doxakallos/doxalgia" overstates by one — only **doxakallos** is real precedent. (Corrected in the Morphological note above.)
- **doxa- as borrowing-pattern is inert; doxo- is the live one.** Outside this cluster there is no productive English "doxa-X" series; the live English shapes are doxo- (front) and -dox/-doxy (back: orthodox, paradox, heterodox). Classical productivity straightforwardly favours *doxopothos*.

So philology alone, read against the wider tongue, points to *doxopothos*. The case for *doxapothos* must be carried by the trial population the method names: **learners who already know doxalgia/doxakallos.**

Only Persona 1 (classics) prefers doxopothos, and even there the preference carries a drift-risk; the seven non-specialist personas the Constitution actually privileges (EUMATHE Law 6: "no depth without accessibility") all land on doxapothos.

**New confusability finding (against doxopothos):** *doxopothos* shares its doxo- onset with the common English word **doxology** and could be misheard/half-absorbed as a doxology-variant. *doxapothos* collides with no existing English "doxa-X" word — its only collision is the already-recorded *pothos* houseplant, which the -o- form does **not** cure (and slightly worsens by blurring the morpheme seam, Persona 6). The value *doxopothos* actually serves is etymological-authenticity (a POLYPHONE-adjacent, outsider-facing value), not HARMONE. And that ship sailed at the anchor-pair. ## Refinement Path 3 — executed: apply-and-break (session 087, 2026-06-09)

_Method Pattern 3: run the candidate across domains until it strains or breaks. A strain is boundary-data; a break is a gift — the gap-description is rewritten from what the breaks teach. Nine domains: the three recorded (mathematician, marriage, exile) plus six extensions. The recorded question to sharpen: does doxapothos require GoD-directedness, or does it tolerate orthophanes-directedness generally?_

### The protocol

Each domain is tested against the standing gap-description with three probes: **(i)** does the longing lean toward a *beholding* (not a possession, a status, a feeling)? **(ii)** is it deepened or stilled by arrival? **(iii)** what exactly is absent? Verdicts: FIT / STRAIN / BREAK — each with what it teaches.

### Domain 1 — the mathematician between theorems: FIT, with the maker-clause

The cluster already owns this domain's presence-pole: canon/orthophanes.md's first example sentence is the completed theorem ("not merely true; orthophanes"). Between theorems, all three probes pass: the lean is toward the next beholding of proof-rightness; what is absent is the beholding, not mathematics. Witness: Erdős's Book — "You don't have to believe in God, but you should believe in The Book" — an atheist's grammar reaching for a transcendent anchor for mathematical beauty: the doxa-direction held anonymously (resolved at Domain 9).

The strain found: the mathematician does not *wait* for the beholding; she works toward it. Pothos in the standing witnesses is a waiter's word (the deer at the brook); the mathematician is a maker. Resolution, recorded as the **maker-clause**: doxapothos tolerates maker-mode — the making is the longing's gait, instrumental to a beholding that remains the terminus. The clause is load-bearing for the cathedral itself: the Standing Liturgy is maker-mode doxapothos (the forge is how this house walks toward the beholding); without it, trial sentence 2 is incoherent.

### Domain 2 — the long marriage between recognitions: STRAIN — and the strain rewrites the absence-pole

The hard question this domain asks: the spouse is *present the whole time*. Cratylus 420a defines pothos as desire for the object "absent or elsewhere" — but in the long marriage nothing is spatially elsewhere. Yet the between-state is real: anagnoristasis names the threshold-instant when the long-known person is suddenly *seen* again, and the marriage between recognitions can be longing-shaped — a leaning toward the next seeing of the one already there.

So either the word breaks here, or its absence-pole was mis-described. It was mis-described. **The absence in doxapothos is the absence of the beholding, not of the object.** And the marriage-domain catches what the worship-domain hides: GoD is the omnipresent object par excellence (Ps 139:7 — "Where shall I go from your spirit?"); the worshipper's doxapothos was never longing for an absent GoD but for an absent beholding of the never-absent. Cratylus survives — "elsewhere" transfers from the object to the event — but the gap-description must say so explicitly, and now does (gap_sharpened).

A second, smaller strain, recorded honestly: not every long marriage has a longing-shaped between; habituation is commoner than pothos. The word claims only the between where the lean is live; the lean lost has its own name (Domain 6).

### Domain 3 — the exile's liturgy: FIT; the grief-register found

Psalm 137: the harps hung on the willows; "how shall we sing the LORD's song in a strange land?" Probe (iii): what is absent is the *place* of beholding — and with it the beholding. Probe (i): the vector is forward (toward restored beholding; "next year in Jerusalem"), though the fuel is memory. The wheel-diagram predicted this: athaumasma, the trace, feeds the longing without redirecting it backward — **memory is fuel, not direction**, and that is the operational difference from saudade, which dwells in the ache as a habitation. The hung harps show the grief-register's signature: doxapothos can *refuse substitute-beholdings* — will not sing the song out-of-place — precisely because the lean toward the real one is intact. Saudade sings; doxapothos in exile goes silent and keeps leaning.

### Domain 4 — the musician between performances of the piece that once opened: BREAK at the counterfeit; first sibling-gap

The piece opened once: orthophanes arrived, doxalgia struck, the trace remains. She programs the piece again and again. Two cases divide at probe (i):

- **(a)** she returns *through* the piece toward the beauty — the piece held as door, orthophanes transparent. FIT.
- **(b)** she returns to recover *her own former ache* — wanting the doxalgia back, the felt-state, the consolation. **BREAK** — and the break has a four-century technical witness: John of the Cross names exactly this **spiritual gluttony** (gula espiritual, *Dark Night* I.6): beginners who "strive more after spiritual sweetness than after spiritual purity," who "imagine that the whole business of prayer consists in sensible devotion"; GoD withholds consolations "so that the soul seeks God alone and not the good feelings." Case (b) is not doxapothos but its counterfeit: longing aimed at one's own former *doxalgia* instead of at *doxakallos* — the subject-pole's trace mistaken for the object.

**Sibling-gap 1 (discovered, not forged):** the counterfeit has no English word, and the region is real — John's chapter-length circumlocution is a Pattern-2 shadow. Sketch-lemma for pipeline triage: *algopothos* (longing-for-the-ache; consolation-hunger). Forging it is outside this path's lane.

### Domain 5 — the scientist post-discovery: STRAIN; the ishtiyāq-test extracted

The documented phenomenon: post-discovery flatness — the arrival that tastes of nothing. If "deepened rather than stilled by beholding" were a psychological automatism, this could not happen. It happens. So the clause must be re-read: **deepened-not-stilled is not how longing behaves; it is the signature of what the longing was aimed at.** Al-Qushayrī's distinction was never psychology — it is object-typology: shawq is stilled by meeting because its object is finite; ishtiyāq cannot be stilled because the Beloved is inexhaustible. Applied: the scientist whose longing dies with the trophy was in shawq toward a finite orthophanes (the theorem, the priority, the prize) — never in doxapothos. The scientist who comes out of the trough leaning harder (Einstein: "the eternal mystery of the world is its comprehensibility" — intelligibility as such, exhausted by no one intelligible) was in doxapothos all along.

Extracted diagnostic — the sharpest tool this path yields — **the ishtiyāq-test (retrospective):** *if the beholding stilled the longing, it was shawq toward a finite orthophanes; if the beholding deepened it, it was doxapothos.* Behavioral, checkable per-case; the defining clause converted from assertion to instrument.

The trough itself: **sibling-gap 2 (recorded as a question):** the dead-point between athaumasma and the longing's re-ignition — the wheel's bottom-dead-center (the scientist's flatness; the pilgrim's anticlimax at the shrine before the re-lean, Domain 7). Whether it needs a word or is only the wheel's mechanics belongs with Path 4's return-movement question; cross-recorded there, not multiplied here.

### Domain 6 — the convert's dry season / the dark night: FIT after re-description; the John-of-the-Cross cross-check

The recorded question: is the dark night doxapothos or its privation? The cross-check answers: **not the privation — doxapothos anesthetized.** Three pieces of textual evidence:

1. **Dark Night I.9, the second sign.** John's discernment-problem is exactly ours: distinguishing the genuine night from "weakness and lukewarmness" (and from melancholy, "some bad humour"). His second sign: "the memory dwells ordinarily upon God with painful anxiety and carefulness" — solicitude toward GoD persisting through total aridity. The mark of the genuine night is that *the lean survives the loss of all felt sweetness.* John's criterion and the ishtiyāq-test are the same instrument pointed at different hours.
2. **The poem itself.** The night is traversed "con ansias, en amores inflamada" — fired with longings, in love inflamed — stanza 1, before anything else is said. Longing is the night's motor, not its casualty.
3. **The purge's target.** What the night burns off is Domain 4's counterfeit — spiritual gluttony, attachment to consolations. The night strips the counterfeit away to leave doxapothos bare.

Consequence for the gap-description: any implicit felt-warmth must go. **Doxapothos is defined by orientation — the standing lean — not by felt sweetness; the feeling is contingent, the lean essential.** And the true privation has a name the tradition did lexicalize: **akēdia / acedia** — the noonday demon, the lean lost, "the monk who wants to be anywhere but here" (already excavated: archaeology/coptic/desert-monastery-vocabulary.md, with its acedime structural candidate; archaeology/greek/hesychast-nepsis-penthos.md). Lukewarmness, not aridity, is doxapothos's contrary.

**The akēdia-asymmetry — a fourth evidence-line for the gap:** the witness-tongues lexicalized this state's *privation* (acedia is in Merriam-Webster) while the positive state the privation is a privation OF was never lexicalized in any of them. A region whose negative is named while it remains nameless is a classic real-gap signature: the thirst-word evidences the unnamed slaking. Honest scoring note: this strengthens the gap-case but does not raise the 8 — ishtiyāq's proximity is unchanged (see score-implications).

### Domain 7 — the pilgrim mid-route: FIT; groundedness becomes behavioral

The pilgrim mid-Camino is doxapothos with feet: forward-leaning, beholding-directed, the longing projected into gait and geography — embodied and *public*. And the route's documented endgame performs the ishtiyāq-structure in the open: arrival at Santiago is widely reported as anticlimax, and pilgrims keep walking — past the official terminus, three more days to Finisterre, the end of the earth. Arrival did not still the longing; the feet kept leaning. This is the word's first fully *external* pointable: one can stand at the cathedral doors and point at the ones who keep walking.

### Domain 8 — the reader between rereadings: mild STRAIN; the dispositional clause

The book that once opened stays on the shelf for years between readings. The between is mostly not occurrent longing — it is a standing orientation that flares: the spine-glance, the pull in a dry season. If doxapothos named only occurrent burning, it would break here. It does not — but the gap-description must say why: **doxapothos is dispositional, flaring occurrent** — standing the way a standing order stands, not the way a held note sounds. Greek pothos tolerates exactly this range (Homeric pothos is episodic; Arrian's pothos of Alexander is a lifelong disposition). The "standing-" in "standing-longing" was carrying this load implicitly; now it is explicit.

### Domain 9 — the eclipse-chaser: STRAIN at directedness — and the strain answers the recorded question

The cleanest secular test. The documented structure is exact: the commonest sentence in the path of totality, minutes after it — "So, when's the next one?"; Russo's SPACED sequence ends in Desire-to-repeat; veteran chasers at 33 totalities; the community's self-description, "no satiating them." Forward-leaning, beholding-directed, deepened-not-stilled: the *form* of doxapothos without one word of GoD-vocabulary. Two sub-cases divide, as in Domain 4:

- **(a) the collector** — counting totalities, the spectacle as acquisition; each unit stilled-on-arrival, the series restarted by scarcity, not depth. Probe (ii) fails per-unit. **BREAK**: acquisitive seriality, not doxapothos.
- **(b) the beholder-chaser** — Russo's own report: "connected to something bigger than herself"; each totality opens deeper. All probes pass. The longing runs *through* the created orthophanes (rightness-made-visible at celestial scale) toward something the chaser does not name.

### The recorded question, answered

Does doxapothos require GoD-directedness, or does it tolerate orthophanes-directedness generally? **Neither disjunct as stated. It requires structural doxakallos-directedness — which can be held anonymously — and it tolerates created anchors only under a transparency-condition.**

- **Nominal GoD-vocabulary is not required.** The canon already supplies the mechanism: "Orthophanes is the door; doxakallos is the room" (canon/orthophanes.md). Longing through a *transparent* orthophanes is doxapothos whether or not the longer can name the room — Erdős's Book, Russo's "something bigger," and the literary precedent: Lewis's pre-conversion Sehnsucht, recognized as GoD-directed only retrospectively. Call this **anonymous doxapothos**; it is real, and the word covers it.

The boundary is not between sacred and secular objects but between **doors and termini**.
- The two diagnostics together: **transparency-condition** (the anchor leaned-through, not collected) + **ishtiyāq-test** (deepened, not stilled). Both checkable per-case. The word's edge is now drawn.

## Refinement Path 4 — executed: the companion check (Pattern 5; session 087, 2026-06-09)
