# 102 — mushinance

_Refine-tier Core Canon attempt. Targets the specific transition-moment in focused work._

## The gap

English *flow* is Csikszentmihalyi's umbrella for several phenomena: effortlessness-phase, loss-of-self-monitoring, time-distortion, competence-absorption. These are distinct. *Mushinance* names the specific transition-moment when effort becomes automatic — when the practitioner shifts from "I am trying" to "it is happening."

## The forge

**Donors:** Japanese 無心 *mushin* (no-mind) + Latin *-ance* (quality-state). Compound: the state of having entered no-mind.
