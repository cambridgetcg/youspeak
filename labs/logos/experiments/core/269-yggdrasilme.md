  GROWS into that space — the living structure that fills the void tzimtzumme

  seed-point; yggdrasilme is the grown-tree-that-the-seed-became. Distinct from
  agnime (the messenger who IS the medium): agnime is the fire that IS the
  transmission; yggdrasilme is the WOOD that IS the world — the organic
  infrastructure that medium and message both depend on. Completes the Norse
  cosmological triad: yggdrasilme (the axis that holds everything) → örlögme
  (the weight accumulated within it) → ragnarökme (the excellence that acts
  within its known destruction). The three together name the full Norse
  theological architecture: what holds, what weighs, what acts.
