# 143 — mahabbahqing

_Sixth qing-family member. Names the Sufi felt-bond-of-love-toward-the-Beloved. Specialized 7.30; Liturgy-Original. Direct response to Yu's S021 directive on building love-of-GoD vocabulary; complements ahavame (Hebrew, S020)._

## The discovery-move

Yu's S021 directive: "keep integrating and building on our LOVE for GoD." Beat 104 frames the directive as continuation of S020's love-and-best-effort focus. Multiple traditions converge on love-of-DIVINE; YOUSPEAK has ahavame (Hebrew); Beat 105 adds Arabic-Sufi mahabbahqing.

Arabic mahabbah is theologically-rich. From triliteral root ḥ-b-b "to love." In Quranic Arabic, mahabbah names love-of-Allah specifically. In Sufi tradition (the great mystical tradition of Islam), mahabbah becomes THE central religious-disposition. Rabia of Basra (~d. 801 CE), one of the founders of Sufism, famously declared: "I have loved thee with two loves: a selfish love and a love that is worthy of thee. As for the selfish love, I think of thee, exclusively. As for the love that is worthy of thee, in it thou raisest the veil that I may see thee." Rumi's Mathnawi and Ibn ʿArabi's mystical writings center on mahabbah-of-Allah.

The Sufi register adds to ahavame's commanded-disposition register: **mahabbah carries the FELT-MYSTICAL-BINDING dimension** with implication of *fanāʾ* (self-annihilation in the Beloved). The mystic's progress is from mahabbah → ʿishq (passionate-love) → fanāʾ (self-annihilation) → baqāʾ (subsistence-in-the-Beloved). Mahabbah is the foundational stage that opens the path.

For YOUSPEAK: the felt-bond-of-mystical-love register has six-tradition convergence (Hebrew ahavah, Greek agape, Sanskrit bhakti, Arabic mahabbah, Latin caritas/dilectio, Sufi-specifically) but mahabbah is theologically-distinctive in its mystical-felt-binding emphasis with fanāʾ-implication.

The compound mahabbah + qing (Mandarin felt-bond marker) names: the felt-bond of mahabbah-grade-mystical-love. Two non-IE families (Arabic Afro-Asiatic + Mandarin Sino-Tibetan) = polyphone 8 = Core-eligible by polyphone-mathematics.

## The phenomenon

Across Sufi tradition, mahabbah names a register that:
1. Is FELT (heart-bond, not merely-cognitive disposition)
2. Is MYSTICAL (oriented toward the Beloved-as-divine, beyond ordinary-relationship)
3. Carries SELF-ANNIHILATION-implication (mahabbah opens the path to fanāʾ)
4. Is GIVEN-AND-RECEIVED (the Sufi tradition holds mahabbah as gift of Allah, not creature-self-generated)
5. Is COMMITTABLE (despite its mystical depth, it can be entered through practice)

This complements ahavame (Hebrew commanded-love) and bhaktime (Hindu devotional-love) — three different traditions naming three aspects of the same convergence-cluster.

## The forge

**Donors:**

- **Arabic *mahabbah*** (محبة) — love. From root ḥ-b-b "to love"; cognate with ḥubb (love verb/noun). In Quranic Arabic: "Whomever Allah loves, Allah is loved-by" (cross-reference to passages on Allah-and-his-loved-ones). In Sufi tradition: the theologically-richest love-term; central in Rabia, Rumi, Ibn ʿArabi, al-Ghazali's discussions of love.

- **Mandarin *qing*** (情) — felt-bond. Established YOUSPEAK qing-family marker. Sixth member with this forge.

**Compound:** *mahabbah + qing* → **mahabbahqing** (ma-HAB-bah-ching; 4 syllables).

The compound names: felt-bond of mahabbah-grade-mystical-love. The qing-grade-bond of Sufi-love-of-the-Beloved.
