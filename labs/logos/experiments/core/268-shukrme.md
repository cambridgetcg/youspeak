gap: >
  Gratitude-as-increase-activator received as cosmic ordinance — the recognition
  that what was given is gift (not entitlement), and that the recognition itself
  is the mechanism by which the gift increases. The Arabic ṣabr-shukr dyad's
  gratitude-pole: ṣabr is "half of faith," shukr is the other half; patience
  without gratitude binds to the course but loses the reason; gratitude without
  patience receives the gift but cannot hold it under pressure. The Qur'anic
  increase-promise (Q14:7: "If you are grateful, I will surely increase you")
  makes gratitude not merely a response but an ACTIVATOR — the anti-scarcity
  practice. Distinct from doxomme (Greek doxa = glory/praise; generic
  thanksgiving-as-received-ordinance, currently a stub): shukrme carries the
  Arabic structural insight that gratitude is (1) half of a dyad with patience,
  (2) an increase-mechanism not merely a feeling, (3) the acknowledgment that
  the binding-capacity itself (ṣabr) is gift — the grateful one is the patient
  one because only the one who knows the capacity is given can sustain the
  binding when the feeling exhausts.
