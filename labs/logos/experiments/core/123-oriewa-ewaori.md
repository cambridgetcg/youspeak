# 123 — oriewa / ewaori

_First all-Yoruba Anastrophance pair. Both members held at refine; the pair as a whole demonstrates that the diplosemic mechanisms are language-agnostic — they operate in any sufficiently-substantive donor-tradition, not only in Greek-internal compound._

---

## Mechanism note

Anastrophance (grammars/diplosemy/mechanisms/anastrophance.md): swap M₁M₂ → M₂M₁; the second morpheme is semantic head; head-position inversion yields correlated-distinct pair. Established in YOUSPEAK via doxakallos ↔ kallodoxa (both Greek) and partially via orthophanes/kallophanes (phainō-family siblings).

Applied to Yoruba donors:
- M₁=orí (destined-head), M₂=ẹ̀wà (beauty-of-character)
- oriewa (head = ẹ̀wà) — "beauty-of-orí" — the beauty-quality belonging to a destined-head-aligned life
- ewaori (head = orí) — "orí-of-ẹ̀wà" — the destined-trajectory belonging to beauty-cultivation

Both words arise from the same morpheme-pair; the head-inversion produces two distinct concepts that together unfold what the Yoruba proverb *ìwà l'ẹ̀wà* (character is beauty) asserts in two directional readings.

---

## Candidate A — *oriewa*

**Gap:** The visible-beauty of a life lived in alignment with its destined-head. When a person is in oriance, something shows. Their movements, their speech, their face at rest — the alignment radiates as a distinct kind of beauty. Not "glow" (too generic), not "grace" (too Christian-register), not "poise" (too studied). The specific beauty that is THE SHOWING-OF-ALIGNMENT.

**The forge:** orí + ẹ̀wà → oriewa. "Beauty-of-destined-head." The beauty that is a life's alignment-with-its-shape made perceptible.

**Examples:**
- The teacher in their thirtieth year of teaching, whose face in the classroom has an oriewa you can see from the back row.
- The grandmother who served her family for six decades; her oriewa is not a youth-beauty but a life-shape-beauty.
- The craftsman at the wheel, their hands steady: oriewa is why apprentices come to watch, not to learn the technique.

## Candidate B — *ewaori*

**Gap:** The life whose very destined-trajectory IS character-beauty cultivation. Where oriewa is the visible-beauty of the aligned-life, ewaori is the life whose orí is *specifically to cultivate character-beauty*. The artist of inner form. The monk whose vocation is not a task but a quality-of-being.

**The forge:** ẹ̀wà + orí → ewaori. "Destined-head-of-beauty." The calling-to-be-beautiful-in-character.

**Examples:**
- The elder who does no-thing-in-particular but whose presence forms others — ewaori is their vocation.
- The rabbi or sensei whose teaching-is-their-being, not merely their content.
- The grandparent-role at its best: ewaori as the vocation of late life.

## What this forge demonstrates (method-level yield)

1. **Anastrophance is language-agnostic.** Previously demonstrated only in Greek-internal compounds (doxakallos/kallodoxa). Yoruba-internal application yields a valid pair. The mechanism is not tied to Greek.

2. **Specialized-tier diplosemic pairs are valid.** Not every pair needs Core-tier members. A Yoruba-internal Anastrophance pair legitimately sits in Specialized-aesthetics register.

3. **The Niger-Congo expansion now has internal diplosemic structure.** Yoruba donors can pair among themselves via Anastrophance. The Yoruba sub-register has its own mechanism-productivity, not just donor-status.

4. **Future Anastrophance candidates.** The Yoruba donor-set offers further pairs:
   - orí + àṣẹ: oriase (performative-force of destined-head) ↔ aseori (destined-head of performative-force) — candidate
