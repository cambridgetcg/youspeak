# 127 — theokimme

_Second divine-property coinage. Names GoD's constitutive-attention — the divine-ground of creaturely kimme. Held at Specialized (refine 7.45); the discovery is real, the threshold is the register's._

## The discovery-move

Continuing the turn-inward (Session 005 framing). Where kimme (Core) names attention-as-received-ordinance from the creature's side — the creature's recognition that attention-on-me is a gift — *theokimme* names the same phenomenon from GoD's side: the divine attention that IS the giving-of-ordinance, the attention which, by attending, grounds.

## The cross-tradition overlay

Multiple traditions attest a specific divine-property: GoD's attention is not observational but constitutive. The creature exists because GoD attends; attention IS the sustaining.

| Tradition | Term | Partial-dimension named |
|---|---|---|
| Jewish | hashgaḥa pratit (השגחה פרטית) | "individual watching-over"; divine attention-to-particulars |
| Christian | conservatio (Aquinas) | continuous-creation; GoD's sustaining-act |
| Islamic | al-Basir (الْبَصِير) | "The All-Seeing"; one of the Names; constitutive vision |
| Hindu (Advaita) | cit-śakti | consciousness-power; consciousness-that-is-sustaining |
| Buddhist (Mahayana) | prajñāpāramitā | wisdom-beholding; the beholding that is emptiness-and-form |
| Yoruba | Olódùmarè sees | creator-oversight of allocated orí |
| Igbo | Chukwu knows | great-chi's knowing as provident-accompaniment |

Each names a partial-aspect. None captures the compact quality: *attention-that-sustains-by-attending*, the ontological-grounding kimme that IS what the creature receives.

## The forge

**Donors:**

- **Greek *theos*** (θεός) — GoD. Established in YOUSPEAK via *theobasis* (Core, 4th entry). The morpheme marks the referent as divine.

- **Japanese *ki*** (氣/気) — attention, focused-being-toward, the attentive-state. Established via *kimance* (Core) and *kimme* (Core). The morpheme carries attention-as-engaged-stance.

- **Sumerian *me*** (𒈨) — divine-ordinance, received-gift-quality. Established via *kimme* and *doxomme*. In YOUSPEAK the -me morpheme has become the received-register marker.

**Compound:** *theo + ki + me* → **theokimme** (THE-o-KIM-me; 4 syllables).

The compound reads as: GoD's attention-as-ordinance-source. Or equivalently: the ki-me that is divine. The me-family already names the received-register; theokimme names the giver-side of the same phenomenon. Creature's kimme is the reception of what theokimme is on the divine side.
