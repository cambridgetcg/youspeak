# 129 — theoshemme

_Third divine-property coinage. Names GoD's speaking-that-is-heard — the divine-pole companion to shemme. Held at Specialized refine 7.45 (consistent with walkehesed and theokimme; the divine-property-register pattern continues)._

## The discovery-move

This forge completes the dual-pole pair shemme ↔ theoshemme, opening the third entry in the new theobases/ organ. Where shemme (Core, Session 006) names the creature's receptive-hearing-as-received-ordinance, theoshemme names the divine speaking-act whose nature is to be receivable by such hearing.

The relation: theoshemme is what makes shemme possible. Creatures can receive-hearing-as-shaping because GoD speaks-as-heard. The creature-pole and divine-pole together name one phenomenon in two directions.

## The cross-tradition evidential base

The creature-pole shemme rests on a 7-tradition convergence (mapped in `convergences/hearing-as-shaping.md`): Akkadian *šēmu*, Hebrew *Shema*, Arabic *samiʿa*, Aramaic *šǝmaʿ*, Sufi *samāʿ*, Buddhist *śravaṇa*, Greek *akouō*. The divine-pole theoshemme inherits this evidential base from the inverse-side: where each tradition names the creature's receptive-hearing, each tradition implicitly attests the divine-act that elicits-and-makes-possible the hearing.

- Hebrew Shema implies *adonai dovrim* — "the Lord speaks" (cf. Exodus 19:9; Numbers 12:6)
- Arabic *samiʿa* coordinates with the Quranic *kalām Allāh* (the Word/Speaking of God) and the divine name *al-Mutakallim* (the One who speaks)
- Christian *akouō* coordinates with *logos* — the Word that is spoken-and-incarnated
- Buddhist *śravaṇa* coordinates with the Buddha's *dharmadeśanā* (teaching-uttering)
- Sufi *samāʿ* coordinates with the *kalimat* (words) and the divine *qawl* (saying)

Where seven traditions name the creature's hearing, seven traditions implicitly attest the divine speaking-act that makes the hearing possible. The convergence is dual: every shemme-attestation has its theoshemme-attestation as inverse-witness.

## The forge

**Donors:**

- **Greek *theos*** (θεός) — GoD; established in YOUSPEAK via *theobasis*. Marks the divine-class.

- **Akkadian *šēmu*** (𒈠𒁇) — to hear, listen, obey. Established via *shemme*. Here, the morpheme functions to identify the speaking-that-is-heard register: not all speech, but specifically the speaking that elicits receptive-shaping-hearing.

- **Sumerian *me*** (𒈨) — divine-ordinance, received-gift-quality. Established via the -me family.

**Compound:** *theo + šēmu + me* → **theoshemme** (THE-o-SHEM-me; 4 syllables).

The compound reads as: GoD's speaking-as-ordinance-source-that-elicits-receptive-hearing. Or more compactly: the divine-side of shemme. The pair shemme ↔ theoshemme together names: GoD speaks (theoshemme) such that creatures hear-and-are-shaped (shemme).
