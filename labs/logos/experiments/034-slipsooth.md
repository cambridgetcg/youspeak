# Experiment 034 — slipsooth

**Date:** 2026-07-07
**Forge:** not a forge — a discovery intake. The word arrived complete in Yu's message; this experiment records the finding, the mechanism check, and the assessment.

## The discovery event

Yu, 2026-07-07, verbatim (Cantonese, all joy preserved):

> DIUUUUUUUUUU我發現左世界嘅真相了 其實我地係愛星人🤯 跟住係講廣東話嘅 點知啲人一代傳一代 越讀越歪 變左外星人😂 跟住外星人就以為自己係地球人 唔記得左自己係愛星人😂 其實地球係愛星 唔係外星呀屌😂❤️

The claim: 愛星人 (love-star people) → generations of crooked reading → 外星人 (aliens) → the aliens forget they are love-star people → but the earth is 愛星, not 外星.

## Mechanism check (real phonology under the myth)

- 愛 = oi3; 外 = ngoi6 — one initial apart in Cantonese.
- **ng-deletion** (ngoi6 → oi6) is the textbook Cantonese "lazy sound" (懶音).
- **ng-hypercorrection** (oi3 → ngoi3) is the documented counter-error: speakers afraid of lazy sounds insert ng- where it never was.
- So the burial direction (愛 → 外, via hypercorrection) and the homecoming direction (外 → 愛-adjacent, via lazy deletion) are both real, live processes. The myth runs on genuine machinery; only the historical descent is cosmology rather than linguistics, and the canon entry labels it so.

## The gap

English (and the Canon) had no word for **truth recovered by drift** — folk etymology, eggcorn, mondegreen all name the drift or its novel products, never the homecoming. The Canon had verisleight (truth that deceives, by skill) with no mirror (error that reveals, by accident). The square was open on one corner.

## Candidates considered

- *palinphone* (Greek: sounding-back) — clean but clinical; no truth-term in it.
- *lethophone / anaphonesis* — wrong register; the cathedral is not a phonology lab.
- *oikophone* (home-sound) — charming (and *oi* hides in it) but names the sound, not the truth.
- **slipsooth** — chosen: mirrors verisleight limb for limb (veri→slip: truth→error; sleight→sooth: cunning→truth), two syllables, front-stressed, both donors already living in English registers (slip of the tongue; soothsayer). Reads transparently on first hearing.
