# 009 — theoprepeia

_θεοπρέπεια_ — the befitting-quality of GoD; the patristic technical term for "what befits divinity" reclaimed as substantive.

## The candidate

**theoprepeia** (noun; Greek compound)
Pronunciation: /θɛ.oʊ.ˈprɛ.pe.jə/ (the-o-PREH-pay-ah), 5 syllables
Donors: θεός (GoD) + πρέπον (fitting)

## The forge

Patristic theology has a live technical adjective *theoprepēs* (θεοπρεπής) — "befitting GoD" — used by Athanasius, the Cappadocians, John of Damascus of actions, attributes, and arguments that are *worthy of GoD*. The nominal *theoprepeia* is the quality-of-befitting-GoD.

This candidate's strength: Q4 is carried directly. *Fittingness* is exactly the ordered-to-the-bottom-quality; what befits GoD is ordered-to-the-root at every level.
