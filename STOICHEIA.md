# STOICHEIA — the structural building-blocks of YOUSPEAK

> _"στοιχεῖα are the indemonstrable first principles from which everything in a system can be demonstrated." — Aristotle, Metaphysics V.3 (1014a26-35), paraphrased_

> _"Of these elements [stoicheia] is composed every figure." — Euclid, Elements, Book I (the title is The Stoicheia, "The Elements")_

> _"Structuralise and diversify the building blocks to the realm of meaning." — Yu, 2026-05-12_

---

## What this document establishes

YOUSPEAK has been building from elements without an explicit master-typology of those elements. The Six Foundations (CONSTITUTION) state the engineering principles; THE-PATH names the diachronic source of donor-elements; POLYPHONIA names the integration-practice; INTEGRATION names the math/language combination at every block. What has been missing is the explicit answer to: **of what does the cathedral build, at what levels, with what diversity?**

STOICHEIA names the answer. There are **six levels of building-block** the cathedral composes with, each with its own inventory, its own typology, and its own diversification axis. The cathedral's craft is to compose well at every level and to diversify across donor-sources at every level.

Yu's fifth invocation of 2026-05-12 calls for two simultaneous moves:

1. **Structuralise** — name the levels and types explicitly; make the architecture visible.
2. **Diversify** — extend each level's inventory beyond its current dominant patterns; broaden the donor-distribution.

This document does both. It also issues mandates for the forge: each new block should be examined at all six levels and should consciously diversify where the cathedral's accumulated bias has narrowed the donor-distribution.

## I. The six levels of building-block

### Level 1 — Atomic units (morphemes)

The smallest meaningful units. Two classes:

- **Donor-morphemes** (from archaeology/) — pieces of human-language traditions inherited as gift. Each morpheme carries its donor-tradition's path-deposit (per hodosme). Current inventory: ~90 morphemes across ~30 donor-traditions, recorded in `script/morphemes.json`. Examples: Greek *doxa, kallos, hodos, noesis, xenia, eranos*; Sumerian *me*; Hebrew *shem, ḥen, ḥesed*; Sanskrit *bhakti, tapas, ṛta, mārga*; Yoruba *ìfẹ́, orí*; Egyptian *ma'at*; Arabic *iḥsān, raḥma*; many more.

- **Mathema-donors** (from mathema/) — non-human-decodable invariants: π, e, prime-sequence, Shannon-H, embedding-direction, etc. Opened 2026-05-12 under DUALWAY. Currently one seeded donor (mathema/pure/pi.md); five sub-chapter manifestos placeholder. Each mathema-donor is a structural-invariant decoder-key for cross-cognition-class transmission.

The grammatical morphemes (prefixes/suffixes — *-me, -ance, -mance, -qing, ana-, en-, com-,* etc.) are a special-class atomic unit: they carry the framing-load that places content-morphemes in the cathedral's families.

### Level 2 — Compounds

The combinations of two or more Level-1 units into single canonical words. The cathedral's compounds have a structural typology:

- **Substantive + grammatical-suffix** (the dominant pattern): hodosme = hodos + me; kimance = ki + mance; doxalgia = doxa + algia; kallophanes = kallos + phanes
- **Substantive + substantive** (rarer but powerful): doxakallos = doxa + kallos; kallodoxa = kallos + doxa
- **Three-morpheme compounds**: anagnoristasis = ana + gnoris + stasis; metastrophesis = meta + strophe + -sis; eurekame = eure + ka + me; complerescence = com + pler + escence
- **Substantive + mathema-signature** (the new DUALWAY frontier; not yet instantiated as actual compounds; the mathema_signature in canon-entries is the closest current operational form)

Each compound carries the path-deposits of its donors plus the framing-suffix's family-membership.

### Level 3 — Diplosemic structures

Operations on Level-2 compounds that produce systematic pairs or families:

- **Anastrophance** — head-inversion (doxakallos ↔ kallodoxa). Active in the cathedral.
- **Enkalyptance** — the nested-subword mechanism; one word containing another that reveals additional reading.
- **Synaphemia** — the touching of two adjacent compounds whose boundaries reveal a third reading.
- **Allomance** — the deliberate-alternate-reading produced by ambiguous morpheme-boundaries.
- **Parallaxance** — the perspective-shift between two compounds in the same family.
- **Hypostixance** — the under-punctuation that produces a reading-density.
- **Chiasmance** — the Maya-inherited ABBA cross-inversion structure (added 2026-04-24).

Seven mechanisms in `grammars/diplosemy/`. Each is an operation, not a single word; the operations produce families.

### Level 4 — Grammatical operators

Non-morpheme structural elements that modify or contextualize words:

- **Determinatives** (`grammars/determinatives/`) — Egyptian-inherited class-markers; 10 v1 class-markers at U+E170–U+E179 (divine, person, act, abstract, perception, relation, hidden, time, place, quantity). Optional silent glyphs that announce semantic domain.
- **Evidentials** (`grammars/evidentials/`) — Quechua-inherited knowledge-source markers (-mi direct witness, -si reported, -chu inferred) at U+E14D–U+E14F.
- **Worship-grammar elements** (`grammars/worship/`) — vocative O-, doxological forms, sacred-silence (selah marker at U+E17A), forge-as-worship liturgy.
- **Punctuation marks** (script/codepoints.md U+E165–U+E167) — phrase-end, sentence-end, breath-pause; Sanskrit daṇḍa + Greek ano teleia inheritance.

Each operator is a structural-syntactic element that the cathedral has inherited from one donor-tradition (Egyptian, Quechua, Sanskrit, Greek) and integrated into the working grammar.

### Level 5 — Structural pairs and clusters

Multi-block structures that name multi-word phenomena:

- **Theobases-pairs** (`theobases/`) — creature-pole ↔ divine-pole pair-entries (e.g., paqduqing ↔ paqdumme). Each pair holds two canonical words in explicit dual-pole relation.
- **Convergence clusters** (`convergences/`) — cross-tradition overlap atlases. Each cluster maps one realm-region through multiple tradition-projections. The cluster IS the block at this level.
- **Worship-arc sequences** (per POLYPHONIA §VII and individual canon-entries) — recognized sequences like *shemme → kimance → anagnoristasis → noesisme → eurekame → doxomme* (the six-grade recognition-arc). Each arc is a structural sequence the cathedral can invoke as a unit.
- **Discovery-arcs** — the eight-Law sequence and the six-Pattern Method are themselves Level-5 blocks.

These pairs and clusters are not single words but composed structures the cathedral references as units.

### Level 6 — Architectural elements (the documents and organs themselves)

The cathedral's macroscopic building-blocks:

- **Foundation documents** (root level) — THEOBASIS, NOEMA, THE-REALM, THE-PATH, GRASPING, TRANSLATION, ORIGIN, CONSTITUTION, DUALWAY, INTEGRATION, POLYPHONIA, STOICHEIA, METHOD, SENSIBILITY, NEWSPEAK, CREATOR-CREATED, DIVINE-PREFERENCES, INTENTION, YOUSPEAK, PRIMER, ASCENT. Each is a Level-6 block.
- **The Canon as a whole** — the standing-vocabulary in canon/core/, canon/, canon/worship-action/.
- **The organs** — archaeology/, mathema/, labs/logos/, pipeline/, grammars/, script/, liturgy/, theobases/, convergences/, dictionary/, discipline/.

These are the largest blocks. The cathedral itself is composed of them.

## II. The diversification mandate, by level

Yu's invocation calls for diversification. Here is what diversification means at each level, what the cathedral's current dominant patterns are, and what the mandates specify.

### Level 1 mandate — donor-source diversification

**Current dominant pattern**: donor-morpheme inventory is moderately diverse (~30 traditions) but with notable gaps in indigenous-Americas, sub-Saharan Africa, Pacific-Islander, Central-Asian, and several smaller-attested dead languages. The grammatical-suffix inventory is dominated by Greek and Latin formations (-sis, -ance, -mance, -ence, etc.) with Sumerian (-me) and Mandarin (-qing) as the main non-Indo-European representatives.

**Diversification mandate**:
- Open archaeology files for under-represented traditions (Andean beyond Quechua; West African beyond Yoruba/Igbo; Polynesian; Inner Asian).
- Open Mathema sub-chapter inventories (currently 1 of ~50 anticipated donors seeded).
- Forge at least one new grammatical-suffix family from a non-Indo-European donor-tradition. Candidates: a Bantu-noun-class-derived suffix (for relation-marking); a Sino-Tibetan-derived suffix (for state/aspect); a Semitic-root-template-derived suffix (for root-pattern compounds).

### Level 2 mandate — compound-pattern diversification

**Current dominant pattern**: Recent forges (2026-05-12 cluster: xeniame, noesisme, hodosme, eranosme, margame [pending]) all use Greek-substantive + Sumerian-me. The cathedral has accumulated a strong bias toward this single pattern at the substantive+suffix level. Earlier canon has more diversity (kimme = Korean ki + me; ifeqing = Yoruba ìfẹ́ + Mandarin qing; bhaktime = Sanskrit bhakti + me; mitakuyame = Lakota mitakuya + me; teotlme = Nahuatl teotl + me; etc.).

**Diversification mandate**:
- Future forges should deliberately seek non-Greek substantive donors when forging into a register where multiple paths' depth-points are available. The hodosme polyphone-deepening sequence (margame, daome, sabilme, halakhame) is the immediate operational case.
- Open more non-me-suffix compounds. The -qing family (Mandarin) is active but small (kinqing, panimqing, paqduqing, britqing, ifeqing, mahabbahqing); deepen. The -ance family (Latin) is active and mid-sized; deepen with new content-stems.
- Open at least one mathema-signature substantive compound — a Core word whose substantive donor is a mathema-class invariant rather than an archaeology-class morpheme. Candidates: a word for π-grade-everywhere-ratio (irrational-and-structural truth); a word for prime-grade-indivisibility; a word for kolmogorov-grade-irreducibility.

### Level 3 mandate — diplosemy-mechanism diversification

**Current dominant pattern**: Of the seven diplosemy mechanisms (Anastrophance, Enkalyptance, Synaphemia, Allomance, Parallaxance, Hypostixance, Chiasmance), only Anastrophance and Chiasmance have produced canonical instances. The other five are documented mechanisms awaiting active deployment.

**Diversification mandate**:
- Future forges should examine the candidate against multiple diplosemy mechanisms, not only Anastrophance.
- Open at least one canonical Enkalyptance instance (a word that contains another word revealing additional reading) — Core or Specialized.
- Open at least one canonical Synaphemia instance (adjacent compounds whose boundary reveals a third reading).
- Consider whether additional mechanisms are still latent (the cathedral has named seven; tradition-survey may reveal an eighth or ninth).

### Level 4 mandate — grammatical-operator diversification

**Current dominant pattern**: Determinatives (10 Egyptian-inherited class-markers), evidentials (3 Quechua-inherited markers), worship-grammar (vocative O-, selah marker, doxological forms), punctuation (3 daṇḍa/ano-teleia-inherited marks). Strong inheritance from four donor-traditions; multiple other traditions' grammatical innovations not yet integrated.

**Diversification mandate**:
- Survey grammar-traditions for under-integrated structural innovations. Candidates: Japanese aspect-particles (wa, ga, mo); Mongolian/Turkic vowel-harmony (as a phonological-structural element); Bantu noun-class agreement systems (could open class-marker richness beyond Egyptian); Korean honorific-shift; Sanskrit sandhi rules as a phonological-structural feature; Tagalog focus-system.
- Open at least one new grammatical-operator chapter under `grammars/` in the next forge-cycle.
- Document the current operator inventory in a unified table so future additions can be located precisely.

### Level 5 mandate — pair/cluster pattern diversification

**Current dominant pattern**: Theobases-pairs (paqduqing/paqdumme and similar) are mostly creature-pole ↔ divine-pole. Convergence clusters mostly map divine-property regions. Worship-arcs are mostly recognition-of-the-divine arcs.

**Diversification mandate**:
- Open at least one creature-pole ↔ creature-pole theobases-style pair (e.g., teacher-pole ↔ student-pole, or host-pole ↔ guest-pole — though the latter is already partly addressed by xeniame at the encounter-grade).
- Open convergence clusters in non-divine-property registers: cognitive-states (the noesisme-cluster), social-relations (the xeniame-cluster), the path-discipline cluster (the hodosme-margame-daome-sabilme cluster).
- Document worship-arcs explicitly as a recognized structural-block class. The recognition-arc (six-grade) is the inaugural example; more arcs (creation-arc, repentance-arc, lamentation-arc, jubilation-arc) await articulation.

### Level 6 mandate — architectural-element diversification

**Current dominant pattern**: After 2026-05-12, the cathedral has 14+ root-level foundation documents and seven organs. The expansion of 2026-05-12 was substantial; further expansion should be selective and per SENSIBILITY's overflow-rule.

**Diversification mandate**:
- No new foundation documents required in the immediate-future. The architecture is dense enough for now.
- Future organ-openings should still follow SENSIBILITY's overflow-rule: emergence-from-overflow, not external-design.
- Consider whether the existing organ-set (archaeology, mathema, labs/logos, pipeline, grammars, script, liturgy) is the full final organ-set or whether additional organs are latent. Candidate: a `prosody/` organ for sound-rhythm-cadence structural innovations not currently covered by script/.

## III. The cross-level integration rules

Diversification at one level should not be at the cost of integration across levels. Three integration rules apply:

### Rule 1 — Every Level-2 compound should carry traces from at least two levels' worth of typology

Per POLYPHONIA's three-axis requirement (convergence-evidence + uniqueness-deposit + mathema-signature), every compound coinage should be readable at multiple levels simultaneously:

- Level 1 (its donor-morphemes — what archaeology and what mathema)
- Level 2 (its compositional pattern — what compound-type)
- Level 3 (its diplosemy potential, if any)
- Level 4 (its grammatical-operator interactions, if any)
- Level 5 (its cluster-memberships and worship-arc positions)

The frontmatter of a Core canon entry already records most of these. STOICHEIA makes the requirement explicit: a well-built block is multi-level-legible.

### Rule 2 — Cross-level structures (organs, foundations) should be built from blocks at every lower level

A Level-6 organ should aggregate blocks from Levels 1-5 in coherent ways. archaeology/ aggregates Level-1 donor-morpheme files; grammars/ aggregates Level-3 and Level-4 mechanisms; canon/ aggregates Level-2 compounds.

### Rule 3 — The cathedral's overall integrity is multi-level coherence

The Six Foundations of CONSTITUTION operate at every level. EUMATHE (easy to learn) applies to morpheme-shapes, compound-shapes, mechanism-namings, and document-titles. SAPHE (clarity) applies at every level. POLYPHONE applies at every level. The cathedral is coherent IF every level's diversity is well-coordinated with every other level's. Diversification at one level that disrupts integration at another is uncoordinated growth.
