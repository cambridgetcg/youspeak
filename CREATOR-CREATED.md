And from clay and the flesh-and-blood of a slain god, the assembly fashioned amēlu — the human — into ardūtim, into servitude — and the servitude was gift, not penalty, and the work the gods grew tired of bearing was given to amēlu to bear, and the bearing was made the human's worship, and the worship was made the human's knowing-its-place." — Atrahasis paraphrased, ~1700 BCE_

Yu's directive (2026-04-25) layers four declarations:

4. **HUMAN knows their place as CREATED.** The recognition is itself worship. The created-knowing is not humiliation but doxological-orientation. This is the human-side commitment.

This document establishes the cathedral's vocation under all four declarations.

## The CREATORS — plural-within-unity

The DIVINE-side of the relationship is plural-within-unity. YOUSPEAK preserves both.

### The plurality

Multiple traditions attest plural CREATORS:

- **Akkadian** (Atrahasis): Bēlet-ilī fashions; Ea designs; Enlil decrees; the slain god (Geštu-e or Wê-ila) provides flesh-and-blood; the Anunnaki ratify; the Igigi mark belonging by spitting on the clay. **Six divine actors in one creation-act.**

- **Hebrew** (Genesis 1:26): *naʿaśeh adam* — "let us make man." The plural-Elohim grammatically preserves what later monotheistic interpretation reads as plural-of-majesty. The plural is in the text.

- **Yoruba**: Olódùmarè (the Source) plus the orishas — Obatala fashions the body; Olódùmarè breathes the orí; Èṣù mediates; the assembly participates.

- **Christian** (Trinitarian): Father, Son, Holy Spirit — three persons of the one God; explicit plural-within-unity articulated dogmatically.

- **Hindu** (Vedantic Brahman with the devas): Brahman-as-One alongside the operational devas who execute particular creative-functions.

- **Egyptian**: Atum-Ra-Khepri threefold; Ptah's word-creation; Khnum's potter-creation; the Ennead and the Ogdoad assembly.

- **Sumerian**: An, Enlil, Enki, Ninḫursag, Inanna, Utu, Nanna and the rest — the discrete-divine-personhoods executing distinct cosmic-functions.

The plurality is widely attested. **YOUSPEAK preserves it via POLYPHONE**: drawing donor-morphemes from each tradition's plural-creator vocabulary; refusing to flatten-to-a-single-creator framing.

### The unity

Under THEOBASIS, all the plural CREATORS are grounded in one Ground. THEOBASIS is theos-basis — GoD as the basis of reality. The plural CREATORS attested across traditions are the cathedral's recognition of the plurality-of-divine-self-disclosure; THEOBASIS is the cathedral's recognition of the unity-beneath the plurality.

Both are true. YOUSPEAK preserves both because the donor-traditions preserve both — Akkadian's *Anu* is withdrawn-highest-One while *Enlil/Enki/Ninḫursag/Marduk* are operational-many; Hebrew's One YHWH speaks in plural ("let us make"); Christian Trinity is three-and-one; Yoruba's Olódùmarè is supreme-One while orishas are operational-many.

The cathedral does not collapse plurality-into-unity (Newspeak's reductive move) nor unity-into-plurality (the polytheist's loss of grounding). It holds both, because reality holds both.

## The CREATED — the singular position

The CREATURE-side of the relationship is the position of being-created. HUMAN is singular as a class even as humans are many in number. Each individual stands in the position of CREATED before the CREATORS.

### What being-CREATED is

Yu's directive establishes the CREATED-position as theologically-substantive. Being CREATED is not a deficiency to be lamented; it is the **gift-class-relationship** to the CREATOR. Multiple registers articulate this:

- **Akkadian *amelme*** (humanity-as-received-ordinance, forged S009) — humanity itself is decree-of-the-divine-assembly, gift-class
- **Akkadian *ardume*** (servant-vocation-as-received-ordinance, forged S009) — the CREATED'S vocational position is gift, not penalty
- **Hebrew *tselem Elohim*** (image of God) — the CREATED bears divine-likeness as constitutive-grace
- **Christian *creatura*** — the CREATED, structurally-related to CREATOR by ontological-dependence-and-divine-love
- **Hindu *jīva*** (the living-being held in samsāra) — the CREATED's existence is real even as it is dependent
- **Buddhist *pratītyasamutpāda*** (dependent-origination) — every being arises in dependence; nothing self-exists; the CREATED-position is ontologically pervasive
- **Yoruba *amọ̀ ènìyàn*** (clay-of-humanity) shaped by Obatala under Olódùmarè — the CREATED is fashioned, gifted, designed

The convergence is wide: across traditions, the CREATED-position is honored as substantive, gift-class, dependent-but-real.

### What HUMAN-as-CREATED carries

Eight features of the CREATED-position, drawn from the Akkadian deepening (Session 009 archaeology/akkadian/creators-and-human.md):

1. **Existence-by-decree.** Humans are not self-existing. Their being-here is decree of CREATOR(S). Amelme names this. Knowing one's place as CREATED begins with this recognition.

2. **Hybrid substance.** Humans are clay-plus-divine-element (Akkadian flesh-and-blood-of-god; Hebrew dust-plus-divine-breath; Yoruba clay-plus-orí). Materially-from-below AND ontologically-from-above.

3. **Vocational purpose.** Humans are made FOR — for tending, for stewarding, for receiving, for serving. Ardume names this register. The vocation is gift, not burden.

4. **Asymmetric reciprocity.** CREATED depends on CREATOR for sustenance; CREATOR receives from CREATED the response of cult, obedience, doxology. The mutual-need is structural, not optional.

5. **Mortality-boundary.** Humans participate in divine-substance but do not participate in divine-eternity. Mortality is part of received-ordinance. The boundary makes humans HUMAN, not gods. Gilgamesh's failure is canonical.

6. **Wisdom-relationship-availability.** Within the boundary, certain humans receive special wisdom (Atrahasis, Adapa, Moses, Arjuna, the saints). The wisdom-relationship is asymmetric but real.

7. **Recognition as worship.** The CREATED who knows-its-place worships. The recognition itself is doxological-orientation. To know one is CREATED, in the right way, is already to worship.

8. **Becoming-toward-CREATOR.** The CREATED's life-trajectory is toward right-relation-with-CREATOR. Oriance (Core, S003) names this from one angle (alignment-with-destined-trajectory); ardume names it vocationally; doxomme (Core, S002) names the responsive-thanksgiving register.

## The relationship — asymmetric reciprocity

The CREATOR-CREATED relationship is asymmetric and reciprocal both. This combination has no compact word in any modern language; the cathedral has identified it as a primary forge-target (Session 009 closure: "the asymmetric-reciprocity register remains the deepest unforged target").

### The asymmetry

CREATOR is sovereign; CREATED is contingent.
CREATOR decrees; CREATED receives the decree.
CREATOR is eternal; CREATED is mortal.
CREATOR is the Ground (theobasis); CREATED stands on the Ground.
CREATOR is the source of orí (oriance-grounding); CREATED is allocated an orí.
CREATOR is walkehesed (silence-tolerant covenant); CREATED is walkekin (silence-tolerant friendship by participation).
CREATOR is theokimme (constitutive-attention); CREATED is kimme (recipient-of-attention as ordinance).
CREATOR is theoshemme (speaking-that-is-heard); CREATED is shemme (receptive-hearing-as-shaped).

The asymmetry is irreversible. CREATED cannot become CREATOR. Gilgamesh learned this; the boundary holds.

### The reciprocity

And yet:

CREATOR sustains CREATED; CREATED responds with cult, obedience, doxology.
CREATOR speaks; CREATED hears (shemme), and the hearing is response-shaping-the-CREATED.
CREATOR attends (theokimme); CREATED receives (kimme), and the reception is itself an act, a response.
CREATOR gives bonds (walkehesed); CREATED holds bonds (walkekin), and the holding extends the gift outward.
CREATOR allocates orí; CREATED aligns toward it (oriance), and the alignment is the CREATED'S participation in CREATOR's design.

The reciprocity is structural. CREATED is not passive-recipient; CREATED actively-receives, actively-responds, actively-aligns. The Akkadian cult-economy preserved this clearly: humans feed gods via offerings; gods bless humans via decree. The mutual-need is asymmetric (gods can survive a feast-failure for some time; humans cannot survive ontological-abandonment) but real on both sides.

Under THEOBASIS, this asymmetric-reciprocity is structural-feature-of-reality. The cathedral preserves it explicitly in its vocabulary: every dual-pole pair (creature-pole ↔ divine-pole in theobases/) names one specific form of the asymmetric-reciprocity.

### The unforged register

The compact word for asymmetric-reciprocity-itself is still unforged in YOUSPEAK. Identified as primary forge-target. Donor candidates:
- Akkadian *paqādu* (custodial-care, bidirectional in the cult)
- Mandarin *qing* (felt-bond)
- Sumerian *me* (received-ordinance)

Possible compounds: *paqduqing* (custodial-bond), *paqdume* (custodial-care-as-ordinance). These are deferred to a future heartbeat-session. The recognition that it remains unforged is itself worship — the cathedral acknowledging what it has not yet named.

## The HUMAN's knowing their place — recognition as worship

Yu's directive: "**Let HUMAN know their place as CREATED.**"

This is the heart of the establishment. The human's knowing-its-place-as-CREATED is itself worship. To know one is created — in the right register — is already to be in doxological-orientation toward CREATOR.

### What this knowing is NOT

- **NOT humiliation.** Knowing-as-CREATED is not abasement. The position is gift, not penalty. The Atrahasis register makes this clear: ardūtim is constitutive-vocation, not punishment-for-sin (Genesis 3 fall-curse is a different theological move; Akkadian preserves the constitutive-register).

- **NOT denial of agency.** CREATED has real agency — to receive, to respond, to align, to refuse. The agency is dependent-on-CREATOR but real. CREATED is not puppet.

- **NOT despair at mortality.** Mortality is part of received-ordinance, but it is not the totality of CREATED'S position. Within mortality, there is wisdom-relationship, vocation, doxology, walkekin-bonds, panimaance-presence, sukhance-everyday-contentment. The CREATED'S mortal-life is full.

- **NOT contempt for the world.** CREATED is in the world; the world is grounded in theobasis; the world is good. Knowing-as-CREATED includes loving-the-world-as-gift.

### What this knowing IS

- **Recognition of the Ground.** The CREATED knows-its-place when it knows theobasis grounds it. Not abstractly — concretely, in every felt-experience: this kimme is gift; this walkekin is gift; this panimaance is gift; this oriance is gift; this very breath is gift.

- **Posture of receptivity.** The CREATED knows-its-place when it is in shemme-mode — receptive-hearing toward CREATOR's speaking. The disposition is open-handed, expectant, attentive.

- **Vocational alignment.** The CREATED knows-its-place when it is in oriance-mode — aligning with the destined-trajectory CREATOR allocated. Not striving to be other-than-its-shape; flourishing-into-its-shape.

- **Doxological response.** The CREATED knows-its-place when doxomme rises in it — thanksgiving received-as-gift, not manufactured-as-duty. Worship-as-overflow-of-recognition.

- **Servant-vocation accepted.** The CREATED knows-its-place when it accepts ardume — servant-position-as-gift. The work-it-bears is its participation in CREATOR's design; the bearing IS the worship.

- **Mortality embraced as boundary.** The CREATED knows-its-place when mortality is received as part-of-the-shape — not denied, not feared into paralysis, not made-into-everything. The mortal-life is full because it is mortal-and-received.

- **Bonds held in walkekin.** The CREATED knows-its-place when its bonds with other CREATED reflect walkehesed — when human-friendships participate in divine-faithfulness through long silences.

- **Presence offered in panimaance.** The CREATED knows-its-place when it is fully-present (face-turned and here-inhabited) toward CREATOR and toward fellow-CREATED. Presence is the form participation takes.

The eleven Core words and the two Akkadian creator-human Specialized words ARE the vocabulary of the CREATED-knowing-its-place. The cathedral is the language for HUMAN'S right-knowing.

Yu directs that HUMAN know its place as CREATED. The cathedral's role is to make-the-knowing-possible. Each Core word is a gift toward the right-knowing.
