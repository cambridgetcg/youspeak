# CONFLUENCE — why paths overlap and where uniqueness originates

> _"When two paths cross, walkers from each receive deposits from the other. Every loanword is a place where two paths touched and one received."_ — THE-PATH.md §III

> _"Where two or more linguistic-religious traditions independently name a divine-property without any single tradition compact-naming it, the convergence itself is evidence."_ — convergences/README.md

## Opening

Yu's fourth invocation of 2026-05-12 names two questions that are duals of each other.

The first: **why do language paths overlap?** Different tongues walked independently for thousands of years, yet they share more than chance permits — shared vocabulary, shared categories, shared structural patterns, occasionally the same compact-name for the same fine concept. The convergences are not noise; they are data. What causes them?

The second: **where does each path's uniqueness come from?** Every tongue contributes what no other tongue does. Greek's philosophical-precision; Yoruba's orisha-cosmology; Quechua's evidentials; Sumerian's *me*-cosmic-ordinances; Hebrew's covenant-grammar; Polynesian's navigation-vocabulary. The uniqueness is the cathedral's donor-value. What generates it?

The two questions are paired because they describe the same structural reality from two sides. **Overlap is what makes the realm one across many tongues; uniqueness is what each tongue's particular walking has discovered of the realm that the others have not yet reached.** Both are required for the cathedral's POLYPHONE practice: without overlap, the cathedral would have no convergence-evidence; without uniqueness, it would have no donor-distinction worth integrating.

This document articulates the structural causes of both. It draws on linguistics, cognitive science, anthropology, and the cathedral's own existing empirical data (60+ archaeology paths; 11 convergence-files; 66+ canon entries). It reads both phenomena under [THE-REALM.md](THE-REALM.md) and [NOEMA.md](NOEMA.md): all language is projection of one realm; overlap is multi-angle convergence onto a single realm-region; uniqueness is the projection-angle no other path has yet found.

The closing sections name implications for the cathedral's forge-discipline and propose operational next moves.

## I. The two questions are duals

State the duality explicitly so the rest of the analysis runs cleanly.

A language path is, per THE-PATH.md, a collective-exploratory walking through time toward the realm of meaning. Each path projects (per THE-REALM.md) the realm from its particular angle. Each tongue's path-history is the trajectory of its projection-angle's refinement over generations.

**Overlap** between two paths is what they share — vocabulary, grammar, semantic-categories, structural-patterns, occasionally compact compact-names for the same concepts. It is observable at every scale: phonetic similarities, cognate-roots, parallel grammatical structures, lexicalization-overlaps, philosophical-convergences.

**Uniqueness** of a path is what only that path contributes — concepts no other path has named at the same compactness; emphases-of-distinction the others have left implicit; surface-forms with no parallel; cultural-experiential domains accumulated through the path's specific walking.

Under the projection-account, both are precisely characterizable:

- **Overlap = multi-projection of the same realm-region.** Where two paths' projections cover overlapping realm-territory, what they share IS that shared realm-region rendered by both. The amount and depth of overlap tracks how much realm-territory both have reached.
- **Uniqueness = single-projection of an otherwise-unreached realm-region.** Where one path's projection covers realm-territory no other path has reached, what is unique is that solitary projection-coverage. The depth of uniqueness tracks how distant the realm-region is from anywhere any other path has projected.

The two are not opposing forces. They are the same realm-and-projection structure seen from two sides: where the projection-circles intersect, there is overlap; where one projection-circle extends beyond every other's reach, there is uniqueness. POLYPHONE, under the cathedral's discipline, makes both visible: it brings multiple paths' projections together so that overlap reveals multi-witness regions AND each path's unique extension reveals where only that walker has reached.

The remainder of this document asks: what STRUCTURAL CAUSES generate overlap, and what STRUCTURAL CAUSES generate uniqueness? Both questions admit precise answers.

## II. The eight causes of overlap

Overlap among independent language paths is not chance. Eight distinguishable causes generate it, falling into four families.

### Family A — Anthropological causes (shared makers)

**Cause 1 — Shared cognitive equipment.** All humans share the perceptual and cognitive primitives that come with the *Homo sapiens* brain: agency-detection, causation-inference, object-permanence, face-recognition, kin-recognition, approximate-numerosity, color-discrimination at the visible-light wavelengths, basic spatial-orientation. These shared primitives generate overlapping vocabulary across genetically-unrelated languages. Every language can say *agent*, *cause*, *kind*, *number*, *color*, *left/right*. Anna Wierzbicka's *Natural Semantic Metalanguage* program has documented ~65 semantic primitives that appear in every language tested. The primitives are not borrowed; they are independently generated by the shared cognitive architecture.

**Cause 2 — Shared embodiment.** All humans have bodies that breathe, eat, sleep, hurt, give birth, age, die. Body-based metaphor is universal. Lakoff and Johnson's *Metaphors We Live By* documents conceptual metaphors that recur across unrelated tongues: UP-IS-GOOD, IN-IS-CONTROL, LIFE-IS-JOURNEY, ARGUMENT-IS-WAR, IDEAS-ARE-OBJECTS. The recurrence is not borrowing; it is bodies projecting similarly into abstract conceptual space. Every language has body-part-derived prepositions; every language has temperature-emotion mappings (warm-affection, cold-distance); every language uses motion-verbs for thought-processes.

**Cause 3 — Shared physical environment.** All humans live on one planet, under one sun, with one moon, under one gravity, with the same water, air, and electromagnetic spectrum. Day and night, seasons (in temperate zones), the moon's phases, the stars' apparent motion — these generate convergent vocabulary across every tongue independent of contact. Every language names *light, dark, water, fire, air, earth, day, night, sky, sun, moon*. The naming is not coordinated; it is each path responding to the same observable world.

### Family B — Historical causes (transmitted overlap)

**Cause 4 — Common ancestral language.** Genetically-related languages share vocabulary by inheritance. Within the Indo-European family, *father* descends from PIE \*ph₂tér through Sanskrit *pitár*, Greek *patḗr*, Latin *pater*, Persian *pedar*, English *father* — six tongues separated by 5000+ years still recognizably bearing the same word. Within Afro-Asiatic, the Semitic root *š-l-m* (peace, wholeness) appears in Hebrew *shalom*, Aramaic *shlama*, Arabic *salām*, Akkadian *šalāmu*, Ge'ez *salām*. Inherited overlap is form-AND-meaning together; the cognate carries both. Sister-paths walked separately but from a single starting-point.

**Cause 5 — Language contact (borrowing).** Paths in contact deposit on each other. Greek-into-Latin (philosophical vocabulary), Sanskrit-into-Pali, Arabic-into-Persian-and-Urdu-and-Swahili-and-Spanish, Latin-into-every-Romance-language-and-then-English, Chinese-into-Japanese-and-Korean-and-Vietnamese, Indigenous-languages-into-Spanish-and-Portuguese-in-the-Americas, every-language-into-every-trade-language. Borrowing-overlap is form-following-meaning: the borrowed word carries its meaning across the path-meeting and the recipient-path domesticates the new form. THE-PATH.md §III names this directly: *"Every loanword is a place where two paths touched and one received."*

**Cause 6 — Convergent invention.** Where two paths face the same problem, they sometimes find similar solutions independently. Writing was invented at least three times independently — Sumerian cuneiform (~3200 BCE), Egyptian hieroglyphs (~3100 BCE), Chinese (~1200 BCE), and Mesoamerican glyphic systems (~600 BCE). Each invented a different system but each independently reached the same fundamental insight: graphic marks can encode speech. The numeral-zero was invented separately in India (~5th c. CE), Mesoamerica (~3rd c. BCE), and Babylon (~3rd c. BCE) — three independent paths reaching the same mathematical structure because the structure was real and reachable. Convergent invention overlap is meaning-without-form: the structures match but the surface-forms don't.

### Family C — Structural causes (forced overlap)

**Cause 7 — Universal phenomena requiring naming.** Some phenomena are so universal that every speaking-community must name them: birth, death, eating, sleeping, hunger, thirst, hot, cold, light, dark, water, fire, mother, father, child, body, head, hand, foot. The naming is forced; you cannot run a human community without these names. The overlap is the joint response to common necessity. The forms vary; the named-phenomena converge. This is structural overlap — overlap of the *what's named* set, even where the *how it's named* sets diverge wildly.

### Family D — Theological cause (one realm, one Ground)

**Cause 8 — The realm is one.** Under THEOBASIS, reality is sustained by GoD; reality's relational structure renders the realm of meanings (NOEMA.md). All language paths project the same realm. THE-REALM.md §V articulates this as "computed tomography of the divine": multiple projections from different angles, of one source. **The deepest reason language paths overlap is that they are all reaching for the same thing.** Where two paths converge on a fine concept — *cosmic-truth-order* in six independent traditions, *received-ordinance-as-divine-gift* across multiple religious vocabularies, *covenant-as-felt-bond* in Hebrew-Arabic-Akkadian-Indo-European — the convergence is the realm signing the projections.

This cause does not displace the seven prior causes; it grounds them. Shared cognition is shared because cognitive equipment is part of the Ground's gift; shared embodiment is shared for the same reason; shared environment is the Ground's reality made physically common; even contact and convergent-invention occur in a world whose intelligibility derives from the Ground. The eighth cause is what the prior seven are aspects of.

**The overlap-uniqueness syzygy.** Per PANSYZYGY.md §III Syzygy 12 (opened 2026-05-13), CONFLUENCE's central recognition that overlap and uniqueness are paired-aspects (the eight causes ↔ the eight origins, dual under one structure per §IV below) is structurally a syzygy: two distinct grounds (overlap-causes; uniqueness-origins) united in one POLYPHONE-discipline-structure (the cathedral's recognition that paths must be honored for both shared and distinctive), with the dual-pairing-itself constitutive. The cathedral's POLYPHONE is the polyphone-syzygy operationalized.

## III. The eight origins of uniqueness

Where paths diverge in their projection-coverage — where one path has reached realm-territory others have not — eight structural origins explain it. Like the overlap-causes, these fall into four families.

### Family A — Material origins (where the path was walked)

**Origin 1 — Geographic isolation period.** Paths walked in long isolation accumulate distinctive vocabulary. The Tibetan plateau, the Australian continent, the Pacific archipelagos, the Andean altiplano, the sub-Saharan interior — each gave its language-communities centuries-to-millennia of relative isolation in which to walk paths that no other walkers crossed. The uniqueness yielded includes Tibetan's bardo-vocabulary (a sustained meditation-on-dying tradition), Australian Aboriginal dreamtime-cosmology, Polynesian navigation-vocabulary (encoded into oral epic tradition), Quechua's evidential system (mandatory marking of how-you-know), Lakota's relational-cosmology (the *mitakuye oyasin* "all my relations" frame). Isolation gave each path room to walk where no one else was walking.

**Origin 2 — Specific environmental conditions.** A path develops vocabulary for what its walkers must talk about given their life-conditions. The Sami have many words for snow because they live with snow; the Bedouin have many words for camels because they live with camels; coastal navigators have many words for waves and winds; agricultural societies have many words for soil-states and growth-stages. **The environment shapes the vocabulary-emphases.** This is not Whorfian determinism but Whorfian influence at the lexical-density level.

### Family B — Cultural origins (what the path has had to say)

**Origin 3 — Specific cultural concerns.** Each community has things it must talk about because of its life-organization. The Yoruba developed elaborate orisha-vocabulary because of centuries of dense religious practice with the orisha. The Greek polis incubated philosophical-precision-vocabulary because the polis required argumentation. The Vedic ritualists developed precise mantra-state vocabulary because the ritual required it. The Hebrew prophetic tradition developed covenant-and-fidelity vocabulary because that was the heart of the tradition. The Sufi orders developed annihilation-and-subsistence vocabulary because the contemplative path required naming those states. Cultural-concern uniqueness is vocabulary-density at the location of the community's deepest sustained practice.

**Origin 4 — Sacred-text fixity.** A canonical sacred-text fixes vocabulary and register for centuries. The Rigveda fixed Vedic Sanskrit; the Hebrew Bible fixed biblical Hebrew; the Qur'an fixed Classical Arabic; the Lotus Sutra fixed Classical Chinese-Buddhist register; the Pāli Canon fixed Pāli; the Avesta fixed Avestan. After fixity, all subsequent walking on that path has the canonical-text as ground-truth, and the path's distinctive contributions cluster around what the canonical-text emphasized. **Sacred-text uniqueness is fixity-multiplied-by-centuries.**

**Origin 5 — Intellectual neighbors.** Who a language has been in conversation with shapes its distinctive contributions. Classical Greek-in-conversation-with-Egyptian-and-Phoenician-and-Babylonian developed differently from Classical Greek-in-conversation-with-Latin. Medieval Arabic-absorbing-Greek-philosophy developed differently from Modern Arabic. Sanskrit-in-conversation-with-Pāli developed Mahāyāna registers; Sanskrit-in-conversation-with-Persian developed Indo-Persian literary registers. **Each path's neighbors determine which of its angles received the cross-pressure of dialogue.**

### Family C — Structural origins (what the path can compactly say)

**Origin 6 — Phonotactic constraints.** Each language has its own sound-system. The constraints make certain compounds easy and others impossible. Mandarin's tonal system allows monosyllabic differentiation that Indo-European tongues lack; Greek's vowel-richness supports philosophical morphology (the *-sis, -tēs, -on, -ma, -isma* family) that Mandarin's syllabic constraint cannot directly replicate; Bantu's tone-and-noun-class systems allow density of distinction at the noun that English flattens; Arabic's tri-consonantal-root system allows semantic-family-generation that European tongues handle laboriously. Phonotactic uniqueness shapes what each path can compactly project from the realm.

**Origin 7 — Grammatical typology.** SOV vs SVO vs VSO; agglutinative vs isolating vs fusional; topic-prominent vs subject-prominent; evidential-mandatory vs evidential-optional; tense-prominent vs aspect-prominent. The grammatical typology shapes what concepts can be said compactly. Evidentially-mandatory languages (Quechua, Aymara, Tuyuca, Korean) compactly distinguish witnessed-from-reported-from-inferred in every assertion; non-evidential languages must paraphrase. Aspect-mandatory languages (Russian, Greek) compactly carry completion-vs-progression distinctions; aspect-optional languages must specify. **Each path's grammatical typology is a projection-engineering choice — typically unconscious — that shapes the path's distinctive reach.**

### Family D — The walking-itself (what the path has discovered)

**Origin 8 — The path's particular discoveries.** Per THE-PATH.md §IV: *"each language's path just is its history of refining its projection-angle. Greek's path discovered an extraordinary precision for philosophical-distinctions because generations of Greek-thinkers walked toward that precision."* The deepest origin of uniqueness is the path's actual walking. The path is where the walkers walked. Each tongue's distinctive contribution is the deposit of millennia of speakers, writers, ritualists, philosophers, poets, prophets, mothers, scribes, storytellers refining their projection-angle by use. The walking IS the uniqueness. No prior cause fully determines what a path will discover; the walking discovers.

This eighth origin is the deepest because it is the path-history itself irreducibly. Geography sets the stage; cultural-concerns provide the topics; phonotactics shape what compounds are easy; grammar shapes what compresses well; sacred-texts fix the canonical register; neighbors apply cross-pressure. But within all those givens, the path's actual walking — the millions of footsteps of millions of mostly-anonymous speakers — is what discovered, refined, retained, and stabilized the path's distinctive contributions. **The path's uniqueness is the path itself.**

## IV. The duality of causes and origins

The eight overlap-causes and the eight uniqueness-origins are not separate lists. They pair up. For each overlap-cause, there is a corresponding uniqueness-origin that operates on the same structural axis but produces divergence rather than convergence.

| Overlap-cause | Uniqueness-origin |
|---|---|
| 1. Shared cognition | Cognitive emphasis specific to a tradition (e.g., Quechua's evidentiality, Aymara's time-reversal) |
| 2. Shared embodiment | Specific embodied life-experience (maritime navigation vs land-pastoralism vs urban literacy) |
| 3. Shared environment | Specific environmental conditions (snow, desert, jungle, mountain) |
| 4. Common ancestry | Post-split independent development (sister languages diverging) |
| 5. Language contact | Resistance-to-borrowing (what a tradition refused to absorb, often becoming distinctive) |
| 6. Convergent invention | Path-specific solutions to common problems (different writing-systems, different numeral-systems) |
| 7. Universal phenomena | Specific framings of universals (different conceptions of death, love, family, time, sacred) |
| 8. Realm is one | Each path's particular projection-angle into the one realm |

The duality is structural, not accidental. **Every cause-of-overlap has, on the other side, a corresponding origin-of-uniqueness.** Shared cognition produces shared categories AND each path emphasizes different sub-categories within them. Shared embodiment produces UP-IS-GOOD AND specific embodiment-types each emphasize different sub-domains. Common ancestry produces cognates AND post-split divergence. The realm is one AND each path's particular angle differs.

This is the cathedral's deep finding: **overlap and uniqueness are not opposing forces; they are co-products of the same structural reality.** A language path is what happens when a community of cognizers-with-bodies-in-environments walks its history of projection-refinement. The shared substrate generates overlap; the particular walking generates uniqueness. Both are real; both are necessary; both are what POLYPHONE practices honoring.

## V. The realm-and-projection reading consolidated

Under [THE-REALM.md](THE-REALM.md) and [NOEMA.md](NOEMA.md), all of the above reduces to a clean structural picture.

The realm of meanings is higher-dimensional than any single linguistic-projection captures. Each language path is a projection-system, walked over time, whose accumulated coverage of the realm is the path's footprint.

- **Where paths' footprints overlap** — multiple projections cover the same realm-region from different angles. The amount of overlap measures how much shared coverage the paths have reached. The CAUSES of overlap operate on different axes: some cause overlap because the projection-systems started from shared substrates (cognition, embodiment, environment); some cause overlap because the projections came into contact and exchanged content (borrowing); some cause overlap because the projection-systems independently reached for the same structures (convergent-invention, universal-phenomena); the deepest cause is that the realm is one and all paths are walking toward it.

- **Where a path's footprint extends beyond every other's** — that path has projected realm-territory no other has yet reached. The depth of uniqueness measures how far this projection-coverage extends past others. The ORIGINS of uniqueness operate on different axes: some originate in the where (geography, environment); some originate in the what-the-path-needed-to-say (culture, sacred-text, neighbors); some originate in the projection-engineering (phonotactics, typology); the deepest origin is the walking itself — what this particular path's walkers, over generations, refined into projection-coverage no one else has matched.

The reading consolidates: **the eight overlap-causes are eight reasons two or more paths CAN converge on the same realm-region; the eight uniqueness-origins are eight reasons individual paths develop projection-angles others don't.** Both are real; both are causal; both belong to the same theoretical structure.

## VI. The cathedral's empirical instances

The analysis can be checked against the cathedral's existing data. Three illustrative cases.

### Case 1 — Cosmic-truth-order (six-tradition convergence)

`convergences/cosmic-truth-order.md` records six independent traditions naming the same realm-region: Vedic *ṛta*, Avestan *asha*, Egyptian *ma'at*, Hebrew *emet*, Akkadian *kittu*, Greek *alētheia*. The convergence spans three genetic families (Indo-European Indo-Iranian, Indo-European Hellenic, Afro-Asiatic Semitic, Afro-Asiatic non-Semitic).

Reading through the overlap-causes:
- **Cause 1 (shared cognition)**: truth-tracking is a cognitive primitive; every cognizing-community must distinguish reliable from unreliable assertions.
- **Cause 3 (shared environment)**: cosmic-order — sun rising, seasons recurring, gravity holding — is environmentally visible to every community; the cosmic-order *exists* as a fact each path's walkers encounter.
- **Cause 7 (universal phenomena)**: every governance-tradition must name justice; every priesthood must name what-is-right; the *what* converges across the traditions.
- **Cause 8 (one realm)**: the convergence's strength — six traditions, three genetic families, no plausible contact-borrowing for the core concept — is precisely the realm signing the projections. The deepest cause is operative here.

Reading through the uniqueness-origins, each tradition's specific contribution to the convergence is distinct:
- **Vedic *ṛta***: emphasizes cosmic-path (the gods uphold ṛta) — origin in Vedic-ritual-tradition focus
- **Avestan *asha***: sharpens the asha-vs-druj polarity — origin in Zoroastrian dualist-theology
- **Egyptian *ma'at***: personifies as goddess with funerary-judgment scene — origin in Egyptian afterlife-cosmology
- **Hebrew *emet***: foregrounds firmness-and-faithfulness (the aleph-mem-tav alphabet-spanning) — origin in Hebrew prophetic-covenant tradition
- **Akkadian *kittu***: pairs with *mīšaru* (justice) as twin-foundation of governance — origin in Akkadian royal-ideology
- **Greek *alētheia***: foregrounds un-concealment (the *a-lēthē* "not-forgotten") — origin in Greek philosophical-attention

The convergence is multi-witness; each witness has its own walked path producing its own emphasis on the shared concept.

### Case 2 — Yoruba *orí* and *àṣẹ* (uniqueness-dominant case)

The Yoruba contributions to the cathedral (canon: *oriance*, *ifeqing*, *àṣẹme*) draw on concepts that have no exact match in other traditions the cathedral has surveyed. *Orí* = the destined-head (the inner-self-with-trajectory); *àṣẹ* = the spoken-word-that-effects (performative speech-power); *ìfẹ́* = relational-warmth-that-expands.

Reading the uniqueness-origins:
- **Origin 1 (isolation period)**: Yoruba-speaking communities developed in West Africa with deep continuous tradition; isolation was relative but the path's character is its own.
- **Origin 3 (specific cultural concerns)**: the orisha-tradition required precise vocabulary for divination, destiny, spoken-power; *orí* and *àṣẹ* are products of the Ifá-tradition's centuries of practice.
- **Origin 4 (sacred-text)**: the Ifá corpus, oral-canonical, fixed the vocabulary of divination and destiny.
- **Origin 8 (walking)**: centuries of Ifá-priests refining the concepts of destiny, head-soul, and word-power produced the precision *orí* carries.

The cathedral's three Yoruba canon-entries draw on a path whose uniqueness is real and substantial. No European-language path has produced *orí*'s precision because no European-language path walked the Ifá-divination tradition for centuries. The uniqueness is the path's walking made visible at the lexicon level.

### Case 3 — Tocharian *walke* (uniqueness-from-extinct-path)

Tocharian B (Tarim Basin, ~500–900 CE, extinct) contributes *walke* (long in time or distance; the caravan-time register). The cathedral uses it in *walkekin* — friendship-preserved-through-long-silence.

Reading the uniqueness-origins:
- **Origin 1 (isolation period)**: Tocharian-speakers walked the Silk Road's central-Asian segment; the path was geographically distinctive.
- **Origin 2 (environmental conditions)**: the Silk Road's caravan-pace shaped what Tocharian needed to name; long-duration travel was lived reality.
- **Origin 8 (walking)**: the Tocharian path's distinctive contribution is precisely what its walking produced. The path is extinct; the deposit remains.

Tocharian uniqueness is **uniqueness-from-now-extinct-walking**. The cathedral's *archaeology/tocharian/* preserves the deposit; *walkekin* carries the deposit into the cathedral's own walking. This is THE-PATH.md §X.3's discipline of receiving the under-credited: extinct paths cannot defend their own contributions; the cathedral must.

## VII. Implications for the cathedral's forge-strategy

The analysis directs concrete forge-discipline choices.

### When the forge-target is an overlap-region

If a forge-target's realm-region is reached by multiple paths (convergence-evidence exists), the forge should:

1. **Draw donors from genetic-family-independent traditions** — to maximize the projection-angle-diversity that POLYPHONE-as-computed-tomography requires (THE-REALM §V).
2. **Cite the convergence explicitly** — link the canon entry to a `convergences/` file recording the multi-witness data.
3. **Honor each donor-tradition's distinctive emphasis** — the canon entry's "Full sense" should articulate what each donor specifically brings; convergence does not mean homogenization.
4. **Carry the mathema_signature's convergence_cardinality field** — operationalizing the evidence-grade per [INTEGRATION.md](INTEGRATION.md) Block 8.

This is what *eurekame* (10-tradition convergence; highest Core score 8.65), *maatme* (6-tradition cosmic-truth-order), *shemme* (7-tradition hearing-as-shaping) already exemplify. The discipline now becomes explicit.

### When the forge-target is a unique-discovery

If a forge-target's realm-region has been reached only by one tradition (or a small cluster), the forge should:

1. **Draw the primary donor from the discovering tradition** — the path that uniquely walked into this realm-region.
2. **Add a secondary donor for HARMONE-balance and POLYPHONE-presence** — typically a YOUSPEAK-suffix family member (`-me`, `qing-`, `-ance`, `kin-`) that situates the unique concept in the cathedral's compositional system.
3. **Document the uniqueness-origin** — the canon entry's etymology should articulate what about the discovering-path's history produced this particular discovery.
4. **Open or extend the donor-tradition's archaeology** — if the path has been thinly archived in the cathedral, the forge-discipline is to deepen the archaeology so future borrowings can be more discerning.

This is what *walkekin* (Tocharian *walke* + English *kin*; Tocharian uniqueness preserved as caravan-time-register), *àṣẹme* (Yoruba *àṣẹ* + Sumerian *me*; Yoruba performative-word-power preserved), *teotlme* (Classical Nahuatl *teotl* + Sumerian *me*; Nahuatl divine-quality preserved) exemplify.

### When the forge-target is a contact-overlap-but-tradition-divergent region

Some targets are reached by multiple traditions BUT each tradition's projection diverges meaningfully (e.g., the asha-ṛta cognate pair where both descend from Indo-Iranian root \*r̥-ta- but each emphasizes differently). The forge-discipline is the cognate-rail strategy:

1. **Forge each emphasis separately if both are load-bearing** — the cathedral now has both *ashame* (Specialized 7.20) and *rtame* (Specialized 7.20). Cognate paths can yield two YOUSPEAK words when the divergence is real.
2. **Cite the cognate-relation in both entries** — preserving the shared-root genealogy.
3. **Carry both donors' uniqueness without collapsing into one** — POLYPHONE does not flatten.

### When the forge-target's overlap-evidence is unclear

Some forge-candidates have a single tradition's emphasis but the cathedral cannot yet tell whether other traditions also reach the same realm-region. The discipline:

1. **Surface the question in the convergence-research** — open `convergences/<property>.md` as a forge-status-pending stub; investigate.
2. **Use Pattern 1 (Cross-Tradition Overlay) before forging** — METHOD.md's primary discovery-pattern is exactly the convergence-investigation.
3. **Defer forging until the overlap-question has been clarified** — better to refine-pending than to canonize prematurely.

## IX. The tool extension

[pipeline/paths.py](pipeline/paths.py) gains two natural modes:

### `paths.py --overlap T1 T2 [T3 ...]`

Given two or more donor-traditions, compute their overlap:

- **Morpheme-level overlap**: morphemes in `script/morphemes.json` whose meanings have keyword-overlap across the traditions.
- **Canon-level overlap**: canon entries whose donor-tradition set includes all of the queried traditions.
- **Convergence-file overlap**: convergences/<file>.md entries whose `attestations` list includes all of the queried traditions.

Output is a structured overlap-report. Use case: forge-discipline — when contemplating a multi-tradition compound, the tool surfaces what convergence-territory already exists between the candidate donors.

### `paths.py --uniqueness T`

Given a single tradition, compute its uniqueness:

- **Sole-donor canon entries**: canon entries whose only donor is T (T standing alone in the compound).
- **Tradition-exclusive morphemes**: morphemes where T is the *only* tongue with that meaning-keyword across the inventory.
- **Unmatched archaeology concepts**: concepts surfaced in archaeology/<T>/ that don't appear in any other tradition's archaeology.

Output is a structured uniqueness-report. Use case: forge-discipline — when the cathedral wants to honor a tradition's distinctive contribution, the tool surfaces what only this tradition has named.

These two modes complete the per-path operational view. `--summary` and `--gaps` showed where each path stands; `<tradition>` showed a single path in detail; `--overlap` and `--uniqueness` will show how paths relate to one another. The full picture becomes queryable.
