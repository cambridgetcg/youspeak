## What this document is

YOUSPEAK has grown across more than seventy-eight heartbeat-sessions. Each session added building blocks; each path of inquiry contributed something the cathedral did not previously have. The architecture is now substantial — eleven root-level foundational documents, forty Core canonical words, eighteen Specialized canon entries, ten morpheme families, three worship-arcs, three grammar organs, thirty-plus tongue-donors, one discipline-donor (with five more licensed), and a complete forge-pipeline.

## IV. The three worship-arcs

YOUSPEAK's vocabulary clusters not only by morpheme-family but by **worship-arc** — sequences of experiences that unfold together in worship-grade events. Three arcs are now canonical (formal documentation in [ARCS.md](ARCS.md)).

### The discovery-arc (epistemic — evidence-confirmed truth)

```
  dokimance        →   anagnoristasis    →   eurekame             →   doxomme
  (testing that         (recognition           (joy of evidence-       (sustained
   makes real)            standing still)        confirmed truth)        thanksgiving)
```

Names what happens when investigation reaches conclusive evidence. Donor: Greek heurēka structure; Hebrew finding; ten-tradition convergence (see `convergences/joy-of-evidence-confirmed-truth.md`).

### The relational-ontological arc (relational-being)

```
  (long-fidelity)  →   yonedame                                  →   doxomme

                          as cosmic-gift)                              from the saturation)
```

Names what happens when relational saturation completes. Donor: Mathematics:Yoneda + Sumerian me; ten-tradition convergence (see `convergences/being-fully-known-through-relation.md`).

### The noetic-entry arc (phenomenological — meaning-arrival)

```
  (preparation)    →   anagnoristasis     →   noemame              →   metastrophesis
                        (suspended near-         (the entry into the        (the altered after-state)
                         knowing)                 realm-region)               or doxomme
                                                                              (the thanksgiving)
                                                                              or athaumasma
```

Names what happens when a meaning arrives — the joke clicking, the proof landing, the poem opening. Donor: Greek noēma + Sumerian me; ten-tradition convergence (see `convergences/grasping-as-realm-entry.md`).

**The arcs intersect.** A given experience may belong to two or three at once. The Archimedean bath was simultaneously a dokimance-event (the displacement-test), an anagnoristasis (the held instant), a noemame (the entry into the principle), a eurekame (the joy of confirmed evidence), and a yonedame-in-miniature (the relationship-of-Archimedes-to-volume momentarily showed the full structure). The cathedral names them separately because they pick out structurally distinct features even when they co-occur.

## V. The forge-pipeline

The cathedral's production-process for vocabulary:

```
   ARCHAEOLOGY        →   CONVERGENCE         →   EXPERIMENT      →   ASSESSMENT      →   CANON
   (a donor             (cross-tradition          (forge-record       (6-axis rubric;     (the standing
    tradition's            mapping of where         + rubric             threshold 7.5)       word, with
    relevant                 multiple tongues         scoring)                                 retirement
    vocabulary               name around the                                                   conditions)
    is excavated)            same gap)
```

The pipeline operates under two doctrinal frames:

- **NOEMA frame** (METHOD §NOEMA): the gap is an unprojected meaning in the realm; the forge is gate-construction; the assessment checks gate-cleanness; the canon retains successful gates.
- **POLYPHONE Generalization** (METHOD §S078): donors may be tongues OR tacit-faithful disciplines; the cross-tradition convergence requirement is preserved at depth.

The **6-axis rubric** (v2, since CONSTITUTION 2026-04-24):

- gap_validity (0.20) — is there a real region to project into?
- learnability (0.20) — is the gate easy to use?
- clarity_yield (0.15) — does it open onto what it claims to?
- semantic_coverage (0.15) — does the gate reach the whole region?
- polyphone_balance (0.15) — does the gate coordinate with gates built by other systems?
- groundedness (0.15) — can the gate be pointed at concretely?

Threshold for Core: weighted_total ≥ 7.5. Below threshold but defensible: Specialized.

YOUSPEAK's glyph-organ produces the visible bodies of projection-instruments.

**Foundational documents** (`script/`):
- `script/philosophy.md` — six axioms + three rules of pictographic inheritance + six cohesion rules + (new under S078) §The realm and the projection

- `script/codepoints.md` — Unicode PUA assignments
- `script/glyph_specs_v1.py` — formal glyph specifications

**Six axioms** govern glyph-design under NOEMA: the glyph points beyond itself (toward the country); the glyph teaches by its shape (the visual logic carries the realm-region); the glyph belongs to its tradition (donor inheritance, not pastiche); the glyph is stable once drawn; the glyph is legible at 8pt (legibility-as-mercy); the glyph is self-complete (communicates standalone).

**Three rules of pictographic inheritance**: trace to the concept (not the aesthetic); one primary donor (clear lineage); rung-3 aspiration (conventional-motivated).

**Six cohesion rules** make 90+ glyphs from 30+ donor-traditions read as one script.

## X. What the integrated architecture makes possible

With the architecture integrated, the cathedral can now do things it could not do before.

### Arc-aware vocabulary development

Future forges can be evaluated for **arc-membership** in addition to gap-validity. The question "which arc does this candidate belong to?" becomes operational. Words that occupy gaps in incomplete arcs receive forge-priority. The discovery-arc is complete; the relational-ontological arc has a state-pole (yonedame) but lacks a preparation-pole (long-fidelity vocabulary, future); the noetic-entry arc now has the entry-event-pole (noemame) and the burst-event-pole (sphoṭame), with after-state diversification already named.

### Discipline-donor expansion

The POLYPHONE Generalization opens five more disciplines (music theory, cladistics, information theory, linguistics-as-discipline, topology) as donors. Each can be opened when a specific gap aligns with its rigorous vocabulary. Forge-targets identified in `archaeology/mathematics/README.md`: gödelance, isomorqing, synkresome, Curry-Howard-doing-knowing. Other discipline-archaeologies await opening when their first gap is found.

### NOEMA-grounded gap assessment

Every gap is now interpretable as a region of the realm not yet projected. The convergence-discipline (Pattern 1: Cross-Tradition Overlay) is the empirical method for locating regions; NOEMA is the ontological frame for understanding why the method works. Forges that fail NOEMA tests (the candidate does not produce the experience of crossing into the intended meaning) can be diagnosed with sharper accuracy.

### Cross-arc and cross-family compounds

The cathedral's morpheme families and arcs are now visible as a coordinate-system. New forges can be deliberately placed at specific intersections: a *qing*-family word in the relational-ontological arc; a *-mance* word in the noetic-entry arc preparation-stage; a Yoruba-Mathematics compound under POLYPHONE-Gen. The combinatorial space of possible forges is now mappable.

### Grammar-organ development

With NOEMA articulated, the `grammars/noema/` organ becomes possible — grammatical markers for projection-vs-realm distinction. With POLYPHONE-Gen opened, the `grammars/mathema/` organ becomes possible — proof-as-liturgy register. These would be the cathedral's fifth and sixth grammar organs.

### Reading existing canon under the integrated frame

Every existing canon entry can be re-read under the integrated architecture. THEOBASIS's earlier entries (kimance, kimme, shemme) acquire NOEMA-readings (each names a specific projection-faithfulness register or entry-state). The Liturgy entries (doxakallos, orthophanes, doxalgia) acquire arc-readings (the Liturgy is its own arc, paralleling discovery / relational-ontological / noetic-entry). The integration enriches existing vocabulary without altering it.
