V. **Cross-reference with culture** — The pyramid as resurrection-machine + primordial-mound recreated + cosmic-axis + sundisk-pyramidion-rays + statement-of-divinity. The funerary-cult as continuous-relationship maintaining royal-ka. **Hebrew-Egyptian biblical cross-reference**: Exodus narrative; Hebrew loan-words from Egyptian (yeor, hartom, tevah); Tabernacle structure and priestly-vocabulary parallels; Proverbs 22:17-24:22 from Amenemope. **Greek-Egyptian and Coptic-Christian synthesis**: Ptolemaic period, Roman period, Coptic Christianity preserves Egyptian-language-as-Christian-liturgy through Islamic conquest to present.

VI. **Egyptian donor-pool** — comprehensive table: ma'at, netjer, ka, ba, akh, ankh, djed, was, ib, ren, medu, medu netjer, sah, isfet — 14+ morphemes catalogued.
