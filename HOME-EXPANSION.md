## III. The expansion principle — why each integration matters under existing doctrine

Before laying down phases, the principle: **expansion is not arbitrary growth.** Under THEOBASIS, the realm is one and given by the Ground; under POLYPHONIA, different traditions project the one realm from different angles; under INTEGRATION, every block must carry both math-path and existing-language-path. Each integration the roadmap proposes:

1. **Names a realm-feature already present-but-un-named in cathedral vocabulary** (POLYPHONIA Pattern 1 — convergence-as-truth-witness when ≥7 traditions converge; or singular-but-deep-distinctness when only one tradition has named what others circumlocute)
2. **Clears the Six Foundations** (CONSTITUTION) — including the Nuance-pattern as register-discipline (NUANCE-NATURE §VI)
3. **Lands at a specific STOICHEIA level** (atomic morpheme / compound / diplosemic structure / grammatical operator / pair-or-cluster / architectural element) with clear donor-class
4. **Operates the INTEGRATION dual-discipline** — math-path companion or grounding identified, existing-language-path donor identified, both in the mathema_signature
5. **Honors SYMPLOKE's interlinking** — each new block enters the cathedral's standardised-joints rather than free-floating

Expansion preserves the cathedral's discipline. The roadmap below sequences integration-targets in the order that *the cathedral can absorb without losing its register*. Too-fast intake risks lossy-projection; too-slow risks the cathedral stagnating at its present extent. The phases balance throughput with depth.

## VI. Sequencing — which order, why

The phases above are NOT strict — they overlap and Yu retains direction. But the recommended priority-order:

**Phase 1 first** (highest-yield Core forges, ~5-10 sessions) — proves the roadmap by producing 12 cross-stream Core entries that fill the most-glaring gaps. The Phase 1 forges are *self-justifying*; each names a realm-feature so structurally-important that the cathedral feels its absence.

**Phase 2 + Phase 4 in parallel** (next 15-25 sessions) — sub-tradition philosophical depth (Buddhist + Kabbalah + Sufi + Hesychast + Kashmir-Shaiva) PLUS modal-expansion (music + image + gesture + silence + time-architecture + visual-relational). These two phases together expand the cathedral by ~50-60 forges and 5-7 new sub-organs. Run in parallel because they enrich complementary axes (sub-tradition-depth at existing modes vs new-modes-entirely).

**Phase 3 third** (next 20-30 sessions) — Indigenous deep-mining. Sequenced after Phase 2-4 because Indigenous-tradition mining requires more careful ethical-protocol attention and the cathedral benefits from having absorbed the Phase 2-4 deepening before attempting Phase 3's register-sensitivity.

**Phase 5 last** (long-term, ~20-30 sessions) — Modern philosophical + mathema-class systematic completion. Sequenced last because (a) modern philosophical traditions are best mined after the cathedral has secured its pre-modern depth, so the modern can be properly *contextualized* rather than dominating; (b) mathema-class completion benefits from the existing-language-path companion always being already-present (per INTEGRATION discipline), and Phase 1-4 will have substantially deepened the existing-language-path donor base.

Total estimated forges across all 5 phases: **150-220 new Core canon entries** (more than doubling current Core canon); **25-35 new archaeology files or sub-directories**; **15-25 new convergence-overlays**; **8-12 new modal sub-organs or specialized registries**; **30+ new mathema sub-organ entries.**
